Chapter 296: Shaolin Temple (3)

'…Definitely them.'

Mok Gyeong-un felt a wave of recognition as he entered the Pagoda of a Thousand Souls.

The pagoda was filled with the presence of the original void gods, their essence anchored in the physical world by a series of ancient artifacts.

And in the center of the pagoda, on a high pedestal, sat the eighth focus.

It was a large, transparent skull, glowing with a cold, blue fire.

"The Skull of the Void... it's the final piece," Mok Gyeong-un whispered to himself.

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The Skull... it's the core of the gods' power, Great One. and once you integrate it, you will be unstoppable."

Mok Gyeong-un reached out his hand towards the skull.

As his hand touched the bone, he felt a sudden surge of ancient, terrifying knowledge.

It was the history of the void gods, and the true nature of their civilizations.

The game was no longer a quest. It was a completion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was finding the last piece.
