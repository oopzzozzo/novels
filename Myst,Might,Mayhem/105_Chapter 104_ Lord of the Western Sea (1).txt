Chapter 104: Lord of the Western Sea (1)

"Human. Open the wooden box. If you do so, I, the ruler of the seas, shall grant your wish."

'!?'

At the voice echoing in his mind, Mok Gyeong-un looked down at the ornate wooden box he had found in the Treasure Vault.

It was a small, ancient box, covered in sea-shells and barnacles.

"The ruler of the seas? And what wish could you possibly grant me?"

"I can give you the power to control the tides. The power to command the creatures of the deep. The power to become the Lord of the Western Sea."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"And why would I want that? I already have the power of the void."

"The void is a powerful force, human. But it is not the only one. And the sea... the sea is vast and eternal."

Mok Gyeong-un touched the lid of the box.

"A tempting offer. But I've learned that every wish comes with a price."

"The price is simple, human. You must become my vessel. You must allow me to enter your world."

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"Another one who wants a host. It seems I'm quite popular today."

He opened the lid, and a surge of blue, salty energy erupted from the box.

The game was becoming a naval conquest. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was taking the helm.
