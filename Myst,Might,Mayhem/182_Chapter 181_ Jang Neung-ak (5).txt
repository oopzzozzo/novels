Chapter 181: Jang Neung-ak (5)

"This isn't a mere change of leadership. It's a fundamental shift in the nature of reality."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was solemn, and his eyes were filled with an ancient, terrifying wisdom.

He was currently in the Emperor's private quarters, facing the ruler of the empire.

The Emperor was a man of great power and influence, but now he was just a man who was afraid of the void.

"Mok Gyeong-un... you've done it. You've destroyed everything I built," the Emperor said, his voice trembling with a mixture of fear and fury.

"I haven't destroyed it, Emperor. I've merely... transcended it. and now, I'm going to do the same for you."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a blinding, crimson light that illuminated the entire chamber.

"The fourth focus... it's mine."

The Emperor tried to resist, but his own power was dwarfed by the boy's.

The final focus, a small, black gem that sat on the Emperor's desk, began to glow with an intense, dark light.

The ritual was complete. The gateway to the void was fully open. and Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was stepping through.

The game was over for the mortal world. But for the void, it was just beginning.
