Chapter 119: Choice (2)

"Then, there's no other way. For the sake of fairness, I will say this. Please don't be too angry. I only chose what was best for the Society."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was calm, but it carried an underlying threat.

He was facing Wi So-yeon, who had just been informed of his choice to use the tablet as the focus.

"The tablet? But Master, the seal is the symbol of the Mok family's authority! Without it, you will never be truly accepted as the Manor Master!"

"I don't need acceptance, So-yeon. I need power. And the tablet will give me more power than any seal ever could."

He stepped closer to the third disciple.

"Besides, the Mok family's authority is... outdated. The Society needs a new symbol. A symbol of the void."

So-yeon's eyes narrowed.

"You are making a mistake, Mok Gyeong-un. The Society is built on tradition. And you are destroying those traditions."

"I am not destroying them. I am... replacing them. With something better."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"Now, if you'll excuse me. I have a focus to prepare."

The game was becoming a schism. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the breakaway.
