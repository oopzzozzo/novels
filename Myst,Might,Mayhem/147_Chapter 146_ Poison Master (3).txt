Chapter 146: Poison Master (3)

Drip! Drip!

The toxic energy was dripping from Mok Gyeong-un's left arm, sizzling as it hit the floor of the Sun Tower.

He was currently in the leader's private quarters, facing the entity that had consumed him.

The leader was a mass of dark, toxic energy, his form constantly shifting and changing.

"So, you've come to deliver my gift," the leader said, his voice a chorus of a thousand voices.

"I have. and I think you'll find it... very effective," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his voice calm and steady.

He raised his left arm, and the dark, toxic energy surged forward, clashing with the leader's own power.

The room was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the final confrontation.

It was a battle of two masters of the void and the toxins, each seeking to consume the other.

The other members of the Society watched from the base of the tower, their own lives hanging in the balance.

The game was no longer a countdown. It was an explosion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was surviving it.
