Chapter 226: Lady Seo, the Imperial Noble Consort (3)

The internal battle within the Palace was reaching a fever pitch.

Mok Gyeong-un's shadow unit was clashing with the Emperor's remaining guard, and the sound of clashing steel and agonizing screams filled the air.

In the center of the chaos, Lady Seo was doing exactly as Mok Gyeong-un had commanded.

She was moving through the Palace, using her influence and her knowledge of the void to convince the officials and the warriors to switch sides.

"The Emperor is no longer your master! He is a shell of his former self!" she shouted, her voice echoing through the corridors.

"But Mok Gyeong-un... he is the future! He is the one who will lead us to the ultimate truth!"

Some were resistant, but many were swayed by her words and by the sheer power of Mok Gyeong-un's presence.

One by one, the units began to surrender, their swords dropping to the floor.

Mok Gyeong-un watched from the Sun Tower, his eyes glowing with an intense, golden light.

The coalition was growing. and the takeover was almost complete.

The game was no longer a palace intrigue. It was a coup d'état. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was taking the throne.
