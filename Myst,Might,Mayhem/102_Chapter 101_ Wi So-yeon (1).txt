Chapter 101: Wi So-yeon (1)

"I am Wi So-yeon, the third disciple of the Heaven and Earth Society's leader."

'!?'

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes sharpened at So-yeon's words.

The third disciple of the leader... that meant he was one of the most powerful people in the Society.

"And what does the leader want with me, So-yeon?"

So-yeon smiled, a calm and confident smile.

"He wants to meet you, Young Master. He has heard of your... unique talents. and he wishes to discuss the future of the Society with you."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled back.

"The future of the Society? I think I've already decided that future."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"But if the leader wants to meet me, I won't refuse. In fact, I think I'll go right now."

So-yeon's eyes widened in surprise.

"Now? But the leader is in the inner castle..."

"Then that's where I'm going. Lead the way, So-yeon."

The game was becoming a summit. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the delegation.
