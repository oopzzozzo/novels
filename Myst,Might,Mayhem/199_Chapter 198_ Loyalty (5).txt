Chapter 198: Loyalty (5)

Mong Mu-yak's face was a mask of cold fury.

He was currently meeting with the remaining members of his unit, the Blue Tiger Squad.

"Mok Gyeong-un has gone too far," he said, his voice full of hidden malice.

"He's destroying the Society from within. and he's turning us into monsters."

One of the squad members, a young man named Sang-woo, looked up.

"But Master... he's the master of the void. and he's the only one who can lead us to the ultimate truth."

Mong Mu-yak laughed, a cold and hollow sound.

"The ultimate truth? You think he cares about the truth? He only cares about power! He's using the void to achieve his own goals!"

He stood up and drew his sword.

"I will not serve a monster. and I will not allow the Society to be destroyed by a boy."

"And what do you propose, Master?" another squad member asked.

"I propose that we join the Righteous Alliance. and we end the reign of Mok Gyeong-un once and for all."

The game was becoming a defection. And Mong Mu-yak was the one who was leading the exit.
