Chapter 87: Master of the Primal Killing Pavilion (3)

'Master?'

What in the world did that mean?

In Seo-ok, the Pavilion Master of Primal Killing, was momentarily at a loss for words.

The boy before her, Mok Gyeong-un, had just referred to himself as her master.

"You... you're not my master. I am the one who rules the Pavilion!" she shouted, her voice full of desperate denial.

Mok Gyeong-un tilted his head, his eyes as still as a dead person's.

"Not anymore. Now, you are just a servant of the void. And I am the void's representative in this world."

He stepped closer to In Seo-ok, the dark energy swirling around him like a protective cloak.

"The Spirit God Parasitic Poisoning Technique... it's a useful tool. I think I'll keep it."

He reached out his hand towards her forehead.

"Now, tell me about the Society's grand plan. What is the ultimate goal of the Master?"

In Seo-ok tried to resist, but her mind was already beginning to cloud.

"The goal... is the Apotheosis. The transformation of the Master into a true god."

Mok Gyeong-un's smile widened.

"A god... it seems everyone has the same ambition."

He turned to the Demonic Monk, who was watching from the shadows.

"But there can be only one god in this world. And I intend to be the one."

The game was becoming a clash of ambitions. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was winning.
