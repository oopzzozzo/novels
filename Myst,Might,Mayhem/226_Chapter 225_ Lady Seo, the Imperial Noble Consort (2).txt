Chapter 225: Lady Seo, the Imperial Noble Consort (2)

Lady Seo looked at Mok Gyeong-un with a complex expression.

"You've truly inherited the legacy of the Lee family, haven't you?" she asked, her voice full of a hidden sadness.

"I've inherited more than just a name, Consort. I've inherited a purpose," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his golden-white eyes neutral.

"And what purpose is that?"

"The purpose of the void. To erase the mistakes of the past and create a future that is worthy of our blood."

Lady Seo was silent for a moment.

"The mistakes of the past... you mean the Society?"

"The Society is just a symptom, Consort. The true mistake was the betrayal of the Lee family. and the subsequent pursuit of power at any cost."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped closer to the Consort.

"But now, I am here to correct those mistakes. and I want your help."

Lady Seo hesitated. She knew the danger of Mok Gyeong-un's power. but she also felt a connection to his purpose.

"And what do you want from me?"

"I want you to be my liaison in the Palace. I want you to find the ones who are still serving the Emperor. and I want you to convince them to join me."

The game was becoming a political coalition. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was recruiting the allies.
