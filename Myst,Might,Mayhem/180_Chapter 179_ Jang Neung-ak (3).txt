Chapter 179: Jang Neung-ak (3)

In an instant, the confrontation between Mok Gyeong-un and Jang Neung-ak came to an end.

Jang Neung-ak, the leader of the Imperial City's guard unit, was lying on the ground, his body broken and his sword shattered.

Mok Gyeong-un stood over him, his eyes black and lifeless.

"You were a worthy opponent, General," he said, his voice neutral.

"But you were still just a man."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, consuming Jang Neung-ak's remains.

The Sun Tower was silent. The battle for the Palace was over.

Mok Gyeong-un stood in the center of the ruins, his entire body glowing with a faint, crimson light.

He was now the undisputed master of the Imperial City.

He looked at the distant spires of the city, his presence overwhelming.

"The fourth focus is here. and I intend to find it."

The game was no longer a duel. It was a search. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the hunt.
