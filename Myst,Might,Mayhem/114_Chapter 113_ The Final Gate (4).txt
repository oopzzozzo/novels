Chapter 113: The Final Gate (4)

At the moment when Mok Gyeong-un was pushed back, his ribs and chest were struck by Mu Jang-yak's secret technique.

It was a powerful strike, one that should have shattered his bones and ended the fight.

But Mok Gyeong-un didn't even flinch.

He just stood there, his eyes glowing with a dark, intense light.

"Is that all you have, Mu Jang-yak?" he asked, his voice echoing with the power of the void.

Mu Jang-yak's eyes widened in disbelief.

"Impossible... no one can survive that strike!"

"I am not 'no one'. I am the master of the void."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, consuming Mu Jang-yak in a blinding, crimson light.

The battle was over. And Mok Gyeong-un was the winner.

The leader's shadow on the throne seemed to flicker.

"Impressive. You have survived the first challenge. But the next one will not be so easy."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"I'm ready. Bring it on."

The game was becoming a marathon. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the pack.
