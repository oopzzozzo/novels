Chapter 193: The Test (4)

"A dead man who should have stayed dead."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was cold and flat as he faced the projection of the previous Society Leader.

The leader was currently in the ritual chamber, his form made of shadow and bone.

"You think you can just take my place, boy?" the leader asked, his voice a chorus of a thousand voices.

"I haven't taken your place. I've surpassed it," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his transparent eyes fixed on the leader.

He raised his left arm, and the quiet power of the void surged forward, clashing with the leader's dark energy.

The chamber was filled with the sound of a silent explosion, a blast of pure energy that shook the very foundations of the tower.

It was a battle of two masters of the void, one who had been consumed by it and one who had mastered it.

The other members of the Society watched in awe and terror, their own lives hanging in the balance.

The game was over. and Mok Gyeong-un was the winner.

The leader's projection dissolved, and his presence faded away.

The test was complete. and Mok Gyeong-un was the true master of the Society.
