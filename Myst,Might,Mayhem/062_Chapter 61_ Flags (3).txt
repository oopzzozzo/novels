Chapter 61: Flags (3)

-Mortal, you're really…

Cheong-ryeong clicked her tongue.

She had wondered why he was joining as a teammate, but now she understood.

He was using them. Using them to identify the traitors within the Society.

"You're a clever one, Mok Gyeong-un," she said, her voice full of grudging respect.

Mok Gyeong-un didn't look back. He was busy disposing of Sohwa's body.

"I am just... practical, Cheong-ryeong."

He turned to the other teammates, who were still standing in the courtyard.

"Now, tell me. Who else is working for the Heaven & Earth Society?"

The teammates remained silent.

"I won't ask twice. If you don't speak, you die."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

One by one, the teammates began to point fingers.

"He is! And she is!"

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of work for you to do."

The game was becoming an interrogation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the pliers.
