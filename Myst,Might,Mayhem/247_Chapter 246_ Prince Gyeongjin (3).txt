Chapter 246: Prince Gyeongjin (3)

Consort Ho was staring at Prince Gyeongjin with a mixture of suspicion and alarm.

She was one of the most powerful women in the Palace, and she had her own plans for the future of the Empire.

"You're investigating the Shadow Alliance, aren't you, Gyeongjin?" she asked, her voice full of hidden threat.

"I am, Consort. It is my duty to protect the legacy of my ancestor."

Consort Ho laughed, a cold and sharp sound.

"Duty? You mean your obsession with power! You're just like Mok Gyeong-un. and you'll face the same fate."

Prince Gyeongjin smiled, his eyes glowing with a faint, white radiance.

"I'm not like my ancestor, Consort. I'm... different."

He stepped closer to her.

"Now, tell me. What is your connection to the Shadow Alliance? I know you've been in contact with their leader."

Consort Ho's expression hardened.

"I will never tell you, Gyeongjin. and you'll never find the focus."

The game was becoming a confrontation. And Prince Gyeongjin was the one who was providing the challenge.
