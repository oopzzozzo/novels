Chapter 241: Turning Misfortune into Blessing (1)

The news of Mok Gyeong-un's ascension to godhood spread through the universe like wildfire.

The void gods were in a state of shock and terror, and the mortal world was in a state of total awe.

"The Void Sun has risen! The master of all is here!" the people shouted, their voices full of fanatical devotion.

Mok Gyeong-un watched from his new domain in the heavens, his eyes glowing with a blinding, white radiance.

The transformation was complete. and every variable had been accounted for.

"The ritual was a success, Master," the Demonic Monk said, appearing beside him in a new, celestial form.

"The opposites have been fused. and the void is now complete."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For the universe is now mine to reshape."

He raised his left arm, and the golden-white energy surged forward, creating a wave of creation that washed over the entire cosmos.

The game was no longer a battle or a quest. It was a masterpiece. And Mok Gyeong-un was the artist.

The world was reborn. and the shadow of the past was no more.
