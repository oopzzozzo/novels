Chapter 222: Mixed-blooded (3)

"Just because you have the blood of the gods doesn't mean you're divine."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was cold, and his words cut through the remaining pride of the Mixed-blooded Clan.

He was currently meeting with the clan's warriors in the training courtyard, and he was putting them through a series of grueling tests.

"You're relying too much on your heritage. and not enough on your own will," he said, his golden-white eyes scanning their faces.

"The void doesn't care about your lineage. It only cares about your potential."

He demonstrated a technique, his movements a blur of motion that the warriors couldn't even follow.

"This is what true power looks like. Not a gift from the gods, but a mastery of the self."

The warriors watched in awe and humility. They had always believed that their blood made them superior, but now they saw that they were just beginners in the eyes of the master.

"The training begins now. and I don't care who your ancestors were."

The game was no longer a merger. It was a reconstruction. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was building the foundation.
