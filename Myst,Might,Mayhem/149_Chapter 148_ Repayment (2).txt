Chapter 148: Repayment (2)

"But what exactly are you planning to do, Master?"

These were the words of the Shadow Clan Master, who was currently meeting with Mok Gyeong-un in the Sun Tower.

"I'm planning to return the favor, Master. To show the void gods what happens when they interfere in the affairs of men."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was calm, but it carried an underlying fury that made the Shadow Clan Master flinch.

"The void gods... they are powerful beings, Young Master. Even for someone like you."

"They are powerful. but they are also arrogant. and arrogance is a weakness."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"I will find their conduits in this world. and I will destroy them. Starting with the ones who were hiding in this very society."

He looked at the Shadow Clan Master.

"Now, give me the list. I want the names of every agent who has ever been in contact with the void."

The Shadow Clan Master hesitated. He knew that providing such a list would be a death sentence for many of his agents.

"I... I will... provide it, Master."

The game was becoming a manhunt. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the pack.
