Chapter 151: Talent (2)

The dark red poison energy swirling around Mok Gyeong-un's left arm was a sight that made many of the applicants tremble with fear.

He was currently testing one of the candidates, a young man who had displayed remarkable sword skills during the initial rounds.

"Show me your talent," Mok Gyeong-un commanded, his voice cold and flat.

The young man lunged forward, his sword moving in a blur of motion.

But Mok Gyeong-un didn't even move. He just raised his left arm and caught the blade between his fingers.

The toxic energy from his arm instantly corroded the steel, and the young man fell back in shock.

"Your sword is a tool. and like any tool, it can be broken," Mok Gyeong-un said, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

"But your will... your will is what truly matters. And your will is weak."

He raised his hand, and the red thread surged forward, wrapping around the young man's neck.

"Now, give me your soul. I have a monster to create."

The candidate's screams echoed through the training ground, a sound of raw, primal terror that made the other applicants wish they had never come.

The game was no longer a recruitment drive. It was a transformation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the change.
