Chapter 102: Wi So-yeon (2)

"Didn't you say that words carry weight and that you take responsibility for what you say yourself?"

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was calm, but it carried an underlying threat.

He was facing Wi So-yeon, who had just made a statement about the Society's leadership.

"I did, Young Master. And I stand by my words," So-yeon replied, his expression unfazed.

"The Society needs a new direction. A new leader who can lead us to the ultimate truth."

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"And you think I am that leader? Or are you just using me to achieve your own goals?"

So-yeon's eyes glinted.

"Perhaps it's a bit of both. But in the end, it doesn't matter. What matters is the result."

He stepped closer to Mok Gyeong-un.

"The leader is waiting. And he's not a patient man. I suggest we go now."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Very well. Then let's not keep him waiting. I'm curious to see what the 'Great Leader' looks like."

The two of them walked towards the inner castle, the dark energy of the void swirling around them.

The game was becoming a power struggle. And Wi So-yeon was the one who was making the moves.
