Chapter 117: Disciple Scramble (4)

"How do you expect to do anything to me like this? Why don't you try using that cultivation method instead?"

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was mocking.

He was facing the last remaining candidate, a boy who had survived the slaughter in the courtyard.

The boy was exhausted and wounded, but his eyes were filled with a desperate determination.

"I... I will... kill you," he managed to gasp, his sword trembling in his hand.

"Kill me? You can't even touch me," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

He raised his left arm, and the red thread surged forward, wrapping around the boy's neck.

"Now, give me your life. I have a unit to lead."

The boy tried to resist, but the binding was absolute.

"I... I will... serve... Master," he managed to choke out, his eyes turning a cold, blue fire.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For you are the first member of my shadow unit."

He looked at the corpses in the courtyard.

"The test is over. The unit is formed. And soon, the conquest will begin."

The game was no longer a cull. It was a mobilization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the army.
