Chapter 223: Mixed-blooded (4)

"I'll try to find out as much as I can."

Song-ah's voice was firm, and her eyes were filled with a new, quiet determination.

She had been tasked by Mok Gyeong-un with a secret mission: to infiltrate the remaining pockets of resistance within the Society and identify those who were still loyal to the old gods.

"Be careful, Song-ah. The void gods are not to be trifled with," Mok Gyeong-un warned, his golden-white eyes fixed on her.

"I know, Master. but I am no longer afraid. For I have your grace."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For the mission is critical to the completion of the ritual."

He watched as Song-ah disappeared into the shadows of the manor.

The game was no longer a reconstruction. It was a preparation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was readying the field.

The final stage of the ritual was just a few days away. and the world would never be the same again.
