Chapter 140: Annihilating Poison King (1)

The Annihilating Poison King, a man whose name was feared even by the most powerful warriors of the Society.

He was a master of the dark arts of poisoning, and he had spent his entire life creating toxins that could kill even the gods.

His name was Dok Go-jin.

He was currently in his secret laboratory, working on a new and deadly poison.

"The void Gu... it's a fascinating creature," he muttered to himself, his eyes fixed on a pulsating insect in a glass jar.

Suddenly, a figure appeared in the laboratory. It was Mok Gyeong-un.

"You're the one they call the Poison King," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice flat and neutral.

Dok Go-jin stopped his work and looked at the boy.

"Young Master Mok. What brings you to my laboratory?"

"I'm here for your poison. I've heard that you've created a toxin that can kill even a master of the void."

Dok Go-jin smiled, a cold and predatory smile.

"I have. but it is not for sale. It is my masterpiece. My legacy."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled back.

"I'm not here to buy it. I'm here to take it."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

The game was becoming a race for the ultimate weapon. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the sprint.
