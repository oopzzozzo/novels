Chapter 23: Ritual of Attachment (3)

'Shit. At this rate, I won't be able to keep my life.'

Guard Go Chan was heading towards Hyehwa Hall, his mind racing.

The boy, Mok Gyeong-un, had already killed several guards in the courtyard.

And he was coming for Lady Seok.

Go Chan knew he had to warn her. But he also knew that if he did, he might become the next target.

"Go Chan!"

A voice called out from behind him.

He turned to see the second Young Master, Mok Eun-pyeong.

"Young Master! You shouldn't be here. It's dangerous."

"I know. But I also know what's happening. The boy... he's using the Ritual of Attachment."

Go Chan's eyes widened.

"How do you know about that?"

"I've been... researching. And I know how to stop it."

"You do?"

"Yes. But I need your help. We need to get to the secret chamber before the boy does."

Go Chan hesitated. He didn't trust Mok Eun-pyeong. But he also didn't have many options.

"Alright. What do we do?"

"Follow me. And stay close."

The two of them disappeared into the shadows of the main hall.

The game was becoming a race against time. And the stakes were life and death.
