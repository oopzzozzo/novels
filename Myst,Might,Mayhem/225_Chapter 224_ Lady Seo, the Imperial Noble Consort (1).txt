Chapter 224: Lady Seo, the Imperial Noble Consort (1)

Lady Seo, the Imperial Noble Consort, was currently sitting in her private garden within the Imperial Palace.

She was a woman of great beauty and influence, and she was known for her absolute loyalty to the Emperor.

But she also had a secret. She was a master of the void.

"The time has come," she whispered to herself as she watched the sun set over the city.

"The master of the void has finally appeared. and the ritual can begin."

Suddenly, a figure appeared in the garden. It was Mok Gyeong-un.

"You're the one they call the Noble Consort," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice neutral.

Lady Seo smiled, a calm and confident smile.

"I am. and I've been waiting for you, Young Master Mok."

"Waiting for me? And why is that?"

"Because you are the key. The one who will lead us to the ultimate truth."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled back.

"The ultimate truth... it seems everyone has the same goal."

He stepped closer to the Consort.

"Now, tell me. Where is the final focus? I know it's here in the Palace."

Lady Seo's eyes glinted with interest.

"The final focus... it's in the Emperor's private quarters. But you'll have to get past the Three Eyes first."

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"The Three Eyes? I've already taken care of them."

The game was becoming a palace intrigue. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the cards.
