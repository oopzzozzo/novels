Chapter 120: Choice (3)

"But let me add one more thing. I was brought here as a hostage by Bright Blade King, who is present here."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was sharp and clear, cutting through the tension in the meeting hall.

He was facing the Council of Elders, who were staring at him with a mixture of suspicion and alarm.

"A hostage? And why would the Bright Blade King do such a thing?" one of the Elders asked.

"Because he wanted to control the master of the void. Because he wanted to use me to achieve his own goals."

Mok Gyeong-un turned to Son Yun, who was standing at the back of the hall.

"Isn't that right, Bright Blade King?"

Son Yun's expression was a mask of neutral indifference.

"I was merely doing my duty, Young Master. Protecting the Society's interests."

"Protecting your own interests, you mean."

Mok Gyeong-un looked back at the Elders.

"The Heaven & Earth Society is a house of cards. Built on betrayal and deceit. And I am the one who is going to blow it all down."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

The game was no longer a schism. It was a revolution. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the charge.
