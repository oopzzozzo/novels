Chapter 54: Proposal (3)

-The weight is different.

"What?"

-Becoming my disciple means you will bear the karma of the moon and stars. It is a burden that few can withstand.

The Demonic Monk's voice was heavy with warning.

Mok Gyeong-un ignored him and looked at diviner Jo.

"Karma of the moon and stars... it sounds like a lot of trouble."

Jo nodded.

"It is. But it is also the key to the ultimate power. The power that can transcend life and death."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then I'll take it. I've never been one to shy away from a challenge."

Jo's eyes widened in surprise.

"You... you accept?"

"I do. But remember, I am the one who will define this karma. Not the other way around."

Jo bowed his head.

"I understand, Young Master. Then let our journey begin."

The game was becoming a destiny. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was writing it.
