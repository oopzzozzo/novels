Chapter 197: Loyalty (4)

The Third Captain Cho Tae-cheong bowed his head in absolute submission.

He had been one of the old leader's most loyal followers, but now he was a servant of the void.

"Master... I have a report from the Shadow Clan," he said, his voice now a mere rasp.

"They've identified a group of defectors. Warriors who are planning to join the Righteous Alliance."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the captain.

"Defectors? And who are they?"

"They're a group of junior disciples, led by a man named Kang Jin-ho."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes glinted with interest.

"Kang Jin-ho? The one who practiced the Iron Fist?"

"Yes, Master. It seems he wasn't as loyal as we thought."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Interesting. So, he wants to test his Iron Fist against the void again."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"Go and find them, Captain. and bring them to me. I have a special punishment for traitors."

The game was becoming a series of executions. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the sentence.
