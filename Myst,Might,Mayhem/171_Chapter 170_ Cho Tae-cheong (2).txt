Chapter 170: Cho Tae-cheong (2)

Clang! Clang! Clang!

The sound of clashing steel echoed through the street as Mok Gyeong-un and Cho Tae-cheong fought.

Tae-cheong was using his speed to launch a series of rapid strikes, but Mok Gyeong-un was parrying each one with ease.

"You're fast, Tae-cheong. But you're still bound by the limits of human reaction," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice neutral.

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint, crimson glow that illuminated the street.

"I am the master of the void. and the void is beyond reaction."

He stepped forward, his movements a blur of motion that Tae-cheong couldn't even follow.

-Thud!

Mok Gyeong-un's fist landed squarely on Tae-cheong's chest, knocking him back several feet.

"Wh-what? My armor... it's like it wasn't even there!"

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"The void doesn't care about your armor, Tae-cheong. It only cares about the impact."

He raised his hand and struck Tae-cheong again.

The game was becoming a demonstration of absolute power. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the lesson.
