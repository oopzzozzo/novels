Chapter 166: Variables (2)

Originally, Wi So-yeon was the one who was supposed to be the focus of the Society's attention.

He was the prodigy, the future leader, the one everyone looked up to.

But now, he was just a servant of the void, a puppet controlled by Mok Gyeong-un.

And the variables in the Imperial City were even more complex than he had imagined.

"The Temple of the Moon... it's not just a temple, Master," Wi So-yeon said, his voice now a hollow echo of its former self.

"It's a gateway. A way for the void gods to enter our world."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then I'll just have to close that gateway. Or better yet, I'll use it for my own purposes."

He looked at the distant spires of the temple, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

"The second focus is waiting. and I intend to claim it."

Suddenly, they were approached by a group of cloaked figures.

"Who are you? And why are you here?" one of the figures demanded, his voice full of hidden power.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled back.

"I am the master of the void. And I am here to take what is mine."

The game was becoming a battle of shadows. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the assault.
