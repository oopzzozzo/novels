Chapter 183: Submission (2)

"Hmm?"

Mok Gyeong-un felt a sudden, strange sensation as he looked at the now-empty shell of the Emperor.

It was a feeling of... incompleteness.

As if he had only taken a part of the ruler's power, and the rest was still hiding.

" Monk... is the Emperor truly gone?"

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows, his form flickering with unease.

"I... I don't know, Great One. I felt his soul being consumed. but there's something... else."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the black gem on the desk.

It was still glowing with an intense, dark light.

"The focus... it's not just a source of power. It's a container. A container for the Emperor's true essence."

He smiled.

"So the 'Great Ruler' has one last trick up his sleeve. He's hiding inside the focus."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, wrapping around the gem.

"Now, let's see how well you can hide in the void."

The room was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the agonizing screams of the Emperor's true essence.

The game was no longer a submission. It was a liquidation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was doing the counting.
