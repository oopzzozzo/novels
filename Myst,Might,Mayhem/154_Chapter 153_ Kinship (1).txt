Chapter 153: Kinship (1)

[Since the Corpse Blood Valley Master is also from the Lee family, then doesn't that mean he's your relative?]

The Demonic Monk's voice was full of curiosity, echoing in Mok Gyeong-un's mind.

The boy was currently in his study, reviewing the records of the Lee family's history.

"It seems so, Monk. But in the world of the void, kinship is a meaningless concept."

Mok Gyeong-un turned a page in the ancient ledger.

"The Lee family was betrayed by the Society. and now, the survivors are seeking revenge."

"And you? Are you also seeking revenge?"

"I am seeking... justice. The justice of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up and walked to the window.

"The Master of the Valley... he's a part of my past. But he's not a part of my future."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"My future is the void. and everyone who stands in my way will be consumed by it."

The game was becoming a family tragedy. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was ending it.
