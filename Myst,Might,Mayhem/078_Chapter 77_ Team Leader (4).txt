Chapter 77: Team Leader (4)

-Tighten!

The eyes of Mo Ha-rang of the Demon Fire Hall trembled.

The faint form of chains enveloping Mok Gyeong-un's left arm was now fully visible.

It was a manifestation of the binding, a physical representation of the control he held over the void.

And now, those chains were extending towards Mo Ha-rang.

"You... you're not just using the void. You're becoming it," she whispered, her voice full of terror.

Mok Gyeong-un didn't answer. He just tightened his grip on the chains.

"I am the one who defines the void, Mo Ha-rang. And I have defined you as my servant."

The chains wrapped around Mo Ha-rang, and she felt a sudden, sharp pain in her chest.

"I... I will... serve... Master," she managed to choke out, her eyes turning a cold, blue fire.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. Now, go and find the Master of the Corpse Blood Valley. Tell him his time is up."

Mo Ha-rang bowed her head and disappeared into the shadows.

The game was becoming a mobilization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was giving the orders.
