Chapter 154: Kinship (2)

"So, here's the thing."

Mok Gyeong-un was currently meeting with the Master of the Corpse Blood Valley, Lee Ji-yeom.

The two of them were in a secret chamber within the Valley, surrounded by the remnants of the Lee family's power.

"You're a Lee. And I'm a Lee. That makes us family," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice neutral.

Ji-yeom looked at the boy with his sharp, intelligent eyes.

"Family... it's a dangerous word in this society, Young Master."

"I know. but it's also a powerful one. If we work together, we can reclaim our legacy. We can take revenge on those who betrayed us."

Ji-yeom was silent for a moment.

"And what do you want from me?"

"Your loyalty. Your resources. Your absolute control over the Valley."

Ji-yeom smiled, a cold and calculating smile.

"A tempting offer. But what do I get in exchange?"

"You get to live. and you get to witness the destruction of the Heaven & Earth Society."

The game was becoming a family pact. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the alliance.
