Chapter 244: Prince Gyeongjin (1)

"Ah, this is truly a masterpiece."

Prince Gyeongjin was staring at the ancient book in his study, his eyes glowing with a faint, white radiance.

He was the descendant of Mok Gyeong-un, and he had inherited his ancestor's thirst for knowledge and power.

"The Void Sun... it's more than just a myth," he whispered to himself.

"It's a legacy. and I am the one who will carry it forward."

Suddenly, a figure appeared in the study. It was the Demonic Monk, now a mere whisper in the Prince's mind.

"The legacy is a burden, Prince. and power is a fickle force."

"I am aware of that, Monk. but I am ready to bear it."

Prince Gyeongjin stood up and adjusted his robes.

"Now, tell me. Where is the first focus? I want to begin my own journey."

The Monk's voice became solemn.

"The first focus is in the heart of the Northern Mountains. In the Forbidden Temple."

Prince Gyeongjin smiled.

"Then that's where I'm going."

The game was becoming a cycle. And Prince Gyeongjin was the one who was starting it over.
