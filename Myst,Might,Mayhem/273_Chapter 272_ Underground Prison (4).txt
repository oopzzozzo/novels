Chapter 272: Underground Prison (4)

Embroidered Uniform Guard members were currently patrolling the perimeter of the Underground Prison, their expressions full of hidden power.

They had been sent by the Emperor to protect the seventh focus, and they were prepared to die for their duty.

"They're coming," one of the guards said, his voice a mere rasp.

"I can feel the cold wind of the void."

Suddenly, a series of muffled explosions echoed through the prison, followed by the sound of clashing steel.

Mok Gyeong-un's shadow unit had arrived.

The guards rushed to meet them, and the battle began.

It was a brutal and bloody affair, as the unit and the guards fought each other with everything they had.

Mok Gyeong-un watched from the sidelines, his eyes glowing with an intense, crimson light.

"The seventh focus... it's here," he whispered to himself.

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a massacre. And Mok Gyeong-un was the primary assassin.
