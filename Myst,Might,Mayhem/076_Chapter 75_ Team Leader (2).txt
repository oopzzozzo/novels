Chapter 75: Team Leader (2)

Inside a red room illuminated by candlelight.

This place was a brothel located on the outskirts of the town.

It was also a front for the Society's information network.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently sitting at a low table, across from a woman in revealing clothes.

She was the mistress of the brothel, a woman known as Rose.

"You're far from your usual territory, Young Master," Rose said, her voice a sultry purr.

"I'm here for information, Rose. Information regarding the Master of the Corpse Blood Valley."

Rose's expression sobered.

"The Master of the Valley... he's a dangerous topic, Young Master. Even for someone like you."

"I'm aware of the danger. But I'm also aware of the opportunity."

Mok Gyeong-un leaned forward.

"Tell me everything you know about him. His location, his power, his weaknesses."

Rose hesitated.

"It will cost you, Young Master. The price of such information is high."

"I'll pay whatever price you ask. Just give me the information."

Rose nodded.

"Very well. Then listen closely. The Master of the Valley is not human. He is... something else."

The game was becoming a reconnaissance mission. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was gathering the intel.
