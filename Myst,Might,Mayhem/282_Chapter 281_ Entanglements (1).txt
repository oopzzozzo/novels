Chapter 281: Entanglements (1)

[Keep in mind, the duration of the ritual depends on the intensity of your focus.]

The Demonic Monk's voice was a mere whisper in Mok Gyeong-un's mind as he entered the final stage of the ritual.

The boy was currently in the Sun Tower's central ritual chamber, his entire body glowing with a blinding, golden-white radiance.

He had integrated all seven focuses, and his power was now absolute.

But the ritual was also creating a web of entanglements, connecting him to the souls of everyone in the city.

"I can feel them... their thoughts, their fears, their desires," Mok Gyeong-un said, his transparent eyes scanning the void.

"They are all a part of me now. and I am a part of them."

The entanglements were both a source of power and a burden. Mok Gyeong-un had to maintain his focus, or he would be consumed by the collective consciousness of the city.

"The Apotheosis is almost complete, Great One. You just need to hold on for a little longer."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"I'll hold on until the end of time."

The game was becoming a endurance test. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was going the distance.
