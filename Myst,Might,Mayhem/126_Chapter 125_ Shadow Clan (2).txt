Chapter 125: Shadow Clan (2)

"Well, well… You have a remarkable talent for surprises."

-Woooooosh!

The energy suddenly erupting from Mok Gyeong-un's body was unlike anything the Shadow King's agent had ever felt.

It was a cold, suffocating pressure that seemed to drain the very light from the room.

"Who... who are you?" the agent managed to gasp, his hand trembling on his sword.

"I am the one who will end your clan's dominance," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his voice flat and devoid of emotion.

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a shockwave that knocked the agent back.

"The Shadow King... he thinks he can control the shadows. but I am the one who commands the void."

He stepped towards the agent, his eyes glowing with an intense, crimson light.

"Now, tell me. Where is the entrance to the Shadow King's sanctum?"

The agent tried to resist, but the binding was absolute.

"I... I will... never... tell you," he managed to choke out.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"We'll see about that."

The room was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the agonizing screams of the agent.

The infiltration had begun. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the assault.
