Chapter 95: Benefits (1)

Mu Jang-yak always maintained a cheerful and bright demeanor, which naturally lifted the moods of those around him.

He was a high-ranking member of the Society, known for his administrative skills and his ability to navigate the complex politics of the organization.

He was currently reviewing the benefits that would be awarded to the winner of the tournament.

"Let's see... a seat on the Council of Elders, a portion of the secret vault's contents, and... the title of Grand Master of the Void."

Mu Jang-yak's eyes twinkled with interest.

"These are significant rewards. Even for a genius like Mok Gyeong-un."

He leaned back in his chair, his expression thoughtful.

"But the question is: will he use these benefits for the good of the Society? Or will he use them to destroy it?"

Mu Jang-yak knew the danger of Mok Gyeong-un's power. He had seen it firsthand in the tournament arena.

But he also knew the potential. If the boy could be controlled, he would be the greatest asset the Society had ever known.

The game was becoming a management challenge. And Mu Jang-yak was the one who was holding the reins.
