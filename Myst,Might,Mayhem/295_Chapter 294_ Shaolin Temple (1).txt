Chapter 294: Shaolin Temple (1)

Om somani somani hum arihan...

The sound of chanting filled the main hall of the Shaolin Temple, as hundreds of monks meditated in unison.

They were praying for the protection of the Empire, and for the souls of those who had been consumed by the void.

But their prayers were in vain.

For the Void Sun had already risen, and the shadow of the Empire was no more.

Mok Gyeong-un stood at the entrance of the temple, his lifeless eyes scanning the rows of monks.

"Your prayers are meaningless, monks. and your gods are just shadows in my presence."

The monks stopped their chanting and looked up, their eyes full of shock and alarm.

"Who are you? And why are you here?" the Abbot demanded, his voice full of hidden power.

"I am the master of the void. and I am here to take the eighth focus."

The Abbot's eyes widened.

"The eighth focus? But there are only seven!"

"There were seven. but now there is an eighth. and it's hidden here in Shaolin."

The Abbot drew his staff, the wood glowing with a faint, white radiance.

"I will not let you take it, boy! Shaolin is the guardian of the truth!"

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then I'll just have to take the truth from you."

The battle for Shaolin had begun. and the Winner would rule the universe.
