Chapter 17: Evil (1)

The Demonic Monk, a wandering spirit and a monster, bowed with fear and reverence.

As he showed this submissive attitude, the boy, Mok Gyeong-un, just watched with those lifeless eyes.

"So, you're the one who was causing trouble in the Manor Master's body?"

The Demonic Monk trembled.

"I... I was only seeking a host, Great One."

"A host? And you chose the Manor Master?"

"He was... weakened. A perfect vessel for my return."

Mok Gyeong-un tilted his head.

"Return to what?"

"To the world of the living. To reclaim what was stolen from me."

"And what was that?"

The Demonic Monk remained silent for a moment.

"My life. My power."

Mok Gyeong-un let out a small, chilling laugh.

"Power... it seems everyone in this manor is obsessed with it."

He stepped closer to the spirit.

"But you chose the wrong vessel. And you certainly encountered the wrong person."

The Demonic Monk looked up, terror etched on his spectral face.

"What... what are you going to do with me?"

"I haven't decided yet. But for now, you'll stay with me. I might have a use for a monster like you."

The Demonic Monk had no choice but to obey.

He was bound to this boy now.

A boy who was more terrifying than any demon he had ever encountered.
