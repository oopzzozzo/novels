Chapter 252: Demon (2)

"I'm not sure about that, Grandmaster."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was calm, even as he faced the Grand Diviner in the ritual chamber.

The Diviner was staring at him with a mixture of awe and apprehension.

"I am the Void Sun. and the void doesn't consume its master. It... completes them."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the golden-white energy surged forward, creating a faint radiance that illuminated the entire chamber.

"You've spent your entire life studying the void, Diviner. but you've never truly experienced it. You've only seen the shadow."

The Grand Diviner was silent. He knew that the boy was right.

"But I have become the void. and I am the one who commands the shadows."

He stepped closer to the Diviner.

"Now, tell me. Where is the seventh focus? I know it's hidden in the Forbidden City."

The Grand Diviner hesitated. He knew that revealing the location of the seventh focus would be the end of the world as he knew it.

"It's... in the Hall of Eternal Darkness. But you'll have to get past the Demon King first."

The game was becoming a showdown. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the challenge.
