Chapter 277: Blood Saint (4)

Ou Ye-zi, the great swordsmith, was staring at the small, black stone in his hand with a mixture of shock and admiration.

It was the fifth focus, the core of the Blood Saint's power.

"Incredible," he whispered, his eyes glowing with a faint radiance.

"The workmanship... it's beyond anything I've ever seen. It's as if the stone itself is alive."

Mok Gyeong-un stood over him, his transparent eyes fixed on the stone.

"The stone is not just an object, smith. It is a part of the void. and it's mine."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint, crimson glow that reflected in the stone.

"Now, tell me. How do I use this focus to complete the ritual?"

Ou Ye-zi smiled, a wise and knowing smile.

"You don't use it, boy. You let it use you. You become the conduit for its will."

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"I am no one's conduit. I am the master of my own fate."

He reached out his hand and took the stone from the smith.

The game was no longer a quest. It was a completion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was finding the last piece.
