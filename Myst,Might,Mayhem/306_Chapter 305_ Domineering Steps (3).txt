Chapter 305: Domineering Steps (3)

-The patron continues to provide support.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently reviewing the records of the various sects and clans that had joined his new world.

The "patron" was a reference to the void itself, which was providing the boy with the energy and the knowledge he needed to rule.

"The patronage... it's a double-edged sword, Master," the Demonic Monk said, his voice now a mere echo.

"It gives you power, but it also demands a price. and the price is your very existence."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"I am the void, Monk. and there is no price I can't afford to pay."

He stood up and walked to the balcony, overlooking the city.

"The domineering steps... they're just the beginning. and soon, the entire world will be under my feet."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a faint radiance that illuminated the city.

"The future is mine. and I am the only one who will write it."

The game was no longer a domination. It was a manifestation of will. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the script.
