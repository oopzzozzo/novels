Chapter 289: The Northern Sect Blade King (2)

Cheong-ryeong, who was currently in the Sun Tower, felt a wave of cold dread as she sensed the arrival of the Northern Sect army.

The army was massive, numbering in the thousands, and they were all masters of the blade.

"The Blade King... he's here," she whispered, her eyes full of hidden alarm.

Mok Gyeong-un stood on the balcony, watching as the army approached the city walls.

"The Blade King... he thinks his steel can stop the void," he said, his voice flat and neutral.

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a blinding light that illuminated the entire city.

"Let him come. I've been waiting for a challenge worthy of the master of the universe."

Suddenly, a massive shockwave hit the city walls, followed by the sound of clashing steel and agonizing screams.

The battle for the Imperial City had begun. and the North was leading the assault.

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a total war. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was surviving it.
