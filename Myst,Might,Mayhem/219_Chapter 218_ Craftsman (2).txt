Chapter 218: Craftsman (2)

-Through the gaps between the shattered gate, Mok Gyeong-un could see the inner chamber of the Garden of the Sun.

It was a small, beautifully maintained space, filled with rare plants and ancient artifacts.

In the center of the chamber, on a low table, sat the final focus.

It was a large, transparent crystal that seemed to contain all the light of the sun.

"The Sun Crystal... it's beautiful," he whispered to himself.

Han Gyeong-ho, who was still standing in the garden, lunged forward with a desperate cry.

"Don't touch it! It is the core of the Empire's power!"

Mok Gyeong-un didn't even look back. He just raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, shattering the craftsman's sword and knocking him to the ground.

"The Empire's power is a shadow, Gyeong-ho. and shadows are meant to be consumed by the void."

He walked into the chamber and picked up the crystal.

As his hand touched the surface, he felt a sudden surge of heat and radiance.

It was a power that was the exact opposite of the void, a force of life and creation.

The game was becoming a fusion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was bringing the opposites together.
