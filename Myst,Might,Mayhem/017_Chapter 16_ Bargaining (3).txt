Chapter 16: Bargaining (3)

The second young master, Mok Eun-pyeong, had grand ambitions.

From a young age, he not only mastered martial arts but also delved into the art of strategy and governance.

He was the one many believed should be the next Manor Master.

However, his mother, Lady Seok, had other plans.

She favored her eldest son, Mok Jin-pyeong, despite his lack of talent and ambition.

Mok Eun-pyeong knew this, and it fueled a quiet, burning resentment within him.

He was currently in his own study, reviewing reports from his informants.

"Young Master."

A man dressed in black appeared from the shadows.

"What is it?"

"The boy that Lady Seok brought in... he's more than he seems."

Mok Eun-pyeong looked up from his reports.

"Go on."

"He saved the Manor Master's life. And it's rumored he knows where the seal is hidden."

Mok Eun-pyeong's eyes narrowed.

"The seal..."

If he could get his hands on the seal, he wouldn't need his mother's support.

He would be the undisputed master of Yeon Mok Sword Manor.

"Keep a close watch on him. If Lady Seok moves to kill him, intervene."

"Yes, Young Master."

The man in black disappeared as quickly as he had arrived.

Mok Eun-pyeong stared at the flickering candle on his desk.

The game was changing. And he intended to win.
