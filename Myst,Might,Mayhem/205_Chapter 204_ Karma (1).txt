Chapter 204: Karma (1)

Whoosh!

Seop Chun, the leader of the Heaven & Earth Society's investigation unit, was currently making his way towards the Imperial City.

He had been tasked with finding out what had happened to the third disciple, Wi So-yeon, and the first disciple, Wi Maeng.

The two brothers had been missing for weeks, and the leader was becoming increasingly concerned.

"They were supposed to meet with the master of the void," Seop Chun muttered to himself as he rode his horse through the forest.

"But something went wrong. and now, the entire Society is in a state of chaos."

Suddenly, he was stopped by a group of cloaked figures.

"Halt! State your business on this road!" one of the figures commanded.

Seop Chun drew his sword.

"I am Seop Chun, leader of the investigation unit. and I am on official business for the Society."

The figures laughed, a cold and hollow sound.

"The Society? Your society is dead, investigator. and your leader is just a shadow."

The figures threw off their cloaks, revealing themselves to be members of Mok Gyeong-un's shadow unit.

"We are the ones who rule now. and we don't allow investigators on our roads."

The confrontation had begun. and Seop Chun was the next target.

The game was becoming a liquidation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was doing the counting.
