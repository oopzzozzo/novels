Chapter 174: Three Eyes (2)

-A scroll? I have never seen such a thing in the Society's records.

Cheong-ryeong's voice was full of curiosity as she examined the ancient scroll Mok Gyeong-un had found in the Temple of the Moon.

It was a manuscript on the origins of the void gods, and it contained information that was not in any of the Society's other records.

"The void gods... they are not just entities of power," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice solemn.

"They are remnants of a lost civilization. A civilization that was destroyed by its own mastery of the void."

He turned a page, revealing an illustration of a city made of shadow and bone.

"This is their city. The City of the Void. and it's where they are hiding."

Cheong-ryeong's eyes widened.

"Hiding? You mean they're not in the void?"

"No. they're in a threshold. A place between our world and the void. and the second focus is the key to entering that threshold."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up and walked towards the temple's entrance.

"The Three Eyes... they're not here to capture me. They're here to protect the gateway."

The game was no longer a confrontation. It was a discovery. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was uncovering the truth.
