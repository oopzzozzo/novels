Chapter 259: The Mark (6)

Last night.

-Plop!

The small, black symbol on Mok Gyeong-un's arm began to leak a thick, dark fluid.

It was the essence of the void, a materialization of the energy that he had been absorbing for weeks.

"The Mark... it's overflowing," the Demonic Monk said, his voice full of hidden terror.

"The container is no longer sufficient. and the energy is seeking a new outlet."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at his arm, his eyes black and lifeless.

"I am the outlet, Monk. and the world is my container."

He raised his left arm, and the dark fluid surged forward, creating a wave of shadow that washed over the room.

The air became cold and heavy, and the very air seemed to vibrate with the power of the void.

"The final transformation is almost complete. and soon, there will be nothing left of the old world."

He turned towards the Sun Tower's central ritual chamber.

"The Apotheosis is coming. and I am the one who will achieve it."

The game was no longer a deification. It was an apocalypse. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was surviving it.
