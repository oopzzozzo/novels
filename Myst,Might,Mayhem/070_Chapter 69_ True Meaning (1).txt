Chapter 69: True Meaning (1)

Inside a room with walls covered in talismans and numerous ritual instruments.

A white-haired Taoist priest was meditating, his eyes closed.

He was the Grand Diviner of the Heaven & Earth Society, a man who had spent his entire life studying the mysteries of the universe.

Suddenly, his eyes snapped open.

"The gateway... it's open," he whispered, his voice trembling with a mixture of fear and excitement.

He stood up and walked to a large, ornate mirror that sat in the corner of the room.

The mirror's surface was dark and swirling, like a portal to another world.

"Mok Gyeong-un... you've done it."

The Grand Diviner reached out his hand towards the mirror.

"Now, let's see what you've become."

The mirror's surface rippled and cleared, revealing the image of a young boy standing in a secret chamber, his entire body glowing with a crimson light.

"A master of the void... and a vessel for the old gods."

The Grand Diviner smiled.

"The ritual is complete. The true meaning of existence is finally within our reach."

The game was no longer a conquest. It was an enlightenment. And the Grand Diviner was ready to receive it.
