Chapter 184: Submission (3)

"Can you make her submit?"

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was flat, but it carried an underlying threat.

He was facing Wi So-yeon, who was currently holding a young woman captive.

She was the Emperor's daughter, the Princess of the Empire.

"I can, Master. but it will take time," So-yeon replied, her voice now a mere whisper.

"The Princess is strong-willed. and her loyalty to her father is absolute."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Loyalty is just a concept, So-yeon. And strong wills can be broken."

He stepped closer to the Princess.

"Now, tell me. Where is the fifth focus? I know the Emperor had a secret reserve."

The Princess looked at him with her sharp, intelligent eyes.

"I will never tell you, monster! You've destroyed my family! You've destroyed my empire!"

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"I haven't destroyed them, Princess. I've merely... liberated them. Of their mortal burdens."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"Now, give me the focus. Or face the same liberation."

The game was becoming a hostage situation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was making the demands.
