Chapter 91: Goal (2)

Transformation Realm.

It was the supreme martial arts realm that could only be reached by surpassing the boundaries of the physical world.

It was the goal of every warrior in the Society, the ultimate achievement.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently reading a manuscript on the principles of the Transformation Realm.

"surpassing the physical world... it sounds a lot like the void," he muttered.

The Demonic Monk, now a mere shadow, watched with interest.

"The Transformation Realm is... similar to the void, Great One. But it is still tied to the physical world. It is a refinement of human energy, not a connection to the fundamental darkness."

"And can a master of the void reach the Transformation Realm?"

"They can do more than that, Master. They can transcend the realm entirely. They can become something... beyond."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Beyond the Transformation Realm... I like the sound of that."

He turned a page, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

"Now, tell me. How do I achieve this transcendence?"

"It requires... a catalyst. A source of power that is equal to the void."

"And where do we find that?"

"In the heart of the Society. In the Treasure Vault."

Mok Gyeong-un's smile widened.

"The Treasure Vault... it seems I have another heist to plan."

The game was becoming a quest for divinity. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was seeking the prize.
