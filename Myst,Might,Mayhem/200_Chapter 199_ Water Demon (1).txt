Chapter 199: Water Demon (1)

"Now there won't be any more disruptions."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was calm, but it carried an underlying threat.

He was currently in the Sun Tower, facing the Shadow Clan Master.

"The defectors have been eliminated, Master. and the Society is once again unified."

The Shadow Clan Master nodded.

"I understand. and what now, Young Master?"

"Now, we proceed with the next stage of the ritual. The stage that involves the Water Demon."

The Demonic Monk's form flickered with excitement.

"The Water Demon... the ancient entity that rules over the rivers and lakes. and the one who is the key to the final focus."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Yes. And I intend to find him. and I intend to make him mine."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint, blue glow that illuminated the entire tower.

"The quest for the fourth focus begins now."

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a maritime adventure. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was taking the helm.
