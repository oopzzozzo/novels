Chapter 297: Shaolin Temple (4)

"Yes, that footprint is the key."

Mok Gyeong-un was currently examining a large, stone footprint that sat in the corner of the pagoda.

It was a remnant of the first master's presence, and it contained the final clue to the location of the City of the Void.

"The City... it's not in the void. it's in the sky," Mok Gyeong-un whispered, his eyes transparent and clear.

"The heavens are the threshold. and the City is its gateway."

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The sky... but that's impossible, Master! No one can reach the heavens without the gods' permission!"

"I am the Void Sun, Monk. and I don't need anyone's permission."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a gateway that led directly to the sky.

"The final battle is close. and I intend to win."

He stepped through the gateway, followed by the light of the eighth focus.

The game was no longer a quest. It was an invasion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the primary invader.
