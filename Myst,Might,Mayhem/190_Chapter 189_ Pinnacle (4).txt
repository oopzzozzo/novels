Chapter 189: Pinnacle (4)

A hundred years ago, the first master of the void had reached the pinnacle and then disappeared.

No one knew where he had gone or what had happened to him.

But now, Mok Gyeong-un knew.

He was standing in the middle of the void, surrounded by the remnants of that master's power.

"I see... you've been waiting for me," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice echoing through the darkness.

A figure appeared from the shadows. It was a man in ancient robes, his eyes glowing with a cold, blue fire.

He was the first master.

"You've come a long way, boy," the master said, his voice a low and raspy whisper.

"I've come to take your place," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his eyes transparent and clear.

The master laughed.

"Take my place? You think you're ready to rule the void?"

"I am the void. and rule is just a mortal concept."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the final battle for the void began.

The game was no longer a transformation. It was a deification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming a god.
