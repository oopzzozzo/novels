Chapter 290: The Northern Sect Blade King (3)

The energy Gu Seong-baek unleashed was like a tsunami of steel, crushing everything in its path.

He was currently in the center of the city's main square, facing Mok Gyeong-un's shadow agents.

The agents were fast and fluid, but they were no match for the Blade King's raw power and mastery of the sword.

One by one, the agents fell, their bodies broken and their souls consumed by the void.

Gu Seong-baek laughed, a deep and booming sound that echoed through the square.

"Is this all you have, boy?! Is this the power of the master of the void?!"

Mok Gyeong-un appeared at the top of the Sun Tower, his transparent eyes fixed on the Blade King.

"You're loud, Seong-baek. but you're also foolish."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a shockwave that rattled the entire city.

"The void is not just a place of power. It's a place of silence. and soon, you will know the true meaning of that silence."

The battle for the Imperial City was reaching its climax. and the winner would rule the universe.

The game was no longer a total war. It was a deification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming a god.
