Chapter 165: Variables (1)

On the main road leading to the Imperial City, a small group of travelers were making their way towards the capital.

They were Mok Gyeong-un, Wi Maeng, and a few of their top agents.

They were traveling in disguise, blending into the crowd of merchants and pilgrims.

"The Imperial City... it's a place of many variables, Master," Wi Maeng said, his voice low.

"I'm aware of that, Maeng. but variables are just opportunities in disguise."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the distant city walls, his eyes clear and focused.

"The second focus is there. and I intend to find it."

Suddenly, they were stopped by a group of imperial guards.

"Halt! State your business in the capital!" one of the guards commanded.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, his eyes glowing with a faint, dark light.

"We're just simple travelers, guard. Looking for a bit of enlightenment."

The guard hesitated. He could feel the power of the boy's presence, and the absolute dominance of his will.

"Proceed. but be careful. The city is a dangerous place these days."

Mok Gyeong-un nodded and they continued their journey.

The game was becoming a quest. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the way.
