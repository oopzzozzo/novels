Chapter 207: Karma (4)

Flinch!

'He's… smiling?'

The boatman who was steering Mok Gyeong-un's vessel felt a wave of cold dread as he saw the boy's expression.

Mok Gyeong-un was standing at the bow of the boat, staring at the distant city walls with a bright, almost innocent smile.

But his eyes were as still as a dead person's.

"The Imperial City... it's a place of many opportunities," the boy said, his voice calm and melodic.

"Opportunities for what, Young Master?" the boatman managed to stammer.

"Opportunities for... karma. For the repayment of old debts."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint, crimson glow that reflected in the water.

"The city is a web of connections. and I am the one who is going to pull the strings."

The boatman was silent. He could feel the power of the boy's presence, and the absolute dominance of his will.

"Proceed. and don't stop until we reach the main dock."

The game was no longer a pilgrimage. It was an infiltration. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the assault.
