Chapter 89: Master of the Primal Killing Pavilion (5)

"Do you still not want to take the opportunity?"

The moment he heard this, chills ran down his entire body.

He was facing Mok Gyeong-un, who was now a being of pure void energy.

"The opportunity... to become a god?" he managed to gasp, his voice full of fear and longing.

"Yes. A god who rules over the void. A god who is beyond the reach of life and death."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, a smile that seemed to contain all the secrets of the universe.

"But you must be willing to sacrifice everything. Your humanity, your memories, your very soul."

He stepped closer to the man.

"Are you ready to take the opportunity?"

The man hesitated for a second, but then he nodded.

"I am ready. I will do whatever it takes."

Mok Gyeong-un's smile widened.

"Then let the transformation begin."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, consuming the man in a blinding, crimson light.

The game was no longer a countdown. It was an event. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was hosting it.
