Chapter 78: Silent Strides (1)

-Swoosh!

A faint haze rose along with sweat from the muscles of Mok Yu-cheon's bare upper body.

As energy coursed through his meridians, his movements became faster and more precise.

He was currently practicing the Silent Strides, a technique that allowed the user to move without sound or presence.

It was one of the most difficult techniques in the Yeon Mok Sword Style, but Yu-cheon was mastering it with ease.

Mok Gyeong-un watched from the sidelines, his eyes analytical.

"Your technique is improving, Yu-cheon. But you're still thinking too much."

Yu-cheon stopped and wiped the sweat from his forehead.

"Thinking? But Master, you said precision is everything."

"Precision is important. but so is flow. You must become the technique. You must move as if you were the void itself."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped into the courtyard and demonstrated the technique.

He moved in a blur of motion, his body appearing and disappearing as if he were a ghost.

And there was no sound. No sound at all.

Yu-cheon's eyes widened in awe.

"That's... that's incredible."

"It's not incredible. It's just... silent. Now, try again. And this time, don't think. Just move."

The game was becoming a mastery. And Mok Yu-cheon was the one who was learning from the best.
