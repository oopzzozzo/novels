Chapter 157: Coercion (3)

It was not only his body but also his soul that was being tested.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently locked in a spiritual battle with the Society Leader, the two of them clashing in the mental plane.

The leader's presence was a vast, swirling mass of shadow and malice, while Mok Gyeong-un's was a focused, intense light of pure void energy.

"You think you can defeat me in my own domain?" the leader's voice echoed in the mental plane.

"This is not your domain, Leader. This is the domain of the void. And I am its master."

Mok Gyeong-un surged forward, his light cutting through the leader's shadows like a hot knife through butter.

"You are just a man who has lost his way. A man who has allowed himself to be consumed by his own ambition."

"And you? What are you but a boy who has found a power he doesn't understand?"

"I understand it better than you ever could. Because I am not trying to control it. I am trying to become it."

Mok Gyeong-un's light expanded, filling the entire mental plane with a blinding, crimson radiance.

The leader's shadows began to dissolve, and his presence began to weaken.

"No! This... this is impossible!"

"Nothing is impossible when you have the void on your side."

The mental battle was coming to an end. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was standing.
