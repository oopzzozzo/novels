Chapter 308: Reunion (1)

Looking at the backs of the government officials as they left the manor, Mok Gyeong-un felt a sense of finality.

The Empire was now officially at peace, and the new master of the void was recognized as the supreme ruler.

But for Mok Gyeong-un, the true reunion was just beginning.

He turned towards the inner hall, where a group of people were waiting for him.

They were the survivors of his original village, the ones who had been taken by the void gods years ago.

"Master... you've brought them back," Wi Maeng whispered, his sightless eyes filled with tears.

"I have. and I've given them their lives back."

Mok Gyeong-un walked towards the group, his golden-white eyes scanning their faces.

They were all changed, of course. They had been touched by the void, and they would never be truly human again.

But they were alive. and they were free.

"Grandpa... are you there?" a young boy asked, his voice full of hope.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, a smile that was finally real.

"I am here, child. and I'm never leaving again."

The game was over. and the reunion was complete.

But for Mok Gyeong-un, the story was just starting.
