Chapter 253: Demon (3)

Just a moment ago…

-Swoosh!

The dark energy was swirling around Mok Gyeong-un as he entered the Hall of Eternal Darkness.

It was a place of absolute shadow, where the light of the sun could never reach.

In the center of the hall sat the Demon King, a being of pure void energy.

"So, you've come to face me, boy," the Demon King said, his voice a chorus of a thousand voices.

"I haven't come to face you. I've come to integrate you," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his eyes transparent and clear.

He raised his left arm, and the golden-white energy surged forward, clashing with the Demon King's own power.

The hall was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the final confrontation.

It was a battle of two masters of the void, each seeking to consume the other.

The other members of the Society watched from the entrance of the hall, their own lives hanging in the balance.

The game was no longer a showdown. It was an apocalypse. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was surviving it.
