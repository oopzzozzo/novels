Cheong-ryeong clicked her tongue as she saw Mok Gyeong-un using the convergence energy to refine his own soul.

She had wondered how he would handle the overwhelming power of the seven focuses, but now she saw that he was integrating them with a precision that was almost terrifying.

He was currently in the Sun Tower's central ritual chamber, his entire body glowing with a blinding, white radiance.

The final stage of the ritual was just a few moments away, and the air in the chamber was thick with the power of the void.

"The Apotheosis is here, Master," the Demonic Monk said, his voice now a mere ripple in the air.

"The opposites have been fused. and the void is now complete."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For the world is now mine to reshape."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a wave of creation that washed over the entire cosmos.

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a masterpiece. And Mok Gyeong-un was the artist.

The world was reborn. and the shadow of the past was no more.
