Chapter 126: Shadow Clan (3)

The name of the sharp-featured young man was Hwan Yun-myeong.

Abandoned by his parents at a young age, he had been raised by the Shadow Clan, trained to be a master of deception and lethal force.

He was one of the clan's most promising agents, and he had never failed a mission.

Until now.

He was currently facing Mok Gyeong-un in the middle of the manor's courtyard.

"You're good, Yun-myeong. But you're still bound by the laws of this world," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice neutral.

"I am bound by my duty to the clan!" Yun-myeong replied, his sword moving in a blur of motion.

But Mok Gyeong-un just raised his left hand and caught the blade between his fingers.

"Your duty is meaningless in the face of the void."

With a flick of his wrist, he snapped the blade in two.

Yun-myeong fell back, his face a mask of cold determination.

"I will not yield. The Shadow King will have your head."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then tell him to come and take it. I'm waiting."

The game was becoming a duel of principles. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the truth.
