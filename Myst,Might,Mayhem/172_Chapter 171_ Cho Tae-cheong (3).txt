Chapter 171: Cho Tae-cheong (3)

Gasp!

The girl, Wi So-yeon, was staring in shock as she saw Cho Tae-cheong fall.

She had always believed that Tae-cheong was invincible, but now she saw him as just another mortal.

"Tae-cheong... no..." she whispered, her eyes filling with tears.

Mok Gyeong-un ignored her and looked at Tae-cheong, who was struggling to get up.

"Now, tell me. Where is the focus? I want it. and I want it now."

Tae-cheong gritted his teeth, his face a mask of pain and fury.

"I... I will... never... tell you," he managed to choke out.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"We'll see about that."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, wrapping around Tae-cheong's neck.

The street was silent as the interrogation continued.

The game was becoming a liquidation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was doing the counting.
