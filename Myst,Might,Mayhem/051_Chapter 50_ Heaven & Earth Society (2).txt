Chapter 50: Heaven & Earth Society (2)

"You... saw that?"

Mok Gyeong-un sensed bewilderment in the Bright Blade King Son Yun's expression.

In the midst of the chaos in the courtyard, the Bright Blade King, a high-ranking member of the Heaven & Earth Society, had been observing the boy.

He had seen the dark energy, the red thread, and the absolute dominance Mok Gyeong-un displayed.

"I saw... something that shouldn't exist," Son Yun replied, his voice a mixture of awe and fear.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"And what was that?"

"A master of the void. A being who can command the very fabric of reality."

Son Yun stepped forward, his hand on the hilt of his sword.

"The Heaven & Earth Society has been searching for someone like you for a long time. Someone who can lead us to the ultimate truth."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes glinted.

"And you think I am that someone?"

"I am certain of it. But first, you must prove yourself. You must show us that you are worthy of our service."

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"Prove myself? I have already proven myself to the void. I have no need to prove myself to you."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"But if you want a demonstration of my power... I will gladly provide it."

The courtyard was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the Bright Blade King's counter-attack.

The game was becoming a battle of titans. And Mok Gyeong-un was ready to take the crown.
