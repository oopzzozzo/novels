Chapter 268: The Fourth Office (4)

"Mmph."

The experimental subject was staring at Mok Gyeong-un with a mixture of terror and realization.

He had just been "upgraded" by the boy's power, and he could feel the dark energy of the void coursing through his veins.

"It's... it's perfect," he whispered, his own malice replaced by a fanatical devotion.

Mok Gyeong-un stood over him, his transparent eyes fixed on the subject.

"You are now a part of the void, subject. and you will serve me until the end of the world."

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The upgrade is a success, Great One. and soon, the entire shadow unit will be at your level."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For the world is now ready for the final stage of the ritual."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a blinding, white radiance that illuminated the entire laboratory.

"The Void Sun has risen. and the shadow of the past is no more."

The game was over for the Fourth Office. but for the universe, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un was now the undisputed ruler of the cosmos.
