Chapter 114: Disciple Scramble (1)

'This is troublesome.'

Lee Ji-yeom, the Corpse Blood Valley Master, had intended to ascend the platform and claim his place as the leader's successor.

But now, he was facing a scramble for disciples.

The other high-ranking members of the Society were all trying to recruit the most talented warriors for their own units.

And Mok Gyeong-un was the primary target.

"Young Master Mok. I would be honored to have you in the Corpse Blood Valley," Ji-yeom said, his voice full of hidden desperation.

"I can provide you with the resources and the training you need to reach the Transformation Realm."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at him with his black, lifeless eyes.

"The Transformation Realm... I've already told you, I'm aiming for something more."

Ji-yeom's expression hardened.

"And what could be more than the Transformation Realm?"

"transcendence. Divinity. The absolute control of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un turned to the other members.

"I have no need for your training or your resources. I have the void. and that's all I need."

He walked away, leaving the high-ranking members in a state of shock and confusion.

The game was becoming a scramble for power. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the ace.
