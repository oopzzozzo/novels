Chapter 111: The Final Gate (2)

The Bright Blade King Son Yun remembered Mok Gyeong-un as clearly being at a mere third-rate level.

One who only knew basic sword forms and possessed almost no internal energy.

But the boy who was now standing in the leader's quarters was something else entirely.

His presence was overwhelming, his power absolute.

"Young Master... you've changed," Son Yun managed to say, his voice full of awe.

"Change is the only constant in the void, Son Yun," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his eyes black and lifeless.

He was currently facing the leader of the Society, a man who sat on a high throne, his face hidden in shadow.

"So, you are the one who has been causing all this chaos," the leader said, his voice deep and raspy.

"I am the one who has been creating a new order," Mok Gyeong-un corrected him.

"A new order? And what role do you think I have in this order?"

"You have no role. Your time is over. I am the new master of this world."

The leader laughed, a cold and hollow sound.

"You think you can defeat me? I am the one who built this society. I am the one who has studied the void for decades."

"And yet, you are still a mortal. Bound by your own ambition and your own fears."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"I am the void. And I have no fears."

The game was becoming a battle of ideologies. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was winning.
