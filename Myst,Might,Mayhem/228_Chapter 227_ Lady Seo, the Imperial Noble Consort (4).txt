Chapter 227: Lady Seo, the Imperial Noble Consort (4)

"The Palace is mine."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was calm, even as he made the declaration that would change the history of the Empire.

He was currently standing on the main balcony of the Palace, overlooking the entire city.

Lady Seo was standing beside him, her expression a mask of cold satisfaction.

The takeover was complete. Every unit of the imperial guard had surrendered, and every official had pledged their loyalty to the new master.

"The Empire is a shadow, Consort. and shadows are meant to be consumed by the void."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a blinding, golden light that illuminated the entire city.

"From today onward, there is no more empire. There is only the domain of the Void Sun."

The citizens of the city erupted in cheers, a sound of raw, primal energy that shook the very foundations of the city.

The game was over for the mortal world. but for the void gods, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un sat on the Imperial Throne, his eyes black and lifeless.

He was the new Master of the Universe.
