Chapter 249: Consort Ho (3)

"I wonder if someone like me could really handle the power of the void."

Consort Ho's voice was barely a whisper as she looked at the dark energy swirling around Mok Gyeong-un's left arm.

She was currently in Prince Gyeongjin's library, and she had just been shown a glimpse of the boy's true power.

"The void is not for the faint of heart, Consort. and it's certainly not for those who are seeking petty power," Mok Gyeong-un said, his golden-white eyes fixed on her.

"But if you're willing to sacrifice everything, then anything is possible."

Consort Ho felt a sudden, sharp pain in her chest.

"Everything? You mean... my life? my family? my ambition?"

"I mean your soul, Consort. and your existence."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"But in exchange, you'll become more than just a mortal. You'll become a master of the void."

The game was no longer a seduction or an interrogation. It was a mobilization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the opportunity.

The final stage of the ritual was just a few days away. and the world would never be the same again.
