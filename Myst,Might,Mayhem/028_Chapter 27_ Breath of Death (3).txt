Chapter 27: Breath of Death (3)

The guard Go Chan stared intently at Mok Gyeong-un and soon steadied his breathing.

Everything this fellow did was unsettling.

But he had no choice but to follow him now.

"Go Chan. You're curious, aren't you?"

Mok Gyeong-un spoke without looking back.

"Curious about what, Great One?"

"About the power. The power that can kill without touching."

Go Chan remained silent.

"It's called the Breath of Death. And it's not a martial art. It's a connection to the void."

Mok Gyeong-un stopped and turned to face him.

"The void? What is that?"

"It's the space between worlds. The place where all energy begins and ends."

He raised his left arm, and the red thread pulsed with a dark, intense light.

"And I am the one who can tap into it. I am the one who can control the void."

Go Chan felt a chill run down his spine.

This boy was truly a monster.

"Now, tell me. Do you want to see it?"

Go Chan hesitated, but then he nodded.

"Yes. I want to see it."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. Then watch closely. This is the first lesson."

He reached out his hand towards a nearby tree.

And the tree instantly withered and died.

Go Chan's eyes widened.

The power of the void was real. And it was terrifying.
