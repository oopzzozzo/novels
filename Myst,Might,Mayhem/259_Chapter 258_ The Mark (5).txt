Chapter 258: The Mark (5)

In an office at the main hall of the manor, Mok Gyeong-un was meeting with his top advisors.

They were discussing the final stage of the ritual, and the tension in the room was palpable.

"The gateway is open, Master. and the void is waiting," the Shadow King said, his voice full of anticipation.

"But we still need a catalyst. A source of power that can anchor the void's energy in the physical world."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the Sun Crystal, which sat on his desk.

It was glowing with an intense, white radiance, its power pulsing in rhythm with his own heartbeat.

"The catalyst is here, King. and I am the one who will activate it."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, wrapping around the crystal.

"The final transformation is beginning. and soon, the world will know the true meaning of the Void Sun."

He turned to the Demonic Monk, who was watching from the shadows.

"The time has come, Monk. Let the Apotheosis reach its conclusion."

The room was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the final ritual.

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a deification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming a god.
