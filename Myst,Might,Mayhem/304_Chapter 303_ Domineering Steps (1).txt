Chapter 303: Domineering Steps (1)

-Thud! Thud!

The sound of Mok Gyeong-un's steps echoed through the Imperial City, each one sending a shockwave of power through the ground.

He was currently walking towards the Palace's main hall, his presence now a silent, overwhelming force that made the citizens bow their heads in absolute submission.

"The master is here! The god has returned!" they shouted, their voices full of fanatical devotion.

Mok Gyeong-un ignored them. His focus was entirely on the final stage of the ritual.

"The transformation is complete, Master," the Shadow King said, appearing beside him.

"And the world is now ready for your command."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of changes to make."

He reached the main hall and sat on the Imperial Throne.

"From today onward, the universe is under my control. and I am the only law."

The game was no longer a deification. It was a rule. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the laws.
