Chapter 106: Lord of the Western Sea (3)

The red line drawn on the floor throughout the entire cavity.

Glancing at it, Mok Gyeong-un said to the entity within the wooden box,

"So this is your circle of power. A boundary that separates your domain from the rest of the world."

"It is, human. And once you cross it, you are mine."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"And what if I don't cross it? What if I simply... erase it?"

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a wave of shadow that washed over the red line.

The line flickered and faded, the entity's presence weakening with it.

"Impossible! You cannot erase the boundary of a god!"

"I just did. And now, you're just... an entity without a home."

Mok Gyeong-un reached into the box and pulled out a small, blue pearl.

It was the Heart of the Sea, the source of the deity's power.

"I think I'll take this. It might be useful for the ritual."

The deity's voice became a mere whisper.

"You... you are a monster, human."

"I know. And I'm just getting started."

The game was becoming a collection. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was taking the prizes.
