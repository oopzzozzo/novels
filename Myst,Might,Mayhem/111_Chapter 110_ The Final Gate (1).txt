Chapter 110: The Final Gate (1)

She couldn't even perceive it, yet the tip of his finger was touching her.

"You're slow."

Drip!

At Mok Gyeong-un's words, a drop of blood fell from the finger of the woman who had tried to attack him.

She was an elite warrior of the Society, a member of the guardian unit of the inner castle.

But she had been defeated before she could even see the boy move.

"How... how are you so fast?" she managed to gasp, her eyes full of shock.

"I'm not fast. I'm just... existing in a different rhythm," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his voice neutral.

He was standing in front of the Final Gate, the entrance to the leader's private quarters.

"The gate is closed, Young Master. No one is allowed to enter without the leader's permission."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"I have the void's permission. And that's all I need."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, shattering the gate into a thousand pieces.

The path to the leader was open. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was stepping through.

The game was becoming a confrontation. And the leader was the one who was waiting.
