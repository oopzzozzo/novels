Chapter 67: Gu Poison (3)

It was on the verge of completing its transformation, so it was gradually regaining its reason.

Of course, it was still a monster. But it was a monster with a purpose.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently in his secret chamber, watching as the void Gu emerged from the jar.

It was a humanoid creature, its skin a swirling mass of shadow and light.

Its eyes were a cold, blue fire, just like the Guyeo.

"Master," it said, its voice a haunting melody.

"You are ready," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his own eyes glowing with dark energy.

"I am ready to serve. I am ready to complete the ritual."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then let's not waste any more time. The gateway is waiting."

He raised his left arm, and the red thread connected to the Gu.

Together, they began the final stage of the ritual.

The air in the chamber became thick with the power of the void, and the very foundations of the manor began to tremble.

The game was no longer a transformation. It was a rebirth. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was being reborn.
