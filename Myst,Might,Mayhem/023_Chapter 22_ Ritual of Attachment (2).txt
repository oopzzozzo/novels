Chapter 22: Ritual of Attachment (2)

-Can you feel it? That's the subtle principle of the Ritual of Binding. It can pull and attach anything...

The Demonic Monk's voice echoed in Mok Gyeong-un's mind.

The boy was standing in the courtyard of the main hall, his left arm pulsing with dark energy.

"I feel it. It's like... a magnet. Pulling at the very souls of those around me."

"Yes. And once you have enough blood, you can bind them to your will. Permanently."

Mok Gyeong-un watched as a group of guards approached him.

"You there! What are you doing in the main courtyard?"

Mok Gyeong-un didn't answer. He just raised his left arm.

The red thread glowed with a fierce, crimson light.

"He... he's one of the prisoners! Arrest him!"

The guards rushed forward, their swords drawn.

But as they got closer, they began to slow down.

Their movements became sluggish, as if they were moving through thick mud.

"Wh-what's happening? My body... I can't move!"

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"The binding... it's already started."

He stepped forward, his eyes fixed on the guards.

"Now, give me your blood. I have a ritual to complete."

The courtyard was filled with the sound of steel clashing against steel, followed by the agonizing screams of the dying.

The ritual was progressing. And Mok Gyeong-un was enjoying every moment of it.
