Chapter 292: Convergence (2)

-Slap!

The boy was staring at Mok Gyeong-un with a mixture of disbelief and gratitude.

He had just been "upgraded" by the power of convergence, and he could feel the dark energy of the void and the golden radiance of the sun coursing through his veins.

"It's... it's perfect," he whispered, his own malice replaced by a fanatical devotion.

Mok Gyeong-un stood over him, his transparent eyes fixed on the boy.

"You are now a part of the convergence, boy. and you will serve me until the end of the world."

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The upgrade is a success, Great One. and soon, the entire shadow unit will be at your level."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For the world is now ready for the final stage of the ritual."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a blinding light that illuminated the entire city.

"The Void Sun has risen. and the shadow of the past is no more."

The game was over for the convergence unit. but for the universe, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un was now the undisputed ruler of the cosmos.
