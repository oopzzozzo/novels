Chapter 15: Bargaining (2)

-Bang!

Upon returning to her residence, Hyehwa Hall, the First Madam Lady Seok kicked a chair violently.

The intense anger that had been suppressed in the Manor Master's room erupted all at once.

"That insignificant bastard dare to threaten me?!"

Her face was distorted like a demon's.

She was the one who practically ruled Yeon Mok Sword Manor.

Even the Manor Master couldn't easily ignore her words.

But to be toyed with by a mere boy who was brought in as a substitute?

It was an unbearable humiliation.

"Madam, please calm down."

Her personal maid, who had been waiting, quickly approached and tried to soothe her.

"How can I calm down?! That bastard knows about the seal!"

Lady Seok's eyes were filled with murderous intent.

The Manor Master's seal.

It was the symbol of the Manor Master's authority and the key to the manor's secret vault.

She had been searching for it for a long time, but the Manor Master had hidden it so well that she couldn't find it.

But that boy said he knew where it was.

"We must kill him. We must kill him immediately after we get the seal!"

"But Madam, the Manor Master has regained consciousness. If something happens to the boy now..."

"I don't care! I'll find a way to make it look like an accident!"

Lady Seok gritted her teeth.

She wouldn't let that boy live.

Never.
