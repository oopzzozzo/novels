Chapter 150: Talent (1)

The estate located south of the inner castle, which had been the training ground for the Society's elite units, was now a bustling hub of activity.

Mok Gyeong-un had ordered the recruitment of new disciples, and hundreds of young men and women had come from all over the region to apply.

They were all hoping to catch the attention of the new master, to be chosen for his shadow unit.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently overseeing the initial testing, his lifeless eyes scanning the crowd of applicants.

"They have potential, Great One. But most of them lack the necessary... disposition," the Demonic Monk said, his voice a mere whisper in the boy's mind.

"Disposition? You mean they're too human?"

"Exactly. To command the void, one must be willing to let go of their humanity. One must be willing to become a monster."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then I'll just have to find the monsters among them. Or I'll have to create them myself."

He walked through the rows of applicants, his presence a cold, suffocating pressure that made many of them flinch.

He was looking for talent. but he was also looking for something far more dangerous.

The game was becoming a search for kindred spirits. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was doing the seeking.
