Chapter 284: Entanglements (4)

It was when Six Offices Commander Mu Jang-yak arrived at the Sun Tower that he realized the extent of the entanglements.

The entire tower was now pulsing with a rhythmic, dark energy, and the air was thick with the power of the void.

"The synchronization is complete, Master," he said, his voice full of absolute, fanatical devotion.

"The entire empire is now connected to your soul."

Mok Gyeong-un stood on the high balcony, his golden-white eyes scanning the distant provinces.

"And the world? Are they starting to feel it too?"

"They are, Master. The news of your ascension has reached even the most remote corners of the globe. and the citizens are already bowing their heads."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For the universe is now ready for the final stage of the ritual."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a blinding light that illuminated the entire world.

"The Void Sun has risen. and the shadow of the past is no more."

The game was over for the old world. but for the new one, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un was now the eternal master of all things.
