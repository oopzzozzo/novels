Chapter 72: True Meaning (4)

"I'll accept the seven of you who survive as my teammates."

'!?'

The boys, who had been tensely waiting in the training courtyard, were shocked by Mok Gyeong-un's words.

They were all candidates for the Society's elite assassin unit, and they had been told that only one of them would survive the final test.

But now, the new master of the manor was offering them a different path.

"What do you mean, Young Master?" one of the boys asked, his voice trembling with hope and fear.

"I mean that I have a different use for you. I need warriors who are more than just killers. I need warriors who can command the void."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes glinted with dark energy.

"But the path is dangerous. And only the strongest will survive. So, I will let you choose. You can either fight each other to the death, as planned. Or you can join me and face the challenges of the void."

The boys looked at each other, then at the boy.

They knew they were choosing between a certain death and a chance at ultimate power.

"We... we choose the void," one of them finally said, his voice firm and resolute.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. Then let the training begin."

The game was no longer a competition. It was an initiation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the ceremony.
