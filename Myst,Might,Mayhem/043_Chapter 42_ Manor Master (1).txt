Chapter 42: Manor Master (1)

A man in navy blue martial arts robes, carrying a writing case on his back, paced back and forth not far from the entrance of Yeon Mok Sword Manor.

He was a scholar-warrior, a man who combined the pursuit of knowledge with the mastery of the blade.

His name was Han Gyeong-rok.

He was here to see the Manor Master.

But the atmosphere at the manor was... strange.

The guards were tense and suspicious, and the usual bustle of activity was gone.

"Stop right there!" one of the guards shouted as Han Gyeong-rok approached.

"I am here to see the Manor Master," Han Gyeong-rok said, his voice calm and steady.

"The Manor Master is not receiving visitors."

"I have an appointment. I was told to come today."

The guard hesitated. He knew Han Gyeong-rok's reputation.

"Wait here. I will check with the Inner Manor Master."

Han Gyeong-rok waited, his eyes taking in every detail of the manor.

The silence was eerie. It was as if the very air was thick with a hidden, dark energy.

Something was wrong. Very wrong.

And he intended to find out what it was.

The game was becoming a mystery. And Han Gyeong-rok was the one who was going to solve it.
