Chapter 239: Primordial True Qi (2)

"Mortal. I have been waiting for you."

The voice was like a chorus of a thousand voices, echoing through the ritual chamber of the Forbidden Temple.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently standing in front of a high altar, upon which sat a small, golden orb.

It was the Primordial True Qi, the source of all things.

"I am the Void Sun. and I have come to take the source," Mok Gyeong-un said, his golden-white eyes fixed on the orb.

A figure emerged from the altar. It was a mass of golden energy, its form constantly shifting and changing.

It was one of the original gods.

"Take the source? You think you're ready to handle the power of creation?" the god asked, its voice full of a divine amusement.

"I am the void. and creation is just a part of me."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the golden-white energy surged forward, clashing with the god's own radiance.

The temple was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the final confrontation.

The game was no longer a quest for self-discovery. It was a battle for the universe. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was taking the prize.
