Chapter 271: Underground Prison (3)

"This is properly done."

Mok Gyeong-un was currently examining the mechanisms of the lowest level of the Underground Prison.

The prison was not just a place of incarceration; it was a complex machine, designed to harness and focus the energy of the void.

"The seventh focus... it's not a single point. It's the entire level," he whispered to himself.

"And I am the one who is going to activate it."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, connecting with the prison's machinery.

The floor began to vibrate, and the air became thick with the power of the void.

"The activation is beginning, Master," the Shadow King said, appearing beside him.

"And soon, the final gateway will be open."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For the world is now ready for the final transformation."

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a deification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming a god.
