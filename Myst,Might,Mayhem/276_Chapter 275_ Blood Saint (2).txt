Chapter 275: Blood Saint (2)

Tap tap tap tap tap!

Mok Gyeong-un was currently practicing a series of acupuncture strikes on a training dummy, his movements a blur of motion.

He was using the power of the blood to enhance his precision and his impact, creating a series of "blood points" that could paralyze or kill with a single touch.

"The art of the Blood Saint... it's a useful addition to my arsenal," he said to himself, his transparent eyes scanning the dummy.

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The Blood Saint's techniques are... efficient, Great One. but they are also addictive. and if you're not careful, they will consume you."

"I am not like the Blood Saint, Monk. I am the Void Sun. and the blood is just a part of me."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint, crimson radiance that illuminated the training ground.

"Now, for the final test. I want to see what these 'blood points' can really do."

He called for one of his top agents.

"Show me your strength, agent. show me your will."

The game was becoming a demonstration of biological dominance. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the lesson.
