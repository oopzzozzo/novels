Chapter 187: Pinnacle (2)

Mok Gyeong-un followed Gyu Soha's instructions, entering the central ritual chamber of the Sun Tower.

It was a large, circular room, its walls covered in ancient, glowing runes.

In the center of the room sat a high pedestal, upon which rested the final focus.

"This is the Pinnacle, Master," Gyu Soha said, her voice full of awe.

"The place where the physical world and the void meet."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped onto the pedestal and closed his eyes.

He could feel the power of the void coursing through his meridians, and the absolute dominance of his presence filling the entire chamber.

"I am ready," he whispered to himself.

Suddenly, the runes on the walls began to glow with an intense, crimson light.

And Mok Gyeong-un was plunged into a dark, swirling abyss.

The Demonic Monk appeared in his mind.

"Great One! The transition is beginning! You must remain focused!"

Mok Gyeong-un didn't panic. He just synchronized his heartbeat with the pulse of the void.

And instead of being consumed, he began to expand.

The game was no longer an ascension. It was an expansion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming the void.
