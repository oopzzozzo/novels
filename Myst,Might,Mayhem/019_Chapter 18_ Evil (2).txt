Chapter 18: Evil (2)

"Wh-what the …. did you do to my body?"

In response to his question, Mok Gyeong-un smiled brightly and said,

"I didn't do anything special. I just gave you a little... upgrade."

The Demonic Monk felt a strange energy coursing through his spectral form.

It was dark, cold, and incredibly powerful.

"This... this is not my energy."

"No, it's mine. Or rather, a part of it."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes seemed to glow with a faint, ominous light.

"You wanted power, didn't you? Now you have it. But remember, it comes with a price."

The Demonic Monk felt a sudden, sharp pain in his chest.

"A price?"

"Your loyalty. You are mine now. My servant, my weapon."

The Monk tried to resist, but the dark energy within him tightened its grip.

"I... I understand."

"Good. Now, tell me about the Manor Master's secret. Why was he targeted?"

The Demonic Monk hesitated for a second.

"It's about... the ancient ritual. The Ritual of Attachment."

Mok Gyeong-un's smile widened.

"Tell me more."

The game was getting more interesting by the minute.
