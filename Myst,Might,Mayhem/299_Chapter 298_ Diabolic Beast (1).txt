Chapter 298: Diabolic Beast (1)

"It's Cheonma… Cheonma (Heavenly Demon)!"

The voice was full of absolute terror as it echoed through the City of the Void.

Mok Gyeong-un had finally arrived at the gates of the city, and he was met by a monstrous entity.

It was a diabolic beast, a creature of pure shadow and bone, with eyes that burned with a cold, blue fire.

It was the guardian of the city, and its name was Cheonma.

"I am the Void Sun. and I have come to reclaim my kingdom," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice echoing with the power of the void.

Cheonma laughed, a sound like grinding stone.

"Your kingdom? You are just a mortal who has found a power he doesn't understand! I am the true master of the void!"

The beast lunged forward, its massive claws tearing through the air.

But Mok Gyeong-un just raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, clashing with the beast's own power.

The battle for the City of the Void had begun. and the Winner would rule the universe.

The game was no longer an invasion. It was a deification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming a god.
