Chapter 168: Variables (4)

-You can't mean…?

"I mean exactly what I said, Han Gyeong-jin."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was mocking.

He was currently facing Gyeong-jin on the roof, his left arm glowing with a dark, intense light.

"I don't need your support. I don't need your help. I have the void. and that's all I need."

Gyeong-jin's eyes widened in surprise.

"But Master, the Imperial City is a dangerous place! You need the clan's resources!"

"I am the master of the void, Gyeong-jin. And I can create my own resources."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a shockwave that knocked Gyeong-jin back.

"Now, tell me. Where is the leader of the Silent Strides hiding? I have a special gift for him."

Gyeong-jin tried to resist, but the binding was absolute.

"He's... in the Imperial Palace. In the Garden of the Sun."

"Good. Then let's not keep him waiting."

Mok Gyeong-un walked away, the dark energy swirling around him.

The game was no longer a quest. It was an invasion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the primary invader.
