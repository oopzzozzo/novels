Chapter 156: Coercion (2)

"I shall face this 'Great Leader' myself."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was firm, and his eyes were filled with a cold, focused light.

He was currently at the base of the Sun Tower, preparing to enter the leader's private quarters.

Wi Maeng, the blind disciple, was standing beside him.

"Young Master... the leader is more than just a man. He is a force of nature. Are you sure you're ready?"

"I am the void, Wi Maeng. and nature is just a subset of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped into the tower, the dark energy of the void swirling around him.

The staircase was long and winding, and the air became colder and thinner with every step.

Finally, he reached the top.

The chamber was large and ornate, but it was also filled with a dark, suffocating pressure.

In the center of the room, on a high throne, sat the Society Leader.

His face was still hidden in shadow, but his presence was overwhelming.

"So, you've come to face me, boy," the leader said, his voice a chorus of a thousand voices.

"I have. and I've come to take what is mine."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the final battle for the Society began.

The game was no longer a confrontation. It was a climax. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was playing the final scene.
