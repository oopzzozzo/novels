Chapter 206: Karma (3)

During the time when the investigator Seop Chun was being eliminated, Mok Gyeong-un was already miles away, heading towards the Imperial City.

He was traveling by boat, moving down the river that connected the manor's territory to the capital.

The air on the river was cool and fresh, a welcome change from the suffocating pressure of the Sun Tower.

"The Imperial City... it's a place of many memories," the Demonic Monk said, his voice a mere whisper in the boy's mind.

"Memories of the previous master? Or memories of the Lee family?"

"Both. and neither. It's a place where the history of the Empire and the history of the void are intertwined."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the distant horizon, where the city walls were beginning to appear.

"Intertwined... like the red thread."

He raised his left arm, the ancient skin pulsing with a faint, dark light.

"The sixth focus is there. and I intend to find it."

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a pilgrimage. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the way.
