Chapter 39: Dangerous Visit (2)

"I need the head of the young master you serve."

'!?'

At the words of Ha Chae-rin, the Flying Killing Blossom, the expression of the guard Go Chan stiffened.

The woman before him was a renowned assassin, known for her beauty and her lethality.

And she was asking for the head of Mok Gyeong-un.

"What do you want with the Young Master?" Go Chan asked, his hand slowly moving towards his sword.

Ha Chae-rin smiled, a cold, predatory smile.

"That's none of your concern. Just tell me where he is."

Go Chan drew his sword.

"I can't do that. I am bound to serve him."

Ha Chae-rin laughed.

"Bound? You mean by that red thread? I can cut that easily enough."

She drew her own weapon, a pair of long, curved daggers.

"Now, stand aside. Or die."

The forest was silent as the two of them faced off.

The game was becoming a duel. And only one of them would walk away.
