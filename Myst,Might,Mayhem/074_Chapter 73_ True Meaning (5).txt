Chapter 73: True Meaning (5)

The monster wolf, no, the monstrous beast demon wolf, lying dead with its snout torn off.

It wasn't real. It was a projection of the void, a physical manifestation of Mok Gyeong-un's inner darkness.

And now, it was gone.

Mok Gyeong-un stood over the "corpse", his eyes glowing with a faint, blue fire.

"I understand now," he whispered.

"Understand what, Great One?" the Demonic Monk asked, his voice full of curiosity.

"The true meaning of the void. It's not just a place of darkness and destruction. It's a mirror. A mirror that reflects our own soul."

He looked at the Monk.

"I summoned that wolf because I was afraid. I was afraid of the power I possessed. But now... now I am the power."

The dark energy around Mok Gyeong-un shifted and changed, becoming more refined, more focused.

"The ritual is complete. The gateway is open. And I am the master of both worlds."

He walked towards the training courtyard, where the boys were waiting.

"Now, let the real training begin. I'll show you how to become more than just humans. I'll show you how to become gods."

The game was no longer an initiation. It was an evolution. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the way.
