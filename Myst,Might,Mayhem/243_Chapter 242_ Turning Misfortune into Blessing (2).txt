Chapter 242: Turning Misfortune into Blessing (2)

Mok Gyeong-un sat on his celestial throne, his golden-white eyes scanning the newly reshaped universe.

The old conflicts and rivalries were gone, replaced by a sense of order and purpose.

But he also knew that his work was not yet finished.

"The seeds of darkness are still there, Master," the Demonic Monk said, his voice full of hidden warning.

"The ones who were consumed by the void... they are not truly gone. They are waiting for their chance to return."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Let them wait. For I am the Void Sun. and I will always be there to meet them."

He raised his left arm, and the golden-white energy surged forward, creating a system of celestial guards who would protect the new world.

"The transformation is complete. and the universe is at peace."

The game was over for the old world. but for the new one, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un was now the eternal ruler of the cosmos.

And he had finally found the answer to the ultimate question.

The question of his own existence.

He was the balance. The point where the void and the light meet.

The game was finished. And Mok Gyeong-un had won.
