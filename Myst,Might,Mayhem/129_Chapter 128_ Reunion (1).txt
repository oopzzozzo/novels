Chapter 128: Reunion (1)

It was truly a bizarre coincidence.

Among the junior disciples, the senior disciple Hwan Yun-myeong, who had been defeated by Mok Gyeong-un, was actually the long-lost brother of one of the current candidates.

They had been separated as children during a purge of their clan, and neither knew the other was still alive.

Until now.

They were currently in the training courtyard, staring at each other with a mixture of disbelief and joy.

"Yun-myeong... is it really you?" the candidate asked, his voice trembling.

"Yes. It's me," Yun-myeong replied, his eyes filling with tears.

Mok Gyeong-un watched from the sidelines, his lifeless eyes neutral.

"A reunion... how touching," he said, his voice flat.

He stepped forward, his left arm glowing with a faint, dark light.

"But remember, your reunion is a result of my grace. And you are both still bound to serve me."

Yun-myeong and his brother bowed their heads.

"We understand, Master. We are yours."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of work for you to do."

The game was becoming a soap opera. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was writing the script.
