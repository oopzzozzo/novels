Chapter 19: Evil (3)

[The fifth level, Blue ghost… extremely dangerous. At least ten or more diviners are required for exorcism...]

Mok Gyeong-un was reading a worn-out book on spiritual entities.

The Demonic Monk, now a spectral presence bound to the boy, watched from the shadows.

"The fifth level... Is that what you are?"

The Monk's form flickered.

"I was... once. But your power has... changed me."

Mok Gyeong-un turned a page.

"Interesting. So, there are even more powerful entities out there."

"Yes. But they are rare. And they usually stay in the deep, dark places of the world."

"Like the one behind the Ritual of Attachment?"

The Monk's form trembled at the mention of the ritual.

"That... that is not a mere entity. It is something much older. Much more... fundamental."

Mok Gyeong-un closed the book.

"Older and fundamental... I like the sound of that."

He looked at the Monk.

"Now, tell me about the seal. Where is it?"

"It's... in the Manor Master's secret chamber. But only he knows the way."

"Then I'll just have to ask him. Or rather, I'll have to make him show me."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up.

"Let's go. We have a lot to do."

The game was becoming a grand adventure. And Mok Gyeong-un was ready for it.
