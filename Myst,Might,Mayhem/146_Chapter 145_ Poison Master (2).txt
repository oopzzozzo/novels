Chapter 145: Poison Master (2)

"Deadly? This?"

Mu Jang-yak was staring at the small, green vial Mok Gyeong-un had just handed him.

It was the Annihilating Poison, the masterpiece of the Poison King.

"Yes. A single drop is enough to kill a master of the Transformation Realm," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice neutral.

Mu Jang-yak's eyes widened.

"And you're giving it to me?"

"I'm giving it to the Society. To be used as a last resort. Or a first strike."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped closer to the administrative head.

"The purge is almost complete. and soon, we will be ready to face the leader."

Mu Jang-yak felt a wave of relief and anxiety.

Relief that the internal conflict was coming to an end. and anxiety about what would happen once Mok Gyeong-un was in charge.

"Now, tell me. Where is the leader's private quarters? I have a delivery to make."

Mu Jang-yak pointed towards the inner castle's highest tower.

"In the Sun Tower. But it's heavily guarded."

"Not for long."

Mok Gyeong-un walked away, the dark, toxic energy swirling around him.

The game was becoming a countdown to the final confrontation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the timer.
