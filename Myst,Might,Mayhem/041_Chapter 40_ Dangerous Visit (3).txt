Chapter 40: Dangerous Visit (3)

Despite her fiery temperament, Ha Chae-rin had undergone rigorous training and education under Flying Blossom.

She was a professional, and she knew that emotions could be a liability in her line of work.

But there was something about this guard, Go Chan, that infuriated her.

"You're standing in the way of a job, guard. That's a very dangerous thing to do."

Go Chan didn't flinch.

"I am doing my duty. And my duty is to protect the Young Master."

Ha Chae-rin lunged forward, her daggers moving like twin strikes of lightning.

Go Chan met her attack with his sword, the sound of metal on metal echoing through the forest.

They moved in a deadly dance, each seeking an opening, each countering the other's moves.

Ha Chae-rin was faster, but Go Chan had the strength and the determination of a man who had nothing left to lose.

"You're good," she admitted, her breath coming in short gasps. "But you're not good enough."

She threw a small, black sphere at his feet.

-Bang!

A cloud of thick, white smoke erupted, obscuring Go Chan's vision.

"Damn it!" he cursed, swinging his sword blindly.

But Ha Chae-rin was already gone.

She had used the distraction to slip away, her eyes fixed on the manor in the distance.

The game was becoming a hunt. And Ha Chae-rin was closing in on her prey.
