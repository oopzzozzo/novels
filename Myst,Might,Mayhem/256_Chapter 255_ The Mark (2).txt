Chapter 255: The Mark (2)

Yesterday,

And early this morning, Mok Gyeong-un had been observing the new mark on his arm.

It was more than just a symbol; it was a living entity, pulsing with a dark energy that felt both familiar and alien.

"The Mark of the Void... it's changing," he whispered.

The Demonic Monk, now a spectral advisor, watched with a mixture of fear and fascination.

"It's adapting to your body, Master. It's becoming a part of your very soul."

Mok Gyeong-un felt a surge of power, a cold, sharp energy that seemed to cut through his very being.

"And what does this 'adaptation' mean for me?"

"It means you are no longer just a master of the void. You are becoming its manifestation."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, a smile that didn't reach his lifeless eyes.

"Manifestation... I like the sound of that."

He stood up and looked out at the city, his presence now a silent, overwhelming force that seemed to darken the very sun.

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a transformation of the world itself. And Mok Gyeong-un was the catalyst.
