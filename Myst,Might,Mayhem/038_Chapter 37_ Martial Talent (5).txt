Chapter 37: Martial Talent (5)

Was this fellow really the Mok Gyeong-un he knew?

Glaring at Mok Gyeong-un with anger-filled eyes, Mok Yu-cheon felt a wave of confusion.

The boy before him was the same one he had seen in the training courtyard, but his presence was entirely different.

It was... overwhelming.

"Who are you?" Yu-cheon demanded, his voice trembling slightly.

Mok Gyeong-un tilted his head, his eyes as still as a frozen lake.

"I am Mok Gyeong-un. The master of this manor."

"Liar! You're a substitute! A mere prisoner brought in to play a part!"

Yu-cheon drew his sword, the steel glinting in the morning sun.

"I'll show you what a true Mok family member can do!"

He lunged forward, his sword moving in a blur of motion.

It was a perfect strike, one that should have ended the confrontation instantly.

But Mok Gyeong-un didn't even move.

He just raised his left hand.

And caught the blade between his fingers.

Yu-cheon's eyes widened in disbelief.

"Impossible..."

"Nothing is impossible when you have the void on your side," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice cold and flat.

With a flick of his wrist, he snapped the blade in two.

Yu-cheon fell back, his face a mask of terror.

The game was becoming a slaughter. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the knife.
