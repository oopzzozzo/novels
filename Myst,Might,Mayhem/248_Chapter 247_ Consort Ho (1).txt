Chapter 247: Consort Ho (1)

"Won't you let me in, Gyeongjin?"

Consort Ho's voice was sultry and persuasive as she stood outside Prince Gyeongjin's library.

She was dressed in a revealing gown, and her eyes were filled with a seductive light.

Prince Gyeongjin didn't look up from his book.

"I'm busy, Consort. and I have no interest in your games."

Consort Ho laughed, a soft and musical sound.

"Games? I'm not playing games, Gyeongjin. I'm offering you a partnership."

She entered the library without waiting for an answer and sat down across from the Prince.

"The Shadow Alliance... they're powerful, Gyeongjin. but they're also dangerous. If you work with them, you can achieve anything you desire."

"And what do I desire, Consort?"

"Power. The same power that your ancestor possessed. The power of the Void Sun."

Prince Gyeongjin finally looked up, his eyes glowing with a faint, white radiance.

"I have no need for your partnership, Consort. and I have no need for your power."

"Then what do you need, Gyeongjin?"

"I need the truth. and I intend to find it."

The game was becoming a seduction. And Prince Gyeongjin was the one who was resisting the temptation.
