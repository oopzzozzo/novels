Chapter 186: Pinnacle (1)

"Heheh."

Gyu Soha, who was now the overseer of the Palace's secret records, let out a small, satisfied laugh.

She was currently reviewing the information regarding the final stage of the ritual.

"The pinnacle... the point where the user becomes one with the void," she whispered to herself.

"It's finally within our reach."

She was interrupted by the arrival of Mok Gyeong-un.

The boy was now the master of the Empire, and his presence was more overwhelming than ever.

"So, you've found it, Soha," he said, his voice a mere echo of its former self.

"I have, Master. The instructions for reaching the pinnacle."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I am ready to ascend."

He looked at the distant spires of the city, his eyes black and lifeless.

"The world of men is a shadow. and I am the one who is going to step into the light."

The game was no longer a conquest. It was an ascension. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was climbing.
