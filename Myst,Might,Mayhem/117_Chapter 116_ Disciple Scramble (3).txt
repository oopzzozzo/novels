Chapter 116: Disciple Scramble (3)

How could he smile and say it was fine to kill his younger brother or turn him into a cripple?

It was a question that was on the minds of everyone in the training courtyard.

Mok Gyeong-un had just given the command for the final test, a fight to the death between the remaining candidates.

"Master... you're not serious, are you?" one of the candidates asked, his voice trembling.

"I am perfectly serious. Only the strongest can lead. and only the strongest can survive the void."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the candidates, his lifeless eyes neutral.

"Now, begin."

The courtyard was silent for a second, and then the fighting erupted.

It was a brutal and bloody affair, as the candidates fought each other with everything they had.

Mok Gyeong-un watched, his expression unfazed.

"He's a monster," the Shadow King whispered from the sidelines.

"No. He's a visionary," Cheong-ryeong replied, her voice full of hidden respect.

The game was becoming a cull. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was selecting the survivors.
