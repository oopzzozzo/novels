Chapter 130: Ability (1)

The second disciple of the Society leader, a man named Kang Jin-ho, was currently training in the inner castle's arena.

He was a master of the Iron Fist Style, a technique that emphasized raw strength and durability.

But he was also a man who was constantly seeking to improve his abilities.

"I need to become faster. I need to become more precise," he muttered to himself as he struck a training dummy.

Suddenly, a figure appeared in the arena. It was Mok Gyeong-un.

"You're training hard, Kang Jin-ho," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice flat and neutral.

Kang Jin-ho stopped his training and looked at the boy.

"Young Master Mok. What brings you to my arena?"

"I'm here to see your ability. I've heard that you're one of the strongest warriors in the Society."

Kang Jin-ho smiled, a confident and competitive smile.

"I am. And I'm ready to prove it. If you're looking for a sparring partner, I'm more than happy to oblige."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled back.

"Good. Then let's see what your Iron Fist is really capable of."

The game was becoming a series of tests. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the challenges.
