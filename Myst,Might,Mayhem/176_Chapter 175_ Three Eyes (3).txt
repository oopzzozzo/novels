Chapter 175: Three Eyes (3)

"Yin and Zhou? Could they be related to the void gods?"

Mok Gyeong-un was currently sitting in the Temple of the Moon, reviewing the contents of the ancient scroll.

The scroll mentioned two ancient dynasties, the Yin and the Zhou, and their mastery of spiritual and metaphysical studies.

"The Yin were the first to discover the void," the Demonic Monk said, his voice full of hidden knowledge.

"But they were also the first to be consumed by it. The Zhou were the ones who managed to seal the gateway, at a high cost."

Mok Gyeong-un turned a page.

"So the Three Eyes are the descendants of the Zhou. The ones who were entrusted with protecting the seal."

"It seems so, Great One. and they have been doing it for centuries."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Centuries of protection... it's a long time to wait for someone to come and break the seal."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"And I am the one who is going to break it."

The game was becoming a historical epic. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was rewriting the end.
