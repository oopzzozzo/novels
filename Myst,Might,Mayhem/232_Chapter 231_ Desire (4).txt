Chapter 231: Desire (4)

On the way back to the Sun Tower, Mok Gyeong-un felt a sense of completion.

The ritual was proceeding perfectly, and he had the support of the most powerful people in the Empire.

But he also knew that there was one final variable he had to account for.

The Embroidered Uniform Guard. The elite unit of the Empire's secret police.

"They're watching, Master," the Shadow King said, his voice full of concern.

"They've already identified several of our movements. and they're targeting our infiltration team."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Let them watch. The Embroidered Uniform Guard is just another shadow in the presence of the Void Sun."

He looked at the distant spires of the Palace, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

"The final stage is close. and no one, not even the secret police, will stop me."

The game was becoming a series of confrontations. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the challenges.
