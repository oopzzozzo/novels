Chapter 256: The Mark (3)

"I was going to wait for a moment, but there was no need to wait. Thousand-men Commander Ma Ra-hyeon..."

Mok Gyeong-un was currently standing in the center of the arena, facing a man who was known for his mastery of the heavy blade.

Ma Ra-hyeon, a Thousand-men Commander of the Society, was staring at the boy with a mixture of disbelief and fury.

"You... you've already defeated the others. and now you're coming for me?"

"I'm not coming for you, Commander. I'm just... finishing the test."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint, crimson glow that illuminated the entire arena.

"The Mark of the Void... it's already working. and soon, you will be a part of it."

Ma Ra-hyeon lunged forward, his heavy blade moving in a blur of motion.

But Mok Gyeong-un didn't even move. He just raised his left hand and caught the blade between his fingers.

The dark energy from his arm instantly shattered the steel, and the Commander fell back in shock.

"Your blade is a tool. and like any tool, it can be broken," Mok Gyeong-un said, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

"But your will... your will is what truly matters. And your will is weak."

He raised his hand, and the red thread surged forward, wrapping around Ma Ra-hyeon's neck.

The game was becoming a completion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was finding the last piece.
