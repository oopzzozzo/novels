Chapter 124: Shadow Clan (1)

In a wide room lit by a single candle.

There was a bed in the room, surrounded by bamboo blinds.

A shadow moved behind the blinds, its movements slow and deliberate.

This was the residence of the Shadow King, the leader of the Society's most secretive unit.

He was currently meeting with one of his top agents.

"What is the report?" the Shadow King asked, his voice a mere rasp.

"Mok Gyeong-un has mastered the Evil Commandment Sword, Master. and he has freed the former master from his cell."

The shadow behind the blinds stopped moving.

"Freed him? Why would he do that?"

"It seems he is seeking knowledge, Master. Knowledge that only the former master possesses."

The Shadow King let out a small, thoughtful hum.

"Mok Gyeong-un... he is becoming more dangerous than I ever imagined. We must find a way to neutralize him."

"And how do we do that, Master? He is a master of the void."

"The void has its own weaknesses. and I intend to find them."

The candle flickered and went out, leaving the room in darkness.

The game was becoming a war of shadows. And the Shadow King was the one who was leading the assault.
