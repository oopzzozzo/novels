Chapter 63: Gal-jeo (1)

A smile that reached his ears.

Sometimes, someone's smiling face can lower the other person's guard.

But in Mok Gyeong-un's case, it only made people more uneasy.

He was currently in the village of Gal-jeo, a small, remote settlement at the edge of the Mok family's territory.

He was here to find a specific person.

"Where is he?" he asked a group of villagers who were staring at him with a mixture of curiosity and fear.

"We... we don't know who you're talking about, Young Master," one of them managed to stammer.

Mok Gyeong-un's smile didn't fade.

"I'm looking for the one they call the Gal-jeo Butcher. The one who's been causing trouble for the Society."

The villagers' faces went pale.

"He... he's in the old tannery at the edge of the village. But please, Young Master, don't go there. He's a monster."

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"A monster? I like the sound of that."

He stepped towards the tannery, his left arm glowing with a faint, dark light.

The game was becoming a confrontation. And Mok Gyeong-un was ready to meet the Butcher.
