Chapter 270: Underground Prison (2)

-Creak!

The massive iron door of the lowest level of the Underground Prison opened, revealing a dark and damp chamber.

Mok Gyeong-un entered the room, his eyes adjusting to the dim light.

In the center of the chamber, a man was chained to the wall.

He was a master of the void, a man who had been captured by the Society years ago and held here ever since.

His name was Han Gyeong-jin.

"So, you've finally come," Gyeong-jin said, his voice a mere rasp.

"I have. and I've come to take the seventh focus."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, shattering the chains like they were paper.

"I am the Void Sun. and I am the one who is providing the liberation."

Gyeong-jin looked at the boy with his sharp, intelligent eyes.

"The seventh focus... it's not a focus, Gyeong-un. It's a choice. A choice between the world of men and the world of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"I've already made my choice, Gyeong-jin. and I've chosen both."

The game was becoming a completion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was finding the last piece.
