Chapter 163: Proposition (3)

'This is?'

For a moment, Wi Maeng felt a wave of recognition as he sensed the technique Mok Gyeong-un was using on his brother.

It was the Spirit God Parasitic Poisoning Technique, but it had been modified by the power of the void.

"Master... you're not just binding him. You're infecting him," Wi Maeng whispered, his voice full of hidden awe.

"I'm giving him a purpose, Maeng. A purpose that is far greater than his own petty loyalty."

Mok Gyeong-un stood over the now-silent So-yeon, his eyes black and lifeless.

"He will be the general of my shadow army. The one who will lead the conquest of the mortal world."

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The parasite is taking hold, Great One. and soon, Wi So-yeon will be yours."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. Now, for the final proposition."

He turned to Wi Maeng.

"Tell the Shadow Clan Master to prepare for the invasion. We're going to the Imperial City."

The game was no longer a liquidation. It was a mobilization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the march.
