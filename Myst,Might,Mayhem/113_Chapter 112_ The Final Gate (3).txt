Chapter 112: The Final Gate (3)

As Yeom Ga from the Vermillion Slaughter Cave drew 五 (five), Mok Gyeong-un naturally became 二 (two).

The numbers represented the order in which they would face the leader's challenge.

Mok Gyeong-un was second.

"Good luck, Young Master," Yeom Ga said, his voice full of hidden malice.

He was hoping that the leader would finish off the boy before it was his turn.

Mok Gyeong-un stepped into the center of the chamber, his eyes fixed on the shadow on the throne.

"The first challenge is simple," the leader said. "You must survive the void."

Suddenly, the floor beneath Mok Gyeong-un's feet disappeared, and he was plunged into a dark, swirling abyss.

The Demonic Monk cried out in his mind.

"Great One! The void is consuming us!"

Mok Gyeong-un didn't panic. He just closed his eyes and synchronized his heartbeat with the pulse of the void.

And instead of being consumed, he began to absorb the energy.

He was becoming the void itself.

The game was no longer a battle. It was a merging. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming whole.
