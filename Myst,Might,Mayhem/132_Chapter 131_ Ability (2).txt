Chapter 131: Ability (2)

'Will this be alright?'

Mu Jang-yak, who was observing the spar between Mok Gyeong-un and Kang Jin-ho, felt a wave of concern.

Kang Jin-ho was a formidable opponent, and his Iron Fist was no joke.

But Mok Gyeong-un was... well, he was Mok Gyeong-un.

The two of them were currently exchanging blows in the center of the arena, the sound of their impact echoing through the castle.

Kang Jin-ho was using his raw strength to overwhelm the boy, but Mok Gyeong-un was using the fluidity of the void to parry and redirect the attacks.

"You're fast, Young Master. But you can't run forever!" Kang Jin-ho shouted, launching a powerful punch at Mok Gyeong-un's chest.

Mok Gyeong-un didn't run. He just stood his ground and raised his left arm.

-Thud!

The punch landed squarely on the ancient skin, but instead of shattering the boy's ribs, it was Kang Jin-ho's hand that felt the impact.

"Wh-what? My fist... it's like hitting a stone wall!"

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"The void is not just a place of darkness, Jin-ho. It's a place of absolute durability."

He raised his right hand and struck Kang Jin-ho's shoulder.

The game was becoming a demonstration of invulnerability. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was untouchable.
