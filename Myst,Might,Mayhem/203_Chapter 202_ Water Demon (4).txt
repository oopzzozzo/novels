Chapter 202: Water Demon (4)

"How does it feel, Demon? To be a part of the void?"

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was calm, but it carried an underlying power that shook the pool.

He had just finished integrating the Water Demon's essence, and he could feel its power coursing through his meridians.

The grotto was silent, save for the sound of the waterfall.

The demon was gone, consumed by its master.

"The fourth focus is mine," Mok Gyeong-un whispered, his eyes transparent and clear.

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The fourth focus... you've found it, Great One. Now, the ritual can proceed to the final stage."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Yes. It's exactly what I needed."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint, blue glow that illuminated the entire grotto.

"The gateway is open. and the void is waiting."

The game was no longer a maritime adventure. It was a mobilization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the march.
