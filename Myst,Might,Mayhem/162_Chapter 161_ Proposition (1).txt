Chapter 161: Proposition (1)

'S-Senior brother…'

Wi So-yeon felt a wave of cold dread as he saw Wi Maeng standing beside Mok Gyeong-un.

He had always looked up to his senior brother, but now he saw him as a traitor.

"Maeng... how could you do this? How could you serve this... monster?" So-yeon asked, his voice trembling with anger.

"He's not a monster, So-yeon. He is the master of the void. and he is the only one who can save us," Wi Maeng replied, his blind eyes fixed on his younger brother.

"Save us? He's destroying the Society!"

"The Society was already dead, So-yeon. It was just waiting for someone to bury it."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Now, So-yeon. I have a proposition for you."

So-yeon drew his sword.

"I'm not interested in your propositions, Gyeong-un. I'm interested in your head."

"A predictable response. but also a foolish one."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

The game was becoming a fratricide. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the weapon.
