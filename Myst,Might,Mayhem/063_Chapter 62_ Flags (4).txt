Chapter 62: Flags (4)

A dead boy's head was grasped by Mok Gyeong-un as he performed the Ritual of Binding.

Not long after, the boy's eyes snapped open, glowing with a cold, blue fire.

He was no longer just a corpse. He was a Guyeo.

"Serve me," Mok Gyeong-un commanded.

The Guyeo bowed his head.

"I... serve... Master."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. Now, go and find the others. Bring them to me."

The Guyeo disappeared into the shadows of the manor.

Mok Gyeong-un watched him go, his eyes glowing with an intense, crimson light.

He was building an army. An army of ghosts that would follow his every command.

The game was no longer a political struggle. It was a supernatural conquest. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the charge.
