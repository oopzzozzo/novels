Chapter 99: Treasure Vault (3)

Moonless Void Sword – Moon Fragrance Defeating Sword.

The moment he imprinted this technique, Mok Gyeong-un felt a surge of power that was unlike anything he had ever experienced.

It was a technique that combined the precision of the Yeon Mok Sword Style with the raw power of the void.

And it was his.

He stood in the center of the Treasure Vault, his entire body glowing with a blinding, crimson light.

"It's... it's done," he whispered, his voice echoing through the chamber.

The Demonic Monk, now a mere shadow, watched in silent awe.

"The Moonless Void Sword... the ultimate art of the void. You've mastered it."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Now, let's see what this technique can really do."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a shockwave that rattled the bookshelves.

"The Society will never be the same again."

The game was no longer an immersion. It was an awakening. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was waking up.
