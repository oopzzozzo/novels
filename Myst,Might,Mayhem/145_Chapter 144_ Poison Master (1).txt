Chapter 144: Poison Master (1)

"If anyone has something to say, speak now."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was flat and neutral, but it carried an underlying threat that made everyone in the meeting hall flinch.

He was currently facing the high-ranking members of the Society, who were staring at him with a mixture of shock and terror.

The news of his "partnership" with the Poison King had spread quickly, and everyone was wondering what the boy was planning next.

"Master... the Poison King is a dangerous man. You shouldn't have made a deal with him," one of the members managed to say, his voice trembling.

"I am not making deals, member. I am making progress. And the Poison King is a valuable asset in that progress."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up, his eyes black and lifeless.

"Now, tell me. Who else is working for the Society Leader? I want names. and I want them now."

The meeting hall was silent for a moment. No one wanted to be the first to speak.

But then, one by one, the members began to point fingers.

"He is! And she is!"

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of work for you to do."

The game was becoming a purge. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the broom.
