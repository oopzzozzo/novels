Chapter 176: Three Eyes (4)

'What was that just now?'

Mok Gyeong-un felt a sudden, sharp pain in his head as he finished reading the scroll.

It was a psychic attack, a mental strike designed to shatter his focus and disconnect him from the void.

"They're here," he whispered, his eyes black and lifeless.

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The Three Eyes... they've found us, Great One."

Suddenly, three figures emerged from the temple's shadows.

They were dressed in white robes, and each one had a third eye tattooed on their forehead.

They were the elite unit of the Imperial City's diviners.

"Evil master! Return the scroll and surrender, or face your judgment!" the leader of the Three Eyes commanded.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Judgment? I am the judge. and I've already decided your sentence."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, clashing with the golden light of the Three Eyes' counter-attack.

The temple was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the final confrontation.

The game was no longer a discovery. It was an extermination. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was surviving it.
