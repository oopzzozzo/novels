Chapter 236: The Disregard for the Embroided Uniform Guard (5)

The news of Zhao Gao's death spread through the Imperial City like wildfire.

The citizens were in a state of shock and terror, and the remaining members of the Embroidered Uniform Guard were in a state of total chaos.

"The leader is dead! The tower is gone!" they shouted, their voices full of desperation.

Mok Gyeong-un watched from the balcony of the Sun Tower, his eyes glowing with a cold, golden light.

The city was now his. and every variable had been accounted for.

"The mobilization is complete, Master," the Shadow King said, appearing beside him.

"The other sects and clans... they've all surrendered. and the citizens are bowing their heads."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For the world is now ready for the final transformation."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a blinding light that illuminated the entire city.

"The Void Sun has risen. and the shadow of the Empire is no more."

The game was over for the mortal world. but for the void gods, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un was now the undisputed ruler of the universe.
