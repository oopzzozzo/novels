Chapter 52: Proposal (1)

"How?"

Surprisingly, Mok Gyeong-un was moving perfectly fine, and on top of that, he had even untied the bindings on Go Chan.

The guard was staring at his Young Master with a mixture of disbelief and gratitude.

"I told you, Go Chan. No one touches my servant," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice calm and steady.

He turned to the Bright Blade King, who was waiting for his orders.

"Now, Bright Blade King. I have a proposal for you."

Son Yun bowed.

"I am listening, Great One."

"I want the Heaven & Earth Society to become my personal army. My enforcers in the mortal world."

Son Yun's eyes widened.

"Your personal army? But the Society has its own goals, its own leadership..."

"Not anymore. Now, it has me."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped closer to the King.

"I will give you the power you seek. I will lead you to the ultimate truth. But in exchange, you will give me your absolute loyalty."

Son Yun hesitated for a second, but then he nodded.

"I accept. The Heaven & Earth Society is yours."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. Now, let's begin the conquest."

The game was becoming an empire. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was building it.
