Chapter 74: Team Leader (1)

'Could it be?'

Yeom Ga of the Vermillion Slaughter Cave frowned, feeling uneasy inside.

The reaction to Mok Gyeong-un's announcement had been mixed.

Some were excited by the prospect of power, while others were terrified by the boy's intensity.

"Young Master. We need a leader for this unit," Yeom Ga said, his voice firm.

Mok Gyeong-un looked at him, his lifeless eyes neutral.

"And who do you suggest?"

"I... I would like to offer my services. I have the experience and the skill to lead these boys."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Experience and skill... those are important qualities. but they are not enough."

He stepped closer to Yeom Ga.

"I need a leader who can command the void. A leader who is not afraid to lose their humanity."

Yeom Ga hesitated.

"And you think I am that leader?"

"We'll see. For now, you will be the temporary team leader. But remember, your position is not secure. You must prove yourself to me. And to the void."

Yeom Ga bowed his head.

"I understand, Master. I will not fail you."

The game was no longer a training session. It was an organization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was building the hierarchy.
