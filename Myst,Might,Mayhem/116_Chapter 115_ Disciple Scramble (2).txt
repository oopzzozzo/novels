Chapter 115: Disciple Scramble (2)

At this distance, when infusing internal energy into sound, it was difficult to discern who had spoken.

But Mok Gyeong-un didn't need to hear the voice to know who was speaking.

He could feel the presence, the unique vibration of their soul.

"It's you, isn't it? The one they call the Shadow King," he said, his voice flat and cold.

A man in dark, form-fitting robes emerged from the shadows of the courtyard.

He was the leader of the Shadow Clan, a secret unit of the Society that specialized in espionage and assassination.

"You have a good ear, Young Master," the Shadow King replied, his voice a mere whisper.

"And you have a lot of nerve, coming here after what I did to the others."

The Shadow King smiled.

"I am not like the others, Young Master. I am not interested in power or resources. I am interested in... you."

Mok Gyeong-un tilted his head.

"Me? And why is that?"

"Because you are a anomaly. A master of the void who has no past. And I want to know where you came from."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes glinted with dark energy.

"I came from the void. and that's all you need to know."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

The game was becoming a pursuit of truth. And the Shadow King was the one who was chasing it.
