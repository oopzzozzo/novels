Chapter 132: Ability (3)

As they all withdrew, Mok Gyeong-un stood alone in the center of the arena.

The spar with Kang Jin-ho was over, and the second disciple had been thoroughly defeated.

But Mok Gyeong-un wasn't satisfied. He knew that there was still much more to learn about his own abilities.

"The durability of the void... it's just the beginning," he whispered to himself.

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"You've shown them a glimpse of your power, Great One. But you must be careful not to reveal too much."

"I reveal only what is necessary, Monk. And right now, it's necessary for the Society to know who is in charge."

Mok Gyeong-un walked to the edge of the arena and looked out at the inner castle.

"The ritual is almost complete. The focus is prepared. and the army is ready."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint, crimson glow that illuminated the entire arena.

"Soon, the world will know the name of Mok Gyeong-un."

The game was no longer a series of tests. It was a declaration of war. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was declaring it.
