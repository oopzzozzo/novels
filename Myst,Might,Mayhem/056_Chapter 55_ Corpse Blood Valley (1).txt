Chapter 55: Corpse Blood Valley (1)

In a dark forest.

A woman with an enchanting appearance, wearing a bamboo hat, was hanging from the trees.

She was an assassin of the Corpse Blood Valley, a group known for their ruthlessness and their mastery of poison.

Her name was Cheon Se-ah.

She was here to eliminate a target.

"Where is he?" she whispered to herself, her eyes scanning the forest floor.

Suddenly, a figure appeared from the shadows.

It was a man in navy blue martial arts robes, carrying a writing case on his back.

Han Gyeong-rok.

Cheon Se-ah smiled.

"Found you."

She lunged forward, her long, sharp nails coated in a deadly toxin.

Han Gyeong-rok drew his sword, the steel glinting in the moonlight.

"Who are you? And why are you attacking me?"

"I am Cheon Se-ah. And you are my target."

The two of them clashed, the sound of steel on steel echoing through the forest.

Cheon Se-ah was fast and lethal, but Han Gyeong-rok was a master of strategy.

He knew he couldn't let her touch him.

"You're good," she admitted, her nails missing him by inches. "But you're not good enough."

She threw a small, black sphere at his feet.

-Bang!

A cloud of thick, green smoke erupted, obscuring Han Gyeong-rok's vision.

"Damn it!" he cursed, swinging his sword blindly.

But Cheon Se-ah was already gone.

She had used the distraction to slip away, her eyes fixed on the manor in the distance.

The game was becoming a war between assassins and scholars. And the stakes were higher than ever.
