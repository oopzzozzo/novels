Chapter 245: Prince Gyeongjin (2)

Despite his royal status, Prince Gyeongjin was not a man who enjoyed the comforts of the Palace.

He preferred the quiet of his library and the focus of his studies.

But he also knew that he had a duty to his lineage.

"The Void Sun... it must be protected," he muttered to himself as he reviewed the records of the recent disturbances in the city.

"There are those who wish to use its power for their own ends. and I must find a way to stop them."

He called for his top advisor, a man named Han Gyeong-rok.

"Find out everything you can about the group that calls themselves the Shadow Alliance. I want to know their leader, their goals, and their connection to the void."

"Yes, Master."

The advisor disappeared, leaving the Prince to his thoughts.

The game was becoming a chess match. And Prince Gyeongjin was the one who was making the moves.
