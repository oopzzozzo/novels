Chapter 262: Clue (2)

Medicine Immortal Hae Yeong.

He was a man of great wisdom and knowledge, known for his absolute mastery of the healing arts.

But he was also a man with a secret. He was a guardian of the final gateway.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently standing in front of his retreat, a small, hidden village in the Northern Mountains.

"So, you've come for the gateway, boy," Hae Yeong said, his voice calm and steady.

"I have. and I've come to take the key."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint, crimson glow that illuminated the village.

"I am the Void Sun. and the gateway is mine to command."

Hae Yeong smiled, a wise and knowing smile.

"The gateway is not a prize, boy. It is a responsibility. and I'm not sure if you're ready to bear it."

"I don't need your approval, Immortal. I only need the key."

He stepped closer to the old man.

"Now, tell me. Where is it?"

The game was becoming a trial of character. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was being tested.
