Chapter 274: Blood Saint (1)

[The foundation of illusion techniques lies in the power of the blood.]

Mok Gyeong-un was currently reading a manuscript on the history of the Blood Saint, a legendary figure who was said to have mastered the art of biological manipulation.

The Blood Saint was a master of the void, and his techniques were rumored to be the basis for the Society's most deadly toxins and parasites.

"The blood... it's the ultimate conductor of energy," Mok Gyeong-un said to himself, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

The Demonic Monk, now a mere whisper, watched with interest.

"The Blood Saint was a genius, Great One. but he was also a monster. He used his power to create an army of biological weapons."

"And what happened to him?"

"He was betrayed by his own creations. and his soul was consumed by the void."

Mok Gyeong-un turned a page.

"Interesting. So his techniques are still out there."

"They are, Master. but they're hidden in the Hall of Eternal Fire. guarded by the Holy Fire Priestess."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"The Holy Fire Priestess... it seems I have another appointment to keep."

The game was becoming a quest for biological mastery. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was seeking the knowledge.
