Chapter 304: Domineering Steps (2)

The Precepts Hall Master, a man named Han Gyeong-jin, was currently facing Mok Gyeong-un in the Palace's main hall.

He was one of the few high-ranking officials who had resisted the boy's takeover, and he was prepared to die for his principles.

"You're not a god, boy. You're just a monster with too much power!" he shouted, his voice echoing through the hall.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, his golden-white eyes fixed on the Master.

"And what is the difference, Gyeong-jin? Power is the only true god in this universe. and I have more than anyone else."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a shockwave that knocked the Master back.

"Your principles are a shadow, Gyeong-jin. and shadows are meant to be erased."

The Master tried to resist, but his own power was dwarfed by the boy's.

One by one, the officials who had stood with him began to bow their heads.

The game was no longer a rule. It was a domination. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the force.
