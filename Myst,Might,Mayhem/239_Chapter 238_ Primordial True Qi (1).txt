Chapter 238: Primordial True Qi (1)

It was a power that was even older than the void.

The Primordial True Qi.

It was the energy that had existed before the creation of the universe, the source of all things.

And Mok Gyeong-un was currently reading about it in an ancient manuscript he had found in the Forbidden Temple.

"The Primordial True Qi... it's the key to everything," he whispered to himself, his eyes glowing with a faint, white radiance.

The Demonic Monk, now a mere shadow of a shadow, appeared from the darkness of the temple.

"The Primordial True Qi... it's a dangerous topic, Great One. and it's rumored that only the original gods can handle it."

"I am the Void Sun, Monk. and I can handle anything."

Mok Gyeong-un turned a page, revealing an illustration of a swirling mass of golden energy.

"This... this is the source. and I intend to find it."

He raised his left arm, and the golden-white energy surged forward, creating a faint glow that illuminated the entire temple.

"The quest for the ultimate truth continues."

The game was no longer a quest for power. It was a search for the origins of existence. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the way.
