Chapter 211: On the Boat (2)

Whoosh!

The rain began to fall as the boat entered the Imperial City's main dock.

The water was cold and grey, and it seemed to wash away the last remnants of the boy's disguise.

Mok Gyeong-un stood at the bow, his eyes fixed on the city gates.

"The capital... it's a city of a thousand secrets," the boatman said, his voice full of a local's pride.

"And I intend to find every single one of them," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his voice calm and steady.

He stepped off the boat and into the city, his presence a cold, quiet radiance that made the crowds part for him.

"Master... the Shadow Clan is waiting for us at the Moon Fragrance Tower," Wi Maeng whispered, his sightless eyes scanning the area.

"Good. For I have a lot of work for them to do."

Mok Gyeong-un walked through the rain, his cloak billowed by the wind.

The game was no longer a transition. It was an occupation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was taking the territory.
