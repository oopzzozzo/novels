Chapter 195: Loyalty (2)

"Ahem. If you become the master, then what about the Society Leader?"

One of the high-ranking members, a man named Elder Han, asked the question that was on everyone's mind.

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the Elder with his lifeless eyes.

"The Society Leader is gone, Elder Han. and I am his successor. Isn't that what you all agreed to?"

The Elder hesitated.

"We did. but the leader was the one who built this society. He was our father, our mentor."

"And he was also the one who was leading you into the void. the one who was sacrificing your lives for his own ambition."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up and walked to the center of the hall.

"I am not your father, Elder. and I am not your mentor. I am your master. and I will lead you to a future that is far greater than anything the old leader could have imagined."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"Now, choose. Loyalty to a dead man, or loyalty to the void?"

One by one, the members began to bow their heads.

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a conversion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was preaching the gospel.
