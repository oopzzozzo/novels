Chapter 49: Heaven & Earth Society (1)

What is the talent of a diviner?

There would be several qualifications.

Among those qualifications, a clear mind and a strong will are paramount.

For a diviner must be able to peer into the void without being consumed by it.

They must be able to command the spirits without being controlled by them.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently reading a scroll on the history of the Heaven & Earth Society.

The Demonic Monk, now a mere whisper in the boy's mind, watched with interest.

"The Heaven & Earth Society... they were once a noble organization," the Monk said.

"And now?"

"Now, they are a shadow of their former selves. Obsessed with power, they have turned to the dark arts to achieve their goals."

Mok Gyeong-un turned a page.

"Interesting. So, they're not just a group of assassins."

"No. they are a network of diviners, warriors, and scholars. All dedicated to the pursuit of the ultimate truth."

"The ultimate truth? And what is that?"

The Monk's voice became solemn.

"The origins of the world. The nature of existence. The power of the gods."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then we have a lot in common. For I am also a seeker of the truth."

The game was becoming a quest. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the way.
