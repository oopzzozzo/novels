Chapter 123: Demon Sword (3)

A white-haired man with his entire body bound in iron chains.

Having been imprisoned for a long time, his skin was pale and his muscles had withered away.

But his eyes were still sharp and intelligent.

He was the former master of the Evil Commandment Sword, a man who had been driven mad by its power and then imprisoned by the Society.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently standing in front of his cell, the black sword in his hand.

"So, you're the one who lost his mind to this blade," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice neutral.

The man looked up, his eyes fixed on the sword.

"It's not... a loss of mind. it's a... conversion. A realization of the true nature of reality."

His voice was a mere raspy whisper.

"And what realization is that?"

"That everything is... meaningless. Except for the void."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then we have a lot to talk about."

He raised the sword and cut through the chains like they were paper.

"Now, tell me. How do I unlock the sword's true potential?"

The man's eyes glinted with a hidden light.

"It requires... a sacrifice. A sacrifice of something you hold dear."

Mok Gyeong-un's smile widened.

"I hold nothing dear. Except for the void."

The game was becoming a mentorship. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was learning from the master of madness.
