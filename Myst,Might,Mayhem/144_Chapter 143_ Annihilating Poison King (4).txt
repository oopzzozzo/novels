Chapter 143: Annihilating Poison King (4)

'What is this power?'

Dok Go-jin, the Poison King, was staring in shock as Mok Gyeong-un used the new formula to refine his dark energy.

The energy was no longer just cold and suffocating. It was now toxic, a literal breath of death that could kill with a single touch.

"It's... it's perfect," Dok Go-jin whispered, his own malice replaced by a grudging respect.

Mok Gyeong-un flexed his left arm, and the dark, toxic energy surged forward, creating a cloud of green smoke that filled the laboratory.

"Yes. It's exactly what I needed for the ritual."

He turned to the Poison King.

"Now, tell me. Where is the leader of the Heaven & Earth Society? I have a special gift for him."

Dok Go-jin smiled, a cold and predatory smile.

"He's in the inner sanctum. In the Chamber of the Sun."

"Good. Then let's not keep him waiting."

Mok Gyeong-un walked out of the laboratory, the dark, toxic energy swirling around him.

The game was no longer a partnership. It was an assassination. And Mok Gyeong-un was the primary assassin.
