Chapter 301: Diabolic Beast (4)

"Kiririk! You damn Taoist priest!"

The voice was full of absolute fury as it echoed through the City of the Void.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently facing a group of Taoist priests who had been sent by the Righteous Alliance to stop him.

The priests were using their spiritual powers to create a barrier of golden light, but Mok Gyeong-un was cutting through it with ease.

"Your light is a shadow, priests. and shadows are meant to be erased," he said, his voice flat and neutral.

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a shockwave that shattered the barrier and knocked the priests back.

"I am the Void Sun. and I am the one who is providing the judgment."

The priests tried to resist, but their power was dwarfed by the boy's.

One by one, the priests fell, their bodies broken and their souls consumed by the void.

The battle for the City of the Void was over. and the winner was clear.

Mok Gyeong-un was now the true god of the void. and the master of the universe.
