Chapter 191: The Test (2)

Everyone in the training courtyard was staring in shock as Mok Gyeong-un returned from the void.

He was different. His presence was no longer overwhelming or suffocating. It was... quiet.

"Master... you're back," Yeom Ga said, his voice full of relief.

"I am. and the test is complete," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his voice calm and steady.

He looked at the candidates, his transparent eyes scanning their faces.

"You have all survived the initial training. But the real test is just beginning."

He raised his left arm, but there was no dark energy, no red thread.

"I have mastered the void. and now, I will show you how to do the same."

The candidates were silent. They could feel the change in their master, and they were intrigued by this new, quiet power.

"But remember, the void is not a tool for dominance. It is a path to enlightenment."

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a philosophy. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was teaching it.
