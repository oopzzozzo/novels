Chapter 121: Demon Sword (1)

"If you agree to join me, I will give you one of the legendary demon swords, the Evil Commandment Sword."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was tempting, like a siren's song.

He was facing a young man who was one of the Society's most talented swordsmen.

The young man's eyes widened. The Evil Commandment Sword was a weapon of legend, said to be forged in the fires of the underworld.

"A demon sword? And you're giving it to me?"

"Yes. But only if you pledge your absolute loyalty to me. and only if you're willing to use it for my cause."

The young man hesitated. He knew the sword was cursed. But the lure of power was too strong.

"I... I accept."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of enemies who need to feel the bite of that blade."

He reached into the void and pulled out a long, black sword.

The air in the room became cold and heavy as the blade was revealed.

The game was becoming a quest for artifacts. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was handing out the prizes.
