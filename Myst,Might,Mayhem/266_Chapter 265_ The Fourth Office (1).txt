Chapter 265: The Fourth Office (1)

"Oho?"

Su-ryun, the leader of the Fourth Office, let out a small, interested sound as she received the report about Mok Gyeong-un's arrival in the Northern Mountains.

The Fourth Office was the Society's primary research and development unit, and they were responsible for creating new and deadly techniques.

"The master of the void... he's coming here?" she asked her top assistant.

"He is, Master. and it's rumored he's looking for the final focus."

Su-ryun smiled, a cold and analytical smile.

"Fascinating. We've been wanting to test our new techniques on someone with his potential."

She called for her top researchers.

"Prepare the ritual chamber. and make sure all the sensors are active. I want to record every moment of this confrontation."

"Yes, Master."

The game was becoming a scientific experiment. And Mok Gyeong-un was the primary subject.
