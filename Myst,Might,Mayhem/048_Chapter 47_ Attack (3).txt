Chapter 47: Attack (3)

The fight between the warriors of the Yeon Mok Sword Manor and the masked individuals was temporarily halted.

Both sides were staring in shock at the courtyard, where a young boy was holding a diviner captive with a red thread.

"What... what is that boy?" one of the masked individuals whispered.

"I don't know. But he's not human," another replied.

Mok Gyeong-un ignored them. His focus was entirely on Go San.

"Now, let's try this again. Who sent you?"

Go San gritted his teeth, his face pale with pain and fear.

"The... Heaven & Earth Society," he finally managed to say.

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes flickered with interest.

"The Heaven & Earth Society? And what do they want with me?"

"They want... the ritual. They want the power of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"The ritual... it seems everyone is interested in my work."

He looked at the masked individuals.

"And you? Are you with them?"

One of the masked individuals stepped forward.

"We are. And we are here to take what is ours."

The game was no longer a mystery. It was a confrontation between two powerful forces. And Mok Gyeong-un was caught in the middle.
