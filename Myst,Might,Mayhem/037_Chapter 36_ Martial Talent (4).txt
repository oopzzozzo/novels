Chapter 36: Martial Talent (4)

Mok Yu-cheon, the youngest young master of Yeon Mok Sword Manor.

He had no choice but to mature earlier than other children.

Being the youngest son of the Manor Master, he was often overlooked and ignored.

But he had a secret. He was a prodigy.

From a young age, he had mastered the Yeon Mok Sword Style, and he was already more skilled than his older brothers.

However, he kept his talent hidden, waiting for the right moment to strike.

He was currently in the training courtyard, watching as Mok Gyeong-un practiced his sword forms.

His eyes were filled with curiosity and suspicion.

"Who is that boy?" he asked his personal guard.

"He is the one Lady Seok brought in as a substitute, Young Master. It's rumored he saved the Manor Master's life."

Mok Yu-cheon's eyes narrowed.

"A substitute... and he has such talent?"

He watched as Mok Gyeong-un performed a particularly difficult move with ease.

"He's not just a substitute. He's something else entirely."

Mok Yu-cheon stepped forward, his hand on the hilt of his sword.

"I think it's time I met this 'genius'."

The game was becoming a confrontation. And Mok Yu-cheon was ready to play his part.
