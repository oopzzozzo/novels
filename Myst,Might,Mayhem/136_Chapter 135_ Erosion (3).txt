Chapter 135: Erosion (3)

The blind man Wi Maeng, who had been sitting in the corner of the meeting hall, finally spoke.

He was the eldest disciple of the Society Leader, a man who was respected and feared by everyone in the organization.

"Young Master Mok. Your words are bold. but they are also dangerous," he said, his voice calm and steady.

"The leader is not just a man. He is the heart of this society. If you destroy him, you destroy us all."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the blind man.

"I am not destroying the Society, Wi Maeng. I am... purging it. Of the darkness that has infected its heart."

"And you think you are the one who can do that? A boy who has only recently mastered the void?"

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"I don't think. I know. For I am the void's representative in this world."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, filling the hall with a cold, suffocating pressure.

"Now, tell me. Will you stand with me, or will you stand with the darkness?"

Wi Maeng was silent for a moment. He could feel the power of the boy's will, and the absolute dominance of his presence.

"I... I will stand with the truth," he finally said, his voice a mere whisper.

The game was becoming an alliance. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the faction.
