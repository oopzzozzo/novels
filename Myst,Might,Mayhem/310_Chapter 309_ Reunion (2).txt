Chapter 309: Reunion (2)

On a rainy midnight.

Mok Gyeong-un was sitting in his original house, the one he had grown up in before the void gods took him.

He was surrounded by his villagers, who were now free from the void's influence.

"It's... it's really you, Gyeong-un," an old woman said, her voice full of a grandmother's love.

"I am here, Grandmother. and I'm never leaving again."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, a smile that was finally warm and human.

He had achieved everything he had set out to do. He had become a god, he had unified the Society, and he had brought his people back.

But in the end, it was this simple reunion that meant the most to him.

"Master... the Shadow King is here," Wi Maeng whispered, appearing in the doorway.

"He says that the final focus is ready."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at his villagers, then at the Shadow King.

"Tell him to wait. I have a more important meeting to attend."

The game was no longer a conquest. It was a life. And Mok Gyeong-un was finally living it.
