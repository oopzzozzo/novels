Chapter 162: Proposition (2)

If one were to analyze the situation, they would see that Wi So-yeon was in a hopeless position.

He was facing two masters of the void, and his own power was dwarfed by theirs.

But So-yeon was not a man who gave up easily.

"I will never serve you, Gyeong-un! I will die before I betray the leader!" he shouted, launching a powerful sword technique at the boy.

Mok Gyeong-un didn't even flinch. He just raised his left arm and caught the blade between his fingers.

The dark energy from his arm instantly shattered the steel, and So-yeon fell back in shock.

"Your loyalty is admirable, So-yeon. but it is also misplaced," Mok Gyeong-un said, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

"The leader you serve is already gone. There is only the darkness left."

He raised his hand, and the red thread surged forward, wrapping around So-yeon's neck.

"Now, tell me. Will you take the proposition, or will you take the consequences?"

So-yeon's eyes were filled with a mixture of fury and despair.

"I... I will... never..."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then the consequences it is."

The room was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the agonizing screams of Wi So-yeon.

The game was becoming a liquidation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was doing the counting.
