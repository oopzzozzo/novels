Chapter 237: The Disregard for the Embroided Uniform Guard (6)

Mok Gyeong-un sat on the Imperial Throne, his golden-white eyes scanning the meeting hall.

The high-ranking members of the Society, the leaders of the other clans, and the remnants of the imperial family were all gathered there, bowing their heads in absolute submission.

"From today onward, the Imperial City is the capital of the Void Sun domain," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice echoing with the power of the void.

"And all of you are my subjects. My tools for the creation of a new world."

The citizens of the city erupted in cheers, a sound of raw, primal energy that shake the very foundations of the city.

The game was over for the mortal world. but for the void gods, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un was now the undisputed ruler of the universe.

And he had everything he ever wanted.

Except for one thing.

The answer to the ultimate question.

The question of his own existence.

The game was becoming a quest for self-discovery. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the way.
