Chapter 216: Secret Mission (3)

In the inner sanctum of the Garden of the Sun, the Emperor's most elite guard unit was waiting.

They were in position, their swords drawn and their expressions full of hidden power.

"They're coming," one of the guards said, his voice a mere rasp.

"I can feel the cold wind of the void."

Suddenly, a series of muffled explosions echoed through the garden, followed by the sound of clashing steel.

The infiltration team had arrived.

The guards rushed to meet them, and the battle began.

It was a brutal and bloody affair, as the team and the guards fought each other with everything they had.

Mok Gyeong-un watched from the sidelines, his eyes glowing with an intense, crimson light.

He was waiting for the right moment to strike.

"The final focus... it's here," he whispered to himself.

The game was no longer a mission. It was a massacre. And Mok Gyeong-un was the primary assassin.
