Chapter 264: Clue (4)

"I heard that Fire Faith Order believes in the power of the original gods."

Mok Gyeong-un was currently meeting with a representative of the Fire Faith Order in the Northern Mountains.

The Order was a small, secretive group that worshiped the ancient gods of creation and destruction.

"We do, Young Master. and we believe that you are the one who will lead us back to them," the representative said, his voice full of fanatical devotion.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"And why would I do that?"

"Because you have the power of the void. and you have the key to the final gateway."

The representative leaned forward, his voice a mere whisper.

"The seventh focus... it's in the Hall of Eternal Fire. but only the High Priest has the key."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes glinted with interest.

"The High Priest... it seems I have another target to meet."

The game was becoming a series of confrontations. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the challenges.
