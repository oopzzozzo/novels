Chapter 210: On the Boat (1)

Most of the able-bodied men in the village were currently working on the local dock, loading and unloading cargo from the boats that traveled along the river.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently on one of those boats, heading towards the main dock of the Imperial City.

He was traveling in disguise, but his presence was still enough to make the other passengers uneasy.

"The Imperial City... it's a place of many contrasts," he said to himself as he watched the passing scenery.

"Wealth and poverty, power and weakness, light and shadow."

The Demonic Monk, now a mere shadow, appeared from the darkness of the cabin.

"And what about the void, Master? Is there any contrast there?"

"The void is the origin of all contrasts, Monk. and the end of all of them."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at his left arm, the ancient skin pulsing with a faint, dark light.

"I am the void. and I am the contrast."

The boat continued its journey towards the capital, the dark energy of its passenger swirling around it like a hidden current.

The game was becoming a transition. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was moving the pieces.
