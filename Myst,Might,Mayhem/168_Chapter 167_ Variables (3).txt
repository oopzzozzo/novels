Chapter 167: Variables (3)

On the roof of a nearby building, a man was observing the confrontation in the courtyard.

He was a master of the Shadow Clan, a man who had been sent by the Shadow King to oversee the infiltration.

His name was Han Gyeong-jin.

He was thinking about the boy, Mok Gyeong-un.

The boy had achieved something that no one else in the clan had ever managed: he had successfully infiltrated the Imperial City.

"Mok Gyeong-un... you are truly a marvel," Gyeong-jin muttered to himself.

But he also knew the danger. The Imperial City was a place of many variables, and the void was a fickle force.

"I must find a way to support him. To ensure that he achieves his goals."

Gyeong-jin's goal was clear: he wanted to be the one who helped the master of the void.

He wanted to be the power behind the throne.

But he also knew that Mok Gyeong-un was not a boy who could be easily helped.

The game was becoming a long-term strategy. And Gyeong-jin was the one who was planning the moves.
