Chapter 71: True Meaning (3)

The giant monster wolf, frozen in a posture as if about to leap at Mo Ha-rang of the Demon Fire Hall, was a sight to behold.

It was a creature of the void, a being that had been summoned by Mok Gyeong-un's power.

Mo Ha-rang, a powerful warrior in her own right, was staring at the wolf with a mixture of fear and awe.

"Wh-what is that thing?" she managed to gasp.

"It's a gift," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his voice neutral.

"A gift? From where?"

"From the void. And it's here to help us."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped towards the wolf and touched its fur.

And the creature seemed to purr, a low, rumbling sound that vibrated through the very foundations of the courtyard.

"The void... it's not just a place of darkness," he said, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

"It's a place of creation. A place where anything is possible."

He looked at Mo Ha-rang.

"And now, we're going to use that power to change the world."

The game was no longer a heist. It was a revolution. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the charge.
