Chapter 80: Silent Strides (3)

When a person is overwhelmed by extreme fear, they say the sphincter and bladder become uncontrollable.

This was exactly what was happening to the agent of the Silent Strides who had been captured by Mok Gyeong-un.

He was currently hanging from a tree, his body bound by the red thread.

Mok Gyeong-un stood below him, his lifeless eyes watching with intense curiosity.

"So, you're an agent of the Silent Strides. And you were sent to eliminate me."

The agent tried to speak, but his voice was a mere whimper.

"It's... it's a mistake... we didn't know... who you were."

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"Didn't know? Or didn't care? It doesn't matter. Now, you're going to tell me everything about your organization."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

The agent's screams echoed through the forest, a sound of raw, primal terror.

The interrogation had begun. And Mok Gyeong-un was getting exactly what he wanted.

The game was no longer an infiltration. It was a harvest. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was gathering the souls.
