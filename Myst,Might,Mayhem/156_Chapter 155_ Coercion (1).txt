Chapter 155: Coercion (1)

The Society Leader's face was a mask of cold fury.

He was currently meeting with Mok Gyeong-un in the Sun Tower, and the tension in the room was palpable.

"You've been very busy, Young Master Mok," the leader said, his voice a deep and raspy whisper.

"I've only been doing what's necessary, Leader. To secure my position. And to prepare for the ritual."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was calm and steady, but it carried an underlying threat.

"The ritual... you think you can complete it without my help?"

"I don't think. I know. For I am the master of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, filling the room with a cold, suffocating pressure.

"Now, tell me. Where is the focus? I want it. and I want it now."

The leader laughed, a cold and hollow sound.

"You think you can coerce me? I am the founder of this society! I am the one who discovered the void!"

"And you are also the one who is being consumed by it. Your time is over, Leader. Give me the focus, or die."

The game was becoming a confrontation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was making the moves.
