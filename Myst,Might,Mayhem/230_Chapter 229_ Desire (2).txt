Chapter 229: Desire (2)

"Hehehe. Your Majesty, I have found what you were looking for."

Mu Jang-yak's voice was full of hidden excitement as he entered Mok Gyeong-un's private quarters.

He had been tasked with finding a challenge worthy of the master of the void, and he had finally found it.

"A challenge? And what could it be, Jang-yak?" Mok Gyeong-un asked, his eyes black and lifeless.

"It's... the Forbidden Temple. In the heart of the Northern Mountains."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes glinted with interest.

"The Forbidden Temple? I've heard stories about that place. Stories of a power that is even older than the void."

"Yes, Master. and it's rumored that the temple is guarded by the original void gods. The ones who were cast out of the heavens."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Original gods... and a power older than the void. This sounds like exactly what I need."

He stood up and adjusted his cloak.

"Now, tell me. How do I get there?"

"It's a long and dangerous journey, Master. but I've already prepared the route."

"Good. Then let the expedition begin."

The game was becoming a divine hunt. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the pack.
