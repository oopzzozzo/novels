Chapter 215: Secret Mission (2)

"If we need to use this, then the mission has already failed."

Mok Gyeong-un was currently meeting with his infiltration team in the Palace's secret drainage tunnels.

The team was composed of his top agents, and they were all dressed in dark, form-fitting robes.

"I understand, Master. but it's better to be prepared," the team leader replied, his voice a mere whisper.

They were currently approaching the entrance to the Garden of the Sun, and the tension was palpable.

"The final focus... it's just beyond this wall," Mok Gyeong-un said, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

"Now, move out. and remember, silence is your only weapon."

The team disappeared into the darkness, their movements fast and fluid.

Mok Gyeong-un watched them go, his presence a cold, quiet radiance that illuminated the tunnel.

The mission was underway. and the fate of the Empire was hanging in the balance.

The game was becoming a race against time. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the watch.
