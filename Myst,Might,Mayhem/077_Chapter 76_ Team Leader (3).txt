Chapter 76: Team Leader (3)

"Ah. Don't mind it and just continue what you were doing."

Mok Gyeong-un, who was flicking his blood-stained fingers, spoke nonchalantly to the boys in the training courtyard.

They were in the middle of a brutal training session, and one of the boys had just collapsed, his body convulsing from the intensity of the void energy.

"But Master... he's dying," Yeom Ga, the temporary team leader, said, his voice full of concern.

"He's not dying. He's evolving. If he can't withstand the energy, then he was never meant to serve me."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped closer to the collapsed boy.

"The void doesn't care about weakness. It only cares about results."

He raised his left arm, and the red thread connected to the boy.

And suddenly, the boy's convulsions stopped. His eyes snapped open, glowing with a cold, blue fire.

He stood up, his body pulsing with a new, dark power.

"I... I am... ready," he whispered.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. Now, continue the session."

The game was becoming a factory. A factory that produced monsters. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was running the assembly line.
