Chapter 209: Karma (6)

The resentful soul Han Gyeong-jin was staring at Mok Gyeong-un with a mixture of disbelief and fury.

He had been the one who was supposed to be the master of the mental plane, but now he was just a prisoner of the void.

"How... how did you do it?" he managed to gasp, his voice a mere echo in the mental plane.

"I didn't do anything, Gyeong-jin. I just showed you the truth. and the truth is that your mind is just a shadow."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, his transparent eyes fixed on the soul.

"The Indigo Spirit is mine. and now, your memories are mine as well."

He reached out his hand and touched the soul's forehead.

And for a moment, the mental plane was filled with a blinding, white radiance.

Han Gyeong-jin's memories began to flow into Mok Gyeong-un's mind, revealing the secrets of the Shadow Clan and the Society Leader's final plans.

"The seventh focus... it's in the Forbidden City," Mok Gyeong-un whispered, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

The game was no longer a mental wrestling match. It was a heist of memories. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was taking the prizes.
