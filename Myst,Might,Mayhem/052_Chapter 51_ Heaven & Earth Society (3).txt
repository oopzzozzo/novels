Chapter 51: Heaven & Earth Society (3)

The Bright Blade King Son Yun, who had been glaring at Mok Gyeong-un, burst into laughter and said,

"Wonderful! Simply wonderful! To think that such a small boy could possess such overwhelming power!"

He sheathed his sword, the blinding light fading away.

"I accept my defeat. You are truly the master of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un lowered his left arm, the dark energy receding into the ancient skin.

"And what now, Bright Blade King? Will you serve me, or will you die?"

Son Yun bowed his head.

"I will serve you, Great One. And the Heaven & Earth Society will follow my lead."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of work for you to do."

He looked at the survivors in the courtyard.

"The manor is now under my control. And soon, the entire region will follow."

He turned to the Demonic Monk, who was watching from the shadows.

"The ritual is almost complete. The gateway to the void is opening."

The game was no longer a battle. It was a conquest. And Mok Gyeong-un was the new emperor.
