Chapter 85: Master of the Primal Killing Pavilion (1)

'Damn mortal bastard!'

Was he finally trying to find out about her past in this way?

For a moment, she, the Master of the Primal Killing Pavilion, felt a wave of cold fury.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently standing in front of her, his eyes as still as a dead person's.

"You're the one they call the Primal Killer," he said, his voice neutral.

"And you're the one who's been causing trouble for the Society," she replied, her voice full of hidden power.

"I'm not causing trouble. I'm just... curious."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped closer to her.

"I want to know about the Pavilion. And I want to know about your connection to the void."

The Primal Killer laughed, a cold, sharp sound.

"You think you can just walk in here and ask about my secrets?"

"I don't think. I know. For I am the master of the void."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

The battle had begun. And the Primal Killing Pavilion was the arena.
