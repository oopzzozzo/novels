Chapter 109: Fateful Luck (2)

Gyu Soha, who had possessed Elder Yang Mu-won, the keeper of the treasure vault, was diligently carrying out her duties.

She was currently organizing the records of the recent events in the manor.

"The master of the void... he has finally appeared," she whispered to herself, her eyes glinting with a hidden light.

She knew that her own survival depended on her ability to serve this new master.

"I must find a way to become indispensable to him. To ensure that he needs me as much as I need him."

She turned a page in her ledger, recording the details of Mok Gyeong-un's rebirth.

"He is no longer just a boy. He is a force of nature. And I am the one who will help him harness it."

Gyu Soha smiled, a calm and calculating smile.

The game was becoming a long-term strategy. And she was the one who was playing the long game.
