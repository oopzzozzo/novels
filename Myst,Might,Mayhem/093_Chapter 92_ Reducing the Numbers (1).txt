Chapter 92: Reducing the Numbers (1)

Mok Yu-cheon looked at the small, round copper tag resting on his palm with a dazed expression.

This was his entry permit for the Society's annual tournament, a competition that determined the ranking of the disciples.

But it was also a death sentence.

The tournament was known for its high mortality rate, and only the strongest survived.

"Yu-cheon... you're not going, are you?" his personal guard asked, his voice full of concern.

Yu-cheon looked up from the tag.

"I have to. I need to prove that I am worthy of the Mok name. I need to prove that I am more than just a prodigy."

He tightened his grip on the tag.

"And I need to see what Mok Gyeong-un is planning. He's also entered the tournament."

The guard's eyes widened.

"Mok Gyeong-un? But he's the master of the manor! Why would he enter a tournament for disciples?"

"I don't know. But I intend to find out."

Yu-cheon stood up and drew his sword.

"Prepare my gear. I'm going to the tournament."

The game was becoming a battle royale. And Mok Yu-cheon was ready to fight for his life.
