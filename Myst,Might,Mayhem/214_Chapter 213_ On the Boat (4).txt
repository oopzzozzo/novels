Chapter 213: On the Boat (4)

Moonless Void Sword – Moon Fragrance Defeating Sword.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently practicing this technique on the deck of the boat, his movements a blur of motion that the other passengers couldn't even follow.

He was using the quiet power of the void to enhance his speed and his precision, creating a weapon of pure energy.

"Incredible," the boatman whispered as he watched the boy.

"He's not even using a sword. He's just using his fingers."

Mok Gyeong-un stopped and looked at his hands.

"The sword is just a tool, boatman. and the void is the only true weapon."

He turned towards the city, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

"The temple is close. and soon, the Three Eyes will know the true meaning of the Moonless Void Sword."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a shockwave that rattled the boat's hull.

"Proceed. and don't stop until we reach the main dock."

The game was no longer a transition. It was a demonstration. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was showing off.
