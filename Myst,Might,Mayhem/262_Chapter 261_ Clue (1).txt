Chapter 261: Clue (1)

-Stab!

"Uuugh!"

The agent of the Shadow Alliance fell to the floor, his chest pierced by Mok Gyeong-un's finger.

Mok Gyeong-un stood over the now-silent warrior, his eyes as still as a dead person's.

"The Shadow Alliance... they're persistent, I'll give them that," he said to himself.

He was currently in a small, remote cabin in the Northern Mountains, investigating a clue regarding the seventh focus.

The clue had led him to this cabin, which was a hidden retreat for the Alliance's leadership.

"The seventh focus... it's not just a focus. It's a key. A key to the final gateway."

Mok Gyeong-un reached into the agent's robes and pulled out a small, iron key.

It was covered in ancient, glowing runes that seemed to pulse with a hidden power.

"Found it."

The Demonic Monk, now a mere shadow of a shadow, appeared from the darkness of the cabin.

"The final key... you've found it, Great One. Now, the ritual can reach its true conclusion."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Yes. It's exactly what I needed."

The game was becoming a completion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was finding the last piece.
