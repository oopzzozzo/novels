Chapter 295: Shaolin Temple (2)

"Amitabha. I say this for your own sake, boy. You are playing with a fire that will consume you."

The Abbot's voice was full of a hidden wisdom as he faced Mok Gyeong-un in the temple's courtyard.

The battle had been short, but it had shown the Abbot the true extent of the boy's power.

"I am not playing with fire, Abbot. I am the fire," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his golden-white eyes fixed on the old monk.

"And I am the one who is providing the warmth."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a faint radiance that illuminated the courtyard.

"The eighth focus... it's hidden here, isn't it? In the Pagoda of a Thousand Souls."

The Abbot's eyes widened in disbelief.

"How... how do you know about that? That pagoda has been sealed for centuries!"

"I know because I've seen it. and I've seen it because I am the void."

He stepped closer to the Abbot.

"Now, tell me. How do I open the pagoda?"

The game was becoming a trial of faith. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the doubt.
