Chapter 86: Master of the Primal Killing Pavilion (2)

Spirit God Parasitic Poisoning Technique[1].

It was a technique referred to as a forbidden secret art of the Primal Killing Pavilion.

It involved infecting the target with a spiritual parasite that would consume their soul and replace it with the will of the master.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently watching as the Primal Killer prepared to use this technique on him.

"You think you can bind me, boy? You think you can control the void better than I can?" she asked, her voice full of malice.

"I don't think. I know," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

The Primal Killer lunged forward, her hands glowing with a sickly, green light.

But as she touched Mok Gyeong-un, she felt a sudden, sharp pain in her own chest.

"Wh-what's happening? My technique... it's rebounding!"

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"The void doesn't care about your techniques, Killer. It only cares about the host."

He raised his left arm, and the red thread surged forward, wrapping around the Primal Killer.

"Now, tell me. Where is the leader of the Heaven & Earth Society hiding?"

The Primal Killer tried to resist, but the parasite was already working its way through her soul.

"I... I will... serve... Master," she managed to choke out, her eyes turning a cold, blue fire.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of work for you to do."

The game was becoming a parasitic infection. And Mok Gyeong-un was the primary host.
