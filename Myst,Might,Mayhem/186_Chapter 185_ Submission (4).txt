Chapter 185: Submission (4)

"From now on, treat everyone here exactly like you treat me."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was firm, and his eyes were filled with a cold, focused light.

He was currently in the Palace's main hall, facing the remaining members of the imperial family and the high-ranking officials.

The Princess was among them, her expression a mask of cold determination.

"What do you mean, Master?" she asked, her voice trembling slightly.

"I mean that there is no more imperial family. There is no more empire. There is only the void."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped closer to the throne.

"And you are all my servants. My tools for the ritual."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, filling the hall with a cold, suffocating pressure.

"The submission is complete. The fifth focus is mine. and the gateway is fully open."

The members of the imperial family bowed their heads in absolute submission.

The game was over for the Empire. but for the void gods, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un sat on the throne, his eyes black and lifeless.

He was the new Emperor of the Void.
