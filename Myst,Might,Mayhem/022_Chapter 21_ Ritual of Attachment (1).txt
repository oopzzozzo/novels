Chapter 21: Ritual of Attachment (1)

The connected red thread.

It signified that They were connected.

A servant ghost.

The Blue ghost being, which was the Demonic Monk, was now bound to the boy via this thread.

It was a connection that transcended the physical world.

Mok Gyeong-un could feel the Monk's presence, his thoughts, and his power.

"So this is the thread of fate," he muttered.

The Monk bowed his head.

"It is, Great One. We are now one."

"One? No. You are my servant. I am the master."

"Of course, of course."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at his left arm, now covered in the ancient skin.

The red thread seemed to pulse with the same dark energy.

"Now, tell me about the first stage of the ritual."

"The first stage... it requires a sacrifice. A sacrifice of pure blood."

"Pure blood? And where do we find that?"

The Monk's eyes glinted.

"In the main hall. The Manor Master's own kin."

Mok Gyeong-un's smile turned cold.

"His kin... this keeps getting better and better."

He stood up, the dark energy swirling around him.

"Let's go. We have a family reunion to attend."

The game was becoming a tragedy. And Mok Gyeong-un was the lead actor.
