Chapter 32: Guyeo (4)

"Aaargh!"

"M-Madaaaam!"

The maidservants wailed in agony.

The veins on their faces swelled, as if they were about to burst.

Lady Seok watched in horror as her loyal servants were consumed by the dark energy.

"Wh-what is this? What are you doing to them?!" she screamed at Mok Gyeong-un.

The boy stood in the center of the room, his left arm glowing with a blinding, crimson light.

"I'm just giving them what they deserve, Mother. A chance to serve a true master."

The red thread connecting Mok Gyeong-un to the entities of the void pulsed with power.

One by one, the maidservants fell silent, their eyes turning a cold, blue fire.

They were no longer human. They were the Guyeo.

"Now, Mother. It's your turn."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped towards Lady Seok.

"No! Stay back! I am the First Madam of this manor!"

"Not anymore. Now, you are just... blood."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

Lady Seok's screams were cut short as she was consumed by the void.

Yeon Mok Sword Manor was now a house of ghosts. And Mok Gyeong-un was its king.

The game was over for the living. But for the dead, it was just beginning.
