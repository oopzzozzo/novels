Chapter 280: Holy Fire Priestess (3)

Embroidered Uniform Guard members were currently patrolling the perimeter of the Hall of Eternal Fire, their expressions full of grim determination.

They had been sent by the Emperor to stop Mok Gyeong-un, and they were prepared to die for their duty.

"They're coming," one of the guards said, his voice a mere rasp.

"I can feel the heat of the primordial fire."

Suddenly, a series of blinding explosions echoed through the hall, followed by the sound of clashing steel.

Mok Gyeong-un's shadow unit had arrived.

The guards rushed to meet them, and the battle began.

It was a brutal and bloody affair, as the unit and the guards fought each other with everything they had.

Mok Gyeong-un watched from the balcony of the hall, his eyes glowing with an intense, golden light.

The mobilization was complete. and the world was ready for the final stage.

The game was no longer a confrontation of elements. It was a deconstruction of the old world. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was taking the pieces apart.
