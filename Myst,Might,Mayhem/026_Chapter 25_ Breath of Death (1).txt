Chapter 25: Breath of Death (1)

"The dagger and internal energy are yours."

'!?'

Guard Gam's eyes trembled at Mok Gyeong-un's words.

The boy was offering him the very things he had been seeking.

"Wh-what do you mean?"

"Exactly as I said. I have no use for them. But you... you could use them to become more than a mere guard."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes glinted with a strange, dark light.

"You've been loyal to the Manor Master for a long time. It's time you were rewarded."

Guard Gam hesitated. He knew the boy was dangerous. But the offer was too tempting to ignore.

"And what do you want in exchange?"

"Your service. Your complete and absolute loyalty."

Guard Gam looked at the dagger, then at the boy.

He knew he was making a deal with the devil.

But the lure of power was too strong.

"I... I accept."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. Now, let's begin your training. I'll show you how to use the Breath of Death."

The courtyard was silent as the two of them began their dark pact.

The game was no longer about survival. It was about dominance. And Mok Gyeong-un was the master of it all.
