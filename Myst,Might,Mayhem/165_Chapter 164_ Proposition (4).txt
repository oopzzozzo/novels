Chapter 164: Proposition (4)

"To show you how serious I am, I will give you a glimpse of the ultimate truth."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was solemn, and his eyes were filled with an ancient, terrifying wisdom.

He was currently meeting with the remaining members of the Society's leadership in the Sun Tower.

"The void is not just a place of power, members. It is the origin of all things. and the end of all things."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a holographic projection of the universe.

The members watched in awe as the stars and galaxies swirled around them, each one a mere speck in the vast expanse of the void.

"This is what we are fighting for. The chance to become one with the void. The chance to become gods."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the members, his presence overwhelming.

"Now, tell me. Are you ready to take the proposition? Are you ready to follow me to the end of the universe?"

One by one, the members began to cheer, a sound of absolute, fanatical devotion.

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a religion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was the messiah.
