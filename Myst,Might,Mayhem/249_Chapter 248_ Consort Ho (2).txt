Chapter 248: Consort Ho (2)

Ordinary monsters were no match for Prince Gyeongjin.

He had spent his entire life training his mind and his body, and he had reached a level of power that was beyond most mortals.

But now, he was facing a different kind of monster.

Consort Ho was not just a beautiful woman. She was a master of the dark arts of seduction and manipulation.

And she was using everything she had to break the Prince's will.

"You're strong, Gyeongjin. but you're also lonely," she whispered, her hands stroking his shoulders.

"And I can give you the one thing you're missing: a partner. someone who can share your burden and your power."

Prince Gyeongjin felt a sudden surge of desire, but he quickly suppressed it.

"I am not lonely, Consort. and I have no need for a partner."

He pushed her away and stood up.

"The Shadow Alliance is a threat to the Empire. and I intend to eliminate it. and you, Consort, are going to help me."

Consort Ho's eyes glinted with fury.

"Help you? You're a fool, Gyeongjin! The Shadow Alliance is more powerful than you can possibly imagine!"

"We'll see about that."

The game was no longer a seduction. It was an interrogation. And Prince Gyeongjin was the one who was holding the needle.
