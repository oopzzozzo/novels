Chapter 232: The Disregard for the Embroidered Uniform Guard (1)

The leader of the Embroidered Uniform Guard, a man named Zhao Gao, was currently sitting in his private office within the Palace.

He was a man of great power and ruthlessness, known for his absolute control over the city's underbelly.

"Mok Gyeong-un has gone too far," he said to his top assistant, his voice full of hidden malice.

"He's acting as if he rules the city. and he's completely disregarding our authority."

The assistant nodded.

"I know, Master. but he is the master of the void. and he has the support of the high-ranking officials."

"I don't care who he is or who supports him. No one disregards the Embroidered Uniform Guard and lives to tell the tale."

Zhao Gao stood up and walked to the window.

"Prepare the unit. We're going to the Sun Tower. and we're going to show this 'Young Master' what true authority looks like."

The game was becoming a battle for the city's soul. And Zhao Gao was the one who was leading the charge.
