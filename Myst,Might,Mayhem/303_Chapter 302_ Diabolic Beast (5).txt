Chapter 302: Diabolic Beast (5)

The Demon-Subduing Pavilion, one of the most powerful units of the Righteous Alliance, was currently in a state of total chaos.

They had been tasked with stopping Mok Gyeong-un, but they had been thoroughly defeated by the boy's power.

"The master of the void... he is a monster!" one of the survivors managed to gasp as he fled the city.

Mok Gyeong-un watched from the Sun Tower, his eyes glowing with an intense, golden light.

The city was now his. and every variable had been accounted for.

"The deification is complete, Master," the Shadow King said, appearing beside him.

"And now, for the final stage. The creation of a new world."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For the universe is now ready for the Void Sun."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a blinding light that illuminated the entire cosmos.

The game was over for the old universe. but for the new one, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un was now the eternal ruler of all things.
