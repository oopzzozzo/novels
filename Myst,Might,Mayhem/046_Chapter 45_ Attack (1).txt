Chapter 45: Attack (1)

A man in navy blue martial arts robes, with two talismans of the Six Armors Total Symbol[1] on his forehead, stood in front of the main gate of Yeon Mok Sword Manor.

He was a diviner, but not just any diviner. He was a master of the Six Armors style, a powerful form of spiritual warfare.

His name was Go San.

He was here to purge the evil that had taken over the manor.

"Evil spirit! Come out and face your judgment!" he shouted, his voice echoing through the courtyard.

The guards at the gate were momentarily stunned. They had never seen anyone so bold.

"Who are you? And what do you want?" one of them demanded.

"I am Go San. And I am here to end the reign of the 'Mok Gyeong-un' who is not a Mok."

The guards drew their swords, but Go San didn't even move.

He just reached into his robes and pulled out a stack of talismans.

"You are already lost. Now, return to the void!"

He threw the talismans into the air, and they burst into a blinding, golden light.

The attack had begun. And Yeon Mok Sword Manor was the battlefield.
