Chapter 158: Coercion (4)

-Snap!

"Ugh."

Eldest disciple Wi Maeng collapsed to the floor of the Sun Tower, his spiritual connection to the leader severed.

He had been trying to support the leader's presence, but Mok Gyeong-un's power had been too much.

In the center of the chamber, the Society Leader's mass of dark energy was also collapsing.

"It's... over, Leader," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice echoing through the ruins.

"The void has spoken. and it has chosen me."

He raised his left arm, and the final surge of dark energy consumed the leader's remains.

The Sun Tower was silent. The battle for the Society was over.

Mok Gyeong-un stood on the high throne, his entire body glowing with a faint, crimson light.

He was the new master of the Heaven & Earth Society.

He looked down at the blind Wi Maeng.

"Now, tell the others. The old order is dead. A new order has begun."

The game was no longer a climax. It was a resolution. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the end.
