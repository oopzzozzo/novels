Chapter 152: Talent (3)

'Flying Transforming Sword.'

Mok Gyeong-un was currently demonstrating a technique that was beyond the reach of most of the applicants.

It was a technique that involved projecting sword energy from the fingertips, creating a weapon of pure power.

The applicants watched in awe as Mok Gyeong-un cut through a row of training dummies with ease.

"This is the power of the void, applicants. This is the power that can be yours."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was sharp and clear, cutting through the silence of the training ground.

"But it is not a power that is given freely. It must be earned. Through blood, sweat, and sacrifice."

He looked at the remaining applicants, his lifeless eyes scanning their faces.

"Only the strongest will be chosen for my shadow unit. Only the ones who are willing to become more than just humans."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint, crimson glow that illuminated the entire training ground.

"The training begins now. And may the void have mercy on your souls."

The game was no longer a transformation. It was a mobilization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the army.
