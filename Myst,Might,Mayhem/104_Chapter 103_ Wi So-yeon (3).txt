Chapter 103: Wi So-yeon (3)

"I apologize, but I must respectfully decline your proposal."

An unexpected refusal to what she thought was a generous offer.

Wi So-yeon, who had been expecting Mok Gyeong-un to jump at the chance to meet the leader, was momentarily stunned.

"Decline? But Young Master, this is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity!"

"I am aware of that, So-yeon. but I have my own plans. And they don't involve the leader."

Mok Gyeong-un turned away, his lifeless eyes fixed on the distant horizon.

"I am the master of the void. And I have no need for a mortal leader."

So-yeon's expression hardened.

"You are making a mistake, Mok Gyeong-un. The leader is not someone you want to offend."

"I am not afraid of the leader. Or anyone else in this society."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"Now, if you'll excuse me. I have work to do."

Mok Gyeong-un walked away, leaving Wi So-yeon alone in the courtyard.

The game was becoming a defiance. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was breaking the rules.
