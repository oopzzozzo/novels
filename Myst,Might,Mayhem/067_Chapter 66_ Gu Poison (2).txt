Chapter 66: Gu Poison (2)

'Is it the difference in grade?'

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes gleamed with interest at the pale white hands reaching out from the jar.

The void Gu was evolving.

It was no longer a mere insect. It was becoming something more. Something... human?

"Fascinating," he whispered, his left arm pulsing with dark energy.

The Demonic Monk watched from the shadows, his form flickering with unease.

"Great One... the Gu is becoming too powerful. It might... it might resist you."

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"Resist me? I am the one who created it. I am its master."

He reached into the jar and touched the pale hands.

And for a moment, he felt a connection that was far deeper than the red thread.

It was a connection of blood and soul.

"We are one," the Gu whispered, its voice echoing in Mok Gyeong-un's mind.

"Yes. We are one."

The game was becoming a fusion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was being consumed.
