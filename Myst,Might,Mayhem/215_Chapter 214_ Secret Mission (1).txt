Chapter 214: Secret Mission (1)

Scrape scrape.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently sharpening a small, ornate dagger in his room at the Moon Fragrance Tower.

He was preparing for a secret mission, one that would take him to the very heart of the Imperial Palace.

"The Garden of the Sun... it's the final focus," he whispered to himself.

"And I intend to take it tonight."

The Shadow King appeared from the shadows.

"The mission is high-risk, Master. The Palace is guarded by the Emperor's most elite unit."

"I am aware of that, King. but risk is just another word for opportunity."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up and adjusted his cloak.

"Now, tell me. What is the status of our infiltration team?"

"They're in position, Master. Waiting for your signal."

"Good. Then let the mission begin."

Mok Gyeong-un walked out of the room, the dark energy of the void swirling around him.

The game was becoming a heist of the highest order. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the team.
