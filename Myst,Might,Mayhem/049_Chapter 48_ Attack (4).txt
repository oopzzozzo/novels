Chapter 48: Attack (4)

Could this be described as merely a change?

Mok Gyeong-un, who nonchalantly suggested sacrificing Lady Seok, was now facing the masked individuals with a chilling calmness.

"Sacrifice? You think I'm here to negotiate?" one of them laughed.

"No. I think you're here to die," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his voice flat and devoid of emotion.

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a shockwave that knocked several of the masked individuals back.

"The Heaven & Earth Society... you're persistent, I'll give you that."

He stepped towards them, his eyes glowing with an intense, crimson light.

"But you're also foolish. You think you can control the void? You think you can control me?"

He raised his hand, and the red thread surged forward, wrapping around the leader of the masked individuals.

"Now, tell me. Where is your headquarters?"

The leader tried to resist, but the binding was absolute.

"I... I will... never... tell you," he managed to gasp.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"We'll see about that."

The courtyard was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the agonizing screams of the masked individuals.

The attack was over. But the war was just beginning.
