Chapter 149: Repayment (3)

"From today onward, the Heaven & Earth Society is no longer a tool for the void gods."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice boomed through the meeting hall, echoing with the power of the void.

He was facing the remaining members of the Society, who were all bowing their heads in absolute submission.

The purge was complete. Every agent who had been in contact with the void gods had been eliminated.

"We are now a force of our own. A force that will lead this world into a new era."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a blinding, crimson light that illuminated the entire hall.

"And I am your master."

The members erupted in cheers, a sound of raw, primal energy.

The game was no longer a manhunt. It was a mobilization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the army.

He turned to the Demonic Monk, who was watching from the shadows.

"The repayment is just beginning, Monk. Soon, the void gods will know the true meaning of fear."

The game was over for the Society. but for the world, it was just beginning.
