Chapter 278: Holy Fire Priestess (1)

The old woman's eyes were full of a hidden wisdom as she looked at Mok Gyeong-un.

She was the Holy Fire Priestess, the leader of the Fire Faith Order, and she had spent her entire life studying the mysteries of the primordial fire.

"So, you've come for the fifth focus, boy," she said, her voice a low and raspy whisper.

"I have. and I've come to take your place."

Mok Gyeong-un stood in front of the altar, his entire body glowing with a faint, white radiance.

"Your fire is strong, Priestess. but it is still just a shadow. and the void is the only true light."

The Priestess laughed, a cold and sharp sound.

"The void? You think you can handle the power of the primordial fire? It will consume you before you can even touch it!"

"I am the Void Sun. and I can handle anything."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, clashing with the Priestess's primordial fire.

The temple was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the confrontation.

The game was no longer a quest. It was a deification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming a god.
