Chapter 263: Clue (3)

'A taboo by sorcery…'

Mok Gyeong-un felt a wave of recognition as he heard Hae Yeong's words.

The "taboo" was a reference to the origins of the void gods, and the original ritual that had been used to seal them.

"So the key is not a physical object. It is a spiritual one," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice neutral.

"It is, boy. and it can only be obtained by someone who has mastered both the void and the light."

Hae Yeong looked at the boy with his sharp, intelligent eyes.

"You have the power, boy. but do you have the wisdom to use it?"

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"I have the void. and that's all the wisdom I need."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, clashing with the old man's spiritual barrier.

The village was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the confrontation.

The game was no longer a trial of character. It was a battle for the ultimate knowledge. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was winning.
