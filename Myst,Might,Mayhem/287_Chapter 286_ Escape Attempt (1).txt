Chapter 286: Escape Attempt (1)

A symbol of a single line drawn on the floor of the prison cell.

It was an escape attempt, a desperate plan by one of the Society's captives to break free from the entanglements of the void.

The captive was a young woman, a former candidate for the shadow unit who had been deemed "unfit" by Mok Gyeong-un.

But she had a secret. She was a master of the old ways, a practitioner of the traditional martial arts.

"The void... it's a cage. and I am the one who is going to find the key," she whispered to herself as she traced the line with her finger.

Suddenly, the floor of the cell began to vibrate, and the air became thick with the power of her own internal energy.

"The escape is beginning," she said, her eyes full of a fierce determination.

But as she reached for the cell door, she was stopped by a figure in the shadows.

It was Mok Gyeong-un.

He was standing in the doorway, his eyes black and lifeless.

"There is no escape from the void, captive. and there is no key to my cage."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was an internment. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the walls.
