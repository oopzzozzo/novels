Chapter 133: Erosion (1)

"The Society Leader... he is not what he seems."

These were the words of the former master of the Evil Commandment Sword, a man who was now a spectral presence bound to Mok Gyeong-un.

The boy was currently in his secret chamber, contemplating this information.

"And what is he, then?" Mok Gyeong-un asked, his voice neutral.

"He is... a host. A vessel for the ultimate darkness. And he is being eroded by it."

The Demonic Monk's voice was full of hidden terror.

"Eroded? You mean he's losing his mind?"

"More than that. He's losing his very existence. Soon, there will be nothing left of the man he once was. Only the darkness will remain."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then I'll just have to speed up the process. I'll show him what true erosion looks like."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint, crimson glow that illuminated the chamber.

"The gateway is open. And the darkness is coming for everyone."

The game was becoming a race to the bottom. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the descent.
