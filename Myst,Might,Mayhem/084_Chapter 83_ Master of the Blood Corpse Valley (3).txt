Chapter 83: Master of the Blood Corpse Valley (3)

[Grandfather. Is our Lee family truly not a family of traitors?]

[Ji-yeom. Is that question related to the secret records?]

Mok Gyeong-un was currently reliving a memory, one that belonged to the Master of the Corpse Blood Valley.

It was a memory of a conversation between a young boy and his grandfather.

"The Lee family... they were the guardians of the forbidden knowledge," the grandfather had said, his voice full of gravity.

"But they were betrayed by those they sought to protect. And now, they are hunted as traitors."

Mok Gyeong-un watched as the young boy, Ji-yeom, listened with wide eyes.

"But we're not traitors, Grandfather! We're just... different."

"In this world, being different is the same as being a traitor, Ji-yeom. Remember that."

Mok Gyeong-un opened his eyes, the memory fading away.

The Master of the Valley... he was a Lee. A member of the family that had been purged by the Society.

"So that's why he created the Valley. To reclaim his legacy. And to take revenge on those who betrayed him."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"We have more in common than I thought, Ji-yeom."

The game was becoming a shared history. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was connecting the dots.
