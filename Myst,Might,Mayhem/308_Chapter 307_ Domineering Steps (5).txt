Chapter 307: Domineering Steps (5)

Reverse Muscle Scripture.

This was the technique Mok Gyeong-un was using to finish off Kang Jin-ho and the Righteous Alliance.

It was a technique that involved reversing the flow of internal energy, creating a shockwave that could shatter even the most powerful physical defenses.

"The Iron Fist... it's just a shell, Jin-ho," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice echoing through the main square.

"And I am the one who is going to break it."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, clashing with Kang Jin-ho's final strike.

The sound of the impact was like a thunderclap, followed by the blinding light of the final confrontation.

Kang Jin-ho fell back, his fist shattered and his body broken.

"The battle is over, Jin-ho. and the winner is clear."

Mok Gyeong-un stood over the now-silent warrior, his transparent eyes fixed on the distant horizon.

"The world is mine. and the void is supreme."

The game was over for the Righteous Alliance. but for the universe, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un was now the eternal ruler of all things.
