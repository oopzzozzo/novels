Chapter 233: The Disregard for the Embroidered Uniform Guard (2)

Zhao Gao and his unit arrived at the base of the Sun Tower, their expressions full of grim determination.

They were met by a group of Mok Gyeong-un's shadow agents, who were blocking the entrance.

"Halt! This tower is under the master's protection!" one of the agents shouted.

Zhao Gao laughed, a cold and hollow sound.

"The master? You mean the boy who thinks he's a god?"

He drew his sword, the steel glowing with a faint, blue light.

"Move aside, or die."

The agents didn't move. They just raised their own weapons, their eyes glowing with a cold, blue fire.

The confrontation had begun. and the base of the Sun Tower was the battlefield.

It was a clash of two elite units, each seeking to dominate the other.

The game was no longer a battle for authority. It was a massacre. And Zhao Gao was the primary target.
