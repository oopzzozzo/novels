Chapter 136: Erosion (4)

Back to the present.

Mok Gyeong-un was standing in the center of the inner castle's main courtyard, facing the Society Leader.

The leader was no longer a man. He was a mass of dark energy, his form constantly shifting and changing.

"So, you've come to challenge me, boy," the leader said, his voice a chorus of a thousand voices.

"I haven't come to challenge you. I've come to end you," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his voice calm and steady.

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, clashing with the leader's own power.

The courtyard was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the final confrontation.

It was a battle of two masters of the void, each seeking to consume the other.

The other members of the Society watched in awe and terror, their own lives hanging in the balance.

The game was no longer a coup. It was an apocalypse. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was surviving it.
