Chapter 257: The Mark (4)

Thousand-men Commander Ma Ra-hyeon was staring at the boy with a mixture of shock and admiration.

He had witnessed the boy's power firsthand, and he was beginning to understand why the old leader had been so afraid of him.

"You're not human, are you?" he asked, his voice a mere whisper.

"I am the Void Sun, Ma Ra-hyeon. and human is just one of the things I am," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his golden-white eyes fixed on him.

"The Thousand-men Unit... you're all seeking a purpose. A reason for your existence."

Ma Ra-hyeon nodded.

"We are. and we thought the Society was that purpose. but now we see that we were just tools."

"And what if I offer you a new purpose? A purpose that is worthy of your lineage?"

Mok Gyeong-un stepped closer to him.

"Join me. become the vanguard of my new world. and together, we will show the void gods what true power looks like."

Ma Ra-hyeon hesitated for a second, but then he bowed his head.

"I accept. The Thousand-men Unit is yours, Master."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of work for you to do."

The game was becoming a unification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the merger.
