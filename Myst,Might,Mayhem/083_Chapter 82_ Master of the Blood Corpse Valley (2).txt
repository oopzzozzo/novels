Chapter 82: Master of the Blood Corpse Valley (2)

Mok Gyeong-un smiled brightly and said,

"How do I know the secret of the Crimson Flame Sword Art, you ask?"

He was facing a group of warriors from the Corpse Blood Valley, who were staring at him with a mixture of shock and fury.

The Crimson Flame Sword Art was the secret technique of their group, and it was supposed to be known only to the highest-ranking members.

"I know it because I've seen it. And I've seen it because I've been to the place where it was created."

The warriors' eyes widened.

"The place where it was created? You mean the Forbidden Temple?"

"Yes. And I've also met the one who created it. Your 'Master'."

The warriors drew their swords, the steel glowing with a faint, crimson light.

"You lie! No one has seen the Master and lived to tell the tale!"

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"No one? Perhaps you're right. For I am not 'no one'. I am the master of the void."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a wave of heat that was almost unbearable.

The battle had begun. And Mok Gyeong-un was ready to show them the true meaning of the Crimson Flame.
