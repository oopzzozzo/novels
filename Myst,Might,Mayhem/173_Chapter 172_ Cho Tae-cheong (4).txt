Chapter 172: Cho Tae-cheong (4)

From the round copper tag, a faint red light began to seep out.

It was the focus, the point of origin for the void's energy in the physical world.

And Mok Gyeong-un had finally found it.

He stood over the now-silent Cho Tae-cheong, his eyes glowing with an intense, crimson radiance.

"The second focus... it's mine," he whispered, his voice echoing through the street.

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The second focus... you've found it, Great One. Now, the ritual can proceed to the final stage."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Yes. It's exactly what I needed."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a shockwave that rattled the windows of the nearby buildings.

"Soon, the world will know the true meaning of the void."

The game was no longer a liquidation. It was a mobilization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the march.
