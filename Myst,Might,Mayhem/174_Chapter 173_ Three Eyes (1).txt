Chapter 173: Three Eyes (1)

Looking at the empty space where the copper tag had been, Mok Gyeong-un felt a sense of anticipation.

The second focus was now a part of him, and he could feel its power coursing through his meridians.

"The Three Eyes... they're getting closer," the Demonic Monk said, his voice full of concern.

"The Three Eyes? And who are they?"

"They are the elite unit of the Imperial City's diviners. They specialize in tracking and capturing masters of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un turned towards the distant temple.

"Interesting. So, they think they can track me."

"They're a powerful force, Great One. And they have the support of the Emperor."

"The Emperor... he's just another mortal who is afraid of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up and adjusted his cloak.

"Now, tell me. Where are they?"

"They're... in the Temple of the Moon. In the Chamber of the Sun."

"Good. Then let's not keep them waiting."

Mok Gyeong-un walked away, the dark energy swirling around him.

The game was becoming a confrontation between the Society and the Empire. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the assault.
