Chapter 94: Reducing the Numbers (3)

The senior warrior exploded with anger and asked the ordinary red-belted warrior, "Just who the hell are you to interfere in this tournament?!"

The red-belted warrior didn't answer. He just stood there, his eyes fixed on Mok Gyeong-un.

He was a master of the void, a man who had been sent by the leader of the Society to stop the boy.

"I am the one who will end this madness," he finally said, his voice echoing with the power of the void.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Madness? I call it... evolution."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, clashing with the red-belted warrior's own power.

The arena was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the confrontation.

It was a battle of two masters of the void, each seeking to dominate the other.

The other disciples watched in awe and terror, their own petty rivalries forgotten.

The game was no longer a tournament. It was a clash of gods. And the fate of the Society was at stake.
