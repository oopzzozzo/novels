Chapter 31: Guyeo (3)

Unlike the vengeful spirits that lack a physical body and thus cannot be touched or perceived, some entities possess a physical form that exists in the boundary between worlds.

These entities are known as the Guyeo.

They are the guardians of the underworld, the collectors of souls, and the enforcers of the void's will.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently staring at an ancient mural that depicted one such entity.

It was a creature of shadow and bone, with eyes that burned with a cold, blue fire.

"The Guyeo... they are real," he whispered.

The Demonic Monk, now a mere shadow of his former self, stood beside him.

"They are, Great One. And they are not to be trifled with."

"Trifled with? I intend to command them."

Mok Gyeong-un reached out his hand towards the mural.

And the image of the Guyeo seemed to ripple and move.

"You... you're calling them?" the Monk asked, his voice trembling.

"I am. I'm inviting them to this world. To witness the ritual."

"But the ritual is not complete!"

"It will be. Soon."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes glowed with the same cold, blue fire as the mural's entity.

The game was becoming a nightmare for everyone except him. And he was just getting started.
