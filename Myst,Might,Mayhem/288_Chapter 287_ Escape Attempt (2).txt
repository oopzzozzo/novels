Chapter 287: Escape Attempt (2)

The entrance to the underground tunnel was hidden behind a waterfall in the palace gardens.

This was the young woman's planned escape route, a way to bypass the imperial guard and the shadow agents.

She was currently moving through the tunnel, her internal energy illuminating the dark and damp passage.

"The outside world... it's just beyond this tunnel," she whispered to herself, her breath coming in short gasps.

"I can almost feel the fresh air. I can almost see the sun."

Suddenly, she was stopped by a wall of dark, golden energy.

It was a barrier of the void, created by Mok Gyeong-un to prevent any unauthorized exits from the palace.

"There is no 'outside' anymore, captive," Mok Gyeong-un's voice echoed through the tunnel.

"The entire world is a part of the void. and you are just a speck in its vastness."

The woman tried to break through the barrier with her internal energy, but her power was dwarfed by the void's.

"I will not... be bound!" she screamed, launching a final, desperate attack.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"You already are."

The tunnel was filled with the sound of a silent explosion, followed by the complete and absolute silence of the void.

The escape attempt was over. and the captive was no more.

The game was becoming a liquidation of hope. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the end.
