Chapter 134: Erosion (2)

Mok Gyeong-un received a report from his informants about the Society Leader's recent activities.

The leader had been spending more and more time in his private quarters, and his orders had become increasingly erratic and violent.

"The erosion is progressing faster than I thought," Mok Gyeong-un muttered to himself.

He was currently in the meeting hall, facing the Council of Elders.

"The leader is no longer fit to lead, Elders," he said, his voice firm and clear.

The Elders looked at each other, their faces full of suspicion and alarm.

"What are you saying, Young Master? The leader is the founder of the Society!" one of them shouted.

"He was. but now he is something else. Something... inhuman."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"I have seen the darkness that lives within him. And I am the only one who can stop it."

The Elders were silent for a moment. They knew the danger of the Society Leader's power.

But they also knew the potential of Mok Gyeong-un's.

"What do you propose, Young Master?" another Elder asked.

"I propose a change in leadership. I propose that I take the leader's place."

The game was becoming a coup. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the rebellion.
