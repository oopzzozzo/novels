Chapter 147: Repayment (1)

Fssst! Crack! Crush!

The sound of shattering glass and splintering wood filled the inner sanctum.

The final confrontation was over, and the Society Leader's entity had been thoroughly destroyed.

Mok Gyeong-un stood in the center of the ruins, his entire body glowing with a blinding, crimson light.

He was the undisputed master of the Heaven & Earth Society.

But he wasn't finished yet.

"Now, for the repayment," he whispered to himself, his eyes glinting with a dark, intense light.

He was thinking about the ones who had betrayed him, the ones who had tried to use him as a vessel.

"The ones who serve the void gods... their time is up."

He raised his left arm, and the red thread surged forward, creating a web that spanned the entire inner castle.

"One by one, I will find them. And one by one, I will make them pay."

The game was no longer a battle for leadership. It was a crusade for vengeance. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the charge.
