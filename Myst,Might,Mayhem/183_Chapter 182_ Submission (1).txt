Chapter 182: Submission (1)

'My lord… I'm sorry.'

The Emperor's final words were a mere whisper as his soul was consumed by the void.

Mok Gyeong-un stood over the now-empty shell of the ruler, his eyes black and lifeless.

He was the master of the empire. and the void was his kingdom.

"The submission is complete, Great One," the Demonic Monk said, his voice full of absolute, fanatical devotion.

"The mortal world has bowed its head. and the void is supreme."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"But we're not finished yet, Monk. There is still one world left to conquer."

He turned towards the distant horizon, where the gateway to the void was pulsing with an intense, dark light.

"The City of the Void. and its gods."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a shockwave that leveled the Palace's inner sanctum.

"I'm coming for you."

The game was no longer a conquest of the mortal world. It was a war against the gods. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the assault.
