Chapter 46: Attack (2)

-Whoosh!

The amount was incomparable to the energy generated by the death of a single person.

The death of multiple guards at once had created a surge of dark energy that was almost tangible.

Go San, the diviner, could feel it. It was a cold, suffocating pressure that seemed to drain the life out of everything around it.

"This... this is more than just an evil spirit," he muttered, his golden light flickering.

Mok Gyeong-un stood in the center of the storm, his left arm glowing with an intense, crimson light.

He was absorbing the energy, his body pulsing with a hidden, dark power.

"You call this evil? I call it... efficiency," he said, his voice echoing through the courtyard.

Go San threw another set of talismans, but they were consumed by the darkness before they could even reach the boy.

"Your tricks are useless here, diviner. The void is my domain."

He raised his left arm, and the red thread surged forward, wrapping around Go San like a hungry serpent.

"Now, tell me. Who sent you?"

Go San tried to resist, but the binding was too strong.

"I... I will not... speak," he managed to choke out.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"You will. Everyone eventually does."

The courtyard was silent as the two of them faced off.

The game was becoming a torture chamber. And Mok Gyeong-un was the grand inquisitor.
