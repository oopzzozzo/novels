Chapter 192: The Test (3)

"What do you think?"

Mok Gyeong-un was currently meeting with the Shadow Clan Master in the Sun Tower.

The Master was staring at the boy, his expression full of hidden suspicion.

"I think you've changed, Master. and I'm not sure if it's for the better."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Change is necessary, Master. If we want to survive the void."

"But the void... it's a place of darkness. and you've returned with light."

"The light is just a different form of the darkness, Master. and the void is both."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up and walked to the window.

"The test is over. and I have passed. and now, I'm going to lead the Society into a new era."

The Shadow Clan Master was silent for a moment. He knew that the boy was now more powerful than ever.

"And what is this new era, Master?"

"The era of the void. Where the boundaries between worlds are no more."

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a merging. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was merging the worlds.
