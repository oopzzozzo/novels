Chapter 285: Entanglements (5)

As Nine Blood Sect's Blood Saint reached the final stage of the ritual, he realized the true nature of the entanglements.

They were not just connections to other souls; they were chains, binding him to the very fabric of the physical world.

And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the keys.

"You're fast, Saint. but you're still bound by the laws of this world," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice echoing through the ritual chamber.

The Blood Saint gritted his teeth, his face a mask of pain and fury.

"I am the Blood Saint! I am the one who commands the life-force of the universe!"

"You command nothing, Saint. You are just a tool for the void. and your time is up."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, shattering the Saint's spiritual barrier and consuming his essence.

The chamber was silent. The Saint was gone.

Mok Gyeong-un stood in the center of the ruins, his entire body glowing with a blinding, white radiance.

The integration was complete. and the void was finally whole.

The game was over. and Mok Gyeong-un was the only one left.
