Chapter 29: Guyeo (1)

"What in the world is this..."

Diviner Sak couldn't hide her bewilderment.

If that sensation just now was real, then this boy...

"Go on. Tell me what you saw."

Mok Gyeong-un spoke in a calm, almost gentle voice.

But his eyes were as cold as ice.

"I... I saw the void. I saw the darkness that lives within you."

Diviner Sak's voice was barely a whisper.

"The darkness? And what did it look like?"

"It looked like... a monster. A monster that's waiting to be unleashed."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"A monster... I like the sound of that."

He stepped closer to the diviner.

"Now, tell me. How do I unleash it?"

Diviner Sak trembled.

"You don't unleash it, Great One. It's already unleashed. You're just... waiting for the right moment."

"Waiting for the right moment? And when is that?"

"When the ritual is complete. When the blood of the innocent has been spilled."

Mok Gyeong-un's smile widened.

"Then the wait is almost over. For I have a lot of blood to spill."

The game was becoming a nightmare. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was dreaming it.
