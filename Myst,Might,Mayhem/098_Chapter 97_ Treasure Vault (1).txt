Chapter 97: Treasure Vault (1)

Mok Gyeong-un couldn't help but be inwardly astonished by the number of bookshelves surrounding the circular chamber.

This was the Society's Treasure Vault, a place that contained the combined knowledge and wealth of the entire organization.

And he was the first person in decades to be granted access.

"Incredible," he whispered, his eyes taking in the thousands of scrolls, manuscripts, and artifacts that lined the shelves.

The Demonic Monk stood beside him, his form flickering with excitement.

"The Treasure Vault... it's more than I ever imagined, Great One."

Mok Gyeong-un walked to the nearest shelf and picked up a scroll.

It was a manuscript on the ancient language of the gods.

"This... this is exactly what I need," he said, his voice full of intensity.

He began to read, his mind absorbing the information with incredible speed.

The game was becoming a feast. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was eating his fill.
