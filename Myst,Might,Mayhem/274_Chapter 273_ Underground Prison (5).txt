Chapter 273: Underground Prison (5)

-Crackle! Crackle!

The sound of electricity arcing through the air filled the lowest level of the Underground Prison.

Mok Gyeong-un had just finished activating the final piece of the prison's machinery, and the seventh focus was now fully operational.

It was a large, glowing orb of pure void energy, pulsing with an intense, crimson light.

"The seventh focus... it's done," he whispered, his eyes transparent and clear.

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The seventh focus... you've achieved it, Great One. Now, the final gateway is open."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Yes. and now, I am ready to meet the original gods."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a gateway that led directly to the heavens.

"The Apotheosis is complete. and the universe is mine."

He stepped through the gateway, followed by the light of the seventh focus.

The game was over for the Underground Prison. but for the universe, it was just beginning.
