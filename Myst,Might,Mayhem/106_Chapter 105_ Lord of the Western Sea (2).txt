Chapter 105: Lord of the Western Sea (2)

The Classic of Mountains and Seas states:

In heaven and earth, there are countless malevolent spirits and entities that seek to control the destinies of men.

Among them, the Lord of the Western Sea is one of the most powerful and ancient.

He rules over the vast expanses of the ocean, his will carried by the tides and the storms.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently experiencing the presence of this deity, the blue energy from the wooden box swirling around him like a watery vortex.

"So this is the Lord of the Western Sea," he whispered, his own dark energy clashing with the salty radiance.

The Demonic Monk watched in silent awe.

"It's... it's a god of the physical world, Great One. Not a creature of the void."

"And what is the difference, Monk?"

"The physical gods are bound by the laws of nature. They have form, and they have limits. The void... the void is infinite."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then I'll just have to show this 'lord' the infinite nature of my power."

He reached into the blue energy and grasped the source of the deity's presence.

And for a moment, he felt the weight of the entire ocean on his shoulders.

The game was becoming a divine wrestling match. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was pinning the opponent.
