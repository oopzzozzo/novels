Chapter 276: Blood Saint (3)

Before long, Mok Gyeong-un and his shadow unit arrived at the entrance of the Hall of Eternal Fire.

It was a massive building, located in the heart of the Northern Mountains, and it was the base of the Fire Faith Order.

The air around the hall was thick with the scent of burning incense and the heat of a thousand fires.

"The Holy Fire Priestess... she's waiting for us," the Shadow King said, his voice full of concern.

"I know. and I intend to show her the true meaning of fire."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped into the hall, his entire body glowing with a faint, white radiance.

The members of the Fire Faith Order were all gathered in the main hall, their eyes fixed on the boy.

In the center of the hall, on a high altar, sat the Holy Fire Priestess.

She was a woman of great beauty and power, and she was known for her absolute mastery of the primordial fire.

"Evil master! You've come to take the focus! But you'll have to get past me first!" she shouted, her voice echoing through the hall.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Past you? I'm going to integrate you."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, clashing with the Priestess's primordial fire.

The game was no longer a quest. It was a confrontation of elements. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the void.
