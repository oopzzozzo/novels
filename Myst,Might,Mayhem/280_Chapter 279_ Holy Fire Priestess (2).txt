Chapter 279: Holy Fire Priestess (2)

"The doctrine of our order is simple, boy. We serve the fire. and the fire serves us."

Mok Gyeong-un was listening to the Priestess's account of her order's history.

The confrontation was over, and the Priestess had been thoroughly integrated into his own being.

He was now the master of the Hall of Eternal Fire.

"And what about the void, Priestess? Does your order serve the void as well?"

"The void is the origin of the fire, Master. and the fire is the manifestation of the void."

The Priestess's voice was now a haunting melody, echoing in Mok Gyeong-un's mind.

"We are the guardians of the transition. The ones who ensure that the fire never goes out."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of work for you to do. Starting with the final stage of the ritual."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a blinding light that illuminated the entire hall.

"The Void Sun has risen. and the shadow of the past is no more."

The game was no longer a quest. It was a mobilization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the army.
