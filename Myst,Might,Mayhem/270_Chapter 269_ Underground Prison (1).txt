Chapter 269: Underground Prison (1)

The next morning, on a small, isolated island in the middle of the river, Mok Gyeong-un arrived at the entrance of the Underground Prison.

The prison was a high-security facility, used by the Society to hold their most dangerous and valuable captives.

And it was rumored that the seventh focus was hidden somewhere within its depths.

Mok Gyeong-un stood in front of the massive iron gate, his lifeless eyes scanning the fortifications.

"The Underground Prison... it's a place of many sorrows," he whispered to himself.

"And I am the one who is going to end them."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, shattering the gate into a thousand pieces.

The guards at the entrance were momentarily stunned. They had never seen such power before.

"Evil master! You're under arrest!" one of them shouted, drawing his sword.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"I am the master of the void. and I am the one who is providing the judgment."

The battle had begun. and the Underground Prison was the arena.
