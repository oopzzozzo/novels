Chapter 44: Manor Master (3)

From the hill below, the lights of the manor were visible.

Two figures stood in the darkness, gazing at the estate.

"The manor is changing," one of them said, his voice low and raspy.

"Yes. A new master has risen. And he is not one of us."

The other figure, a woman with a veil, nodded.

"What should we do, Master? Should we strike now?"

"No. We must wait for the right moment. The ritual is not yet complete."

The man looked at the manor, his eyes glinting with a cold, blue fire.

"But soon, the void will open. And then, we will take what is ours."

The two figures disappeared into the night, leaving only the cold wind behind.

The game was becoming a war between the living and the dead. And the battleground was Yeon Mok Sword Manor.
