Chapter 190: The Test (1)

Half a moment ago.

Mok Gyeong-un was still in the void, facing the first master.

The battle had been intense, but now, it was reaching its conclusion.

"You're stronger than I thought, boy," the master said, his blue fire flickering.

"But you're still missing one thing."

"And what is that?" Mok Gyeong-un asked, his transparent eyes fixed on the master.

"Compassion. The ability to feel for others. Without it, you are just a monster."

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"Compassion? I am the void. and the void doesn't feel anything."

He raised his left arm, and the final surge of dark energy consumed the first master.

The void was silent. The test was over.

Mok Gyeong-un stood in the center of the darkness, his entire body glowing with a blinding, white light.

He was the new god of the void.

But as he stepped back into the mortal world, he felt a sudden, strange sensation in his chest.

It was a feeling of... emptiness.

The game was no longer a deification. It was a realization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was feeling the void.
