Chapter 217: Craftsman (1)

A single stance could make a difference.

This was the philosophy of the Palace's head craftsman, a man named Han Gyeong-ho.

He was a master of the art of fortification, and he had spent his entire life making the Garden of the Sun impenetrable.

But now, he was facing a boy who could cut through his defenses like they were paper.

"Your walls are strong, craftsman. but they are not the void," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice echoing through the garden.

Han Gyeong-ho was currently standing in front of the final gate, his hand on the hilt of his sword.

"The void? You think you can just walk in here and take the core of my life's work?"

"I don't think. I know. For I am the void's representative in this world."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a wave of shadow that washed over the final gate.

The gate, which had been designed to withstand even the most powerful spiritual attacks, began to crack and splinter.

"Impossible! You cannot destroy the Sun Gate!"

"I just did. And now, I'm going to destroy you."

The game was no longer a massacre. It was a deconstruction. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was taking the pieces apart.
