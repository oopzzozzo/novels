Chapter 306: Domineering Steps (4)

Boom!

A sudden explosion rocked the Imperial City, followed by a shockwave of dark energy that shattered the windows of the Sun Tower.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently facing a new threat, a group of warriors who had managed to breach the city's defenses.

They were members of the Righteous Alliance, and they were led by a man named Kang Jin-ho.

"The Iron Fist... he's back," Mok Gyeong-un said to himself, his transparent eyes glowing with an intense, crimson light.

"And he's brought his friends."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, clashing with the Alliance's counter-attack.

The city was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the confrontation.

It was a battle of two worlds, each seeking to dominate the other.

The game was no longer a manifestation of will. It was a struggle for survival. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the end.
