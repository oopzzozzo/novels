Chapter 38: Dangerous Visit (1)

Four days later,

In a dense forest not far from the estate of Yeon Mok Sword Manor,

A man wearing a bamboo hat was leaning against a tree, his eyes closed.

He was waiting for someone.

"Master."

A figure appeared from the shadows. It was a young woman, her face covered by a veil.

"What is the status of the manor?" the man asked without opening his eyes.

"It's... chaotic, Master. Lady Seok and the second Young Master are gone. And the boy... he's in control."

The man opened his eyes. They were sharp and cold, with a hint of hidden power.

"Gone? You mean dead?"

"Yes, Master. It's rumored the boy killed them himself."

The man let out a small, thoughtful hum.

"Interesting. A mere boy has taken over one of the most powerful manors in the region."

"What should we do, Master? Should we intervene?"

"Not yet. I want to see what this 'Mok Gyeong-un' is capable of. He might be the key to the ritual."

The man stood up and adjusted his bamboo hat.

"Prepare for a visit. I want to meet this boy myself."

The young woman bowed and disappeared back into the shadows.

The game was becoming a grand reunion. And the man in the bamboo hat was ready to make his entrance.
