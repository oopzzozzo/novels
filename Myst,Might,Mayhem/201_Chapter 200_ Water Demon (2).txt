Chapter 200: Water Demon (2)

Swoosh!

The downward pressure of the waterfall was immense, but Mok Gyeong-un stood beneath it, motionless and silent.

He was currently in the heart of the Water Demon's domain, a hidden grotto behind the falls.

The air in the grotto was thick with the scent of damp earth and decay.

"So, you've found me, boy," a voice echoed through the grotto, a deep and watery sound.

A figure emerged from the pool at the base of the falls. It was a humanoid creature, its skin a dark, wet green and its eyes glowing with a cold, blue fire.

This was the Water Demon.

"I am the master of the void. and I am here to take the fourth focus," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice neutral.

The Water Demon laughed, a sound like rushing water.

"Take the focus? You think you can just walk in here and take the core of my power?"

"I don't think. I know. For I am the void."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, clashing with the watery vortex the demon had created.

The grotto was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the confrontation.

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a duel of elements. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the fire.
