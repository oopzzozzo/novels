Chapter 141: Annihilating Poison King (2)

That was their initial best course of action.

Dok Go-jin, the Annihilating Poison King, had intended to use the void Gu to perfect his ultimate toxin.

But now, he was facing the boy who had created the Gu himself.

"The void Gu... it is a concentration of all the toxins and malice of the void," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice flat and cold.

"And now, it is a part of me."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, clashing with the toxic cloud that Dok Go-jin had created.

The laboratory was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the confrontation.

It was a battle of two masters of the dark arts, each seeking to dominate the other.

Dok Go-jin was using his knowledge of chemistry and biology to create a toxin that could kill even a god.

But Mok Gyeong-un was using the fundamental darkness of the void to absorb and neutralize the poison.

"Your toxins are useless here, Poison King. The void is immune to your petty chemicals."

He raised his hand, and the red thread surged forward, wrapping around Dok Go-jin's neck.

"Now, tell me. Where is the formula for your masterpiece?"

Dok Go-jin tried to resist, but the binding was absolute.

"I... I will... never... tell you," he managed to choke out.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"We'll see about that."

The game was becoming an interrogation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the needle.
