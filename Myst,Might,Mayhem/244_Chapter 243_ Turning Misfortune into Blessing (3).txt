Chapter 243: Turning Misfortune into Blessing (3)

The legacy of Mok Gyeong-un lived on for centuries.

The world he had created was one of peace and prosperity, a place where the void was no longer a source of terror, but a source of power and inspiration.

And in the heart of the capital, in the Sun Tower, the golden-white radiance of the Void Sun continued to shine.

"The story is over, Monk," a young boy said, closing an ancient book.

He was currently sitting in the tower's library, his eyes glowing with a faint, white radiance.

"Or is it just the beginning?" the Demonic Monk's voice whispered in his mind.

The boy smiled.

"Perhaps it's a bit of both. but in the end, it doesn't matter. What matters is the result."

He stood up and walked to the window, overlooking the city.

The game was becoming a legacy. And the boy was the one who was carrying it forward.

The End.
