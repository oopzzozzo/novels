Chapter 300: Diabolic Beast (3)

Outside the iron door.

The sound of the battle in the City of the Void was a constant rumble, a background noise that the citizens of the city had become accustomed to.

But now, the rumbling was stopping.

The battle was over. and the winner was clear.

Mok Gyeong-un stood in the center of the city, his entire body glowing with a blinding, white radiance.

The diabolic beast was gone, consumed by its master.

And the City of the Void was finally at peace.

"The deification is complete, Great One," the Demonic Monk said, his voice now a mere whisper in the air.

"You are now the true god of the void. and the master of the universe."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"And now, for the final stage. The creation of a new world."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a wave of creation that washed over the entire cosmos.

The game was over for the old universe. but for the new one, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un was now the eternal ruler of all things.
