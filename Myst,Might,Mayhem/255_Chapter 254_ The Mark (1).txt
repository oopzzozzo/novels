Chapter 254: The Mark (1)

"Hmph. See you again."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was cold as he looked at the now-empty shell of the Demon King.

The confrontation was over, and the entity had been thoroughly integrated into his own being.

He was now the master of the Hall of Eternal Darkness.

But as he looked at his left arm, he saw a new mark.

It was a small, black symbol, shaped like a third eye.

"The Mark of the Void," he whispered to himself, his transparent eyes glowing with a faint radiance.

"The sign of the true master."

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The Mark... it's a sign of your dominance, Great One. and it's also a target."

"A target? For whom?"

"For the other gods. The ones who are still hiding in the void. They won't accept a new master without a fight."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then let them come. I'm ready for all of them."

The game was no longer an apocalypse. It was a deification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming a god.
