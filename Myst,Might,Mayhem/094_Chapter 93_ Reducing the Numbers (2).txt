Chapter 93: Reducing the Numbers (2)

Five boys stood before a door, expressions taut with tension.

Their eyes fixed on the door, unease evident in their postures.

They were in the inner castle of the Society, waiting for their turn to enter the tournament arena.

One of the boys was Mok Yu-cheon.

"Next!" a voice boomed from inside.

One of the boys stepped forward and entered the arena.

The sound of clashing steel and agonizing screams soon followed.

Yu-cheon watched, his face a mask of cold determination.

He knew that only one of them would come out alive.

"Next!"

Another boy entered. Another set of screams.

"Next!"

It was Yu-cheon's turn.

He took a deep breath and stepped into the arena.

He was met with a sight that made his blood run cold.

Mok Gyeong-un was standing in the center of the arena, his left arm glowing with a dark, intense light.

And around him were the corpses of the other boys.

"Welcome, Yu-cheon," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice flat and devoid of emotion.

"I've been waiting for you."

The game was no longer a tournament. It was a massacre. And Mok Yu-cheon was the next victim.
