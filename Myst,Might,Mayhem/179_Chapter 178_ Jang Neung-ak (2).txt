Chapter 178: Jang Neung-ak (2)

"What? Mok Gyeong-un is already at the Palace?"

Jang Neung-ak's voice was full of disbelief.

He had just arrived at the Palace's main courtyard, and he was met with a sight that made his blood run cold.

Mok Gyeong-un was standing in the center of the courtyard, his entire body glowing with a blinding, crimson light.

And around him were the corpses of the Palace's elite guard unit.

"You're late, General," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice flat and devoid of emotion.

"I've already taken the third focus. and now, I'm going for the final one."

Jang Neung-ak drew his sword, the steel glinting in the sun.

"I will not let you pass, Mok Gyeong-un. I will die before I allow you to enter the inner sanctum."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"A noble sentiment. but also a meaningless one."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, clashing with Jang Neung-ak's own power.

The courtyard was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the blinding light of the confrontation.

It was a battle of two masters of the physical world and the void, each seeking to dominate the other.

The game was no longer a military operation. It was a duel of titans. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was taking the crown.
