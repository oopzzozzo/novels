Chapter 129: Reunion (2)

Not long ago, in the Crimson Orchid House located on the eastern outskirts of the outer city…

The Crimson Orchid House was a place of entertainment, but it was also a place where information was traded.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently meeting with a group of people who claimed to have information about his past.

"You say you know where I came from?" Mok Gyeong-un asked, his voice neutral.

The leader of the group, a middle-aged man with a scarred face, nodded.

"We do, Young Master. We were there the night you were taken."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes glinted with interest.

"Taken? By whom?"

"By the ones who serve the void. The ones who wanted to use you as a vessel."

The man leaned forward, his voice a mere whisper.

"They brought you to the village. and then they destroyed it. To cover their tracks."

Mok Gyeong-un was silent for a moment.

"And why are you telling me this now?"

"Because we want revenge, Young Master. And we believe you are the only one who can give it to us."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Revenge... it's a powerful motivator. And I think I can help you with that."

The game was becoming a revelation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was finding the truth.
