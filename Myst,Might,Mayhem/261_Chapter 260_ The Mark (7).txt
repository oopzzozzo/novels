Chapter 260: The Mark (7)

It looked like writing, yet it was not writing.

It was a collection of ancient, glowing runes that were etched into the very fabric of Mok Gyeong-un's skin.

They were the language of the void, and they contained the base principles of the universe.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently standing in the Sun Tower's central ritual chamber, his entire body glowing with a blinding, white radiance.

The final stage of the ritual was underway, and the transformation was almost complete.

"The language of the gods... it's beautiful," he whispered to himself, his transparent eyes scanning the runes on his arm.

"And it's mine to command."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a gateway that led directly to the heavens.

The Demonic Monk, now a mere shadow of a shadow, watched in absolute awe.

"The Apotheosis is complete, Great One. You are now a true god."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Now, for the final stage. The transformation of the world."

He stepped through the gateway, followed by the light of the Primordial True Qi.

The game was over for the Sun Tower. but for the universe, it was just beginning.
