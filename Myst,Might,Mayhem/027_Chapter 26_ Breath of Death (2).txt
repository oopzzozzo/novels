Chapter 26: Breath of Death (2)

This was Mok Eun-pyeong's residence.

-Slurp!

Mok Eun-pyeong, who had finished his breakfast, was elegantly drinking tea.

He was in a good mood.

The boy, Mok Gyeong-un, was now the master of the manor, and Eun-pyeong was his closest advisor.

Or so it seemed to the outside world.

In reality, Eun-pyeong was the one pulling the strings.

"Young Master."

A servant entered the room.

"What is it?"

"Guard Gam is requesting an audience."

Eun-pyeong's eyes narrowed.

"Guard Gam? What does he want?"

"He says he has important news regarding the Breath of Death."

Eun-pyeong set his tea down.

"Show him in."

Guard Gam entered, his face pale and his hands trembling.

"Young Master... I have seen it."

"Seen what?"

"The Breath of Death. It's... it's not a martial art. It's a curse."

Eun-pyeong smiled.

"A curse? Tell me more."

Guard Gam began to recount his training with Mok Gyeong-un.

The dark energy, the coldness, the feeling of his very soul being drained.

Eun-pyeong listened intently.

The game was becoming more dangerous. And he was loving every second of it.
