Chapter 169: Cho Tae-cheong (1)

After traveling for several days, Mok Gyeong-un finally arrived at the residence of Cho Tae-cheong, a man who was rumored to be the leader of the Silent Strides.

The residence was located in a quiet, upscale neighborhood of the Imperial City, and it was guarded by a unit of elite warriors.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently standing in front of the main gate, his eyes fixed on the house.

"So, this is where the Silent Strides are hiding," he whispered to himself.

Suddenly, the gate opened, and a man in form-fitting robes emerged.

He was a master of the Silent Strides, a man who was known for his speed and his lethality.

His name was Cho Tae-cheong.

"You're not from around here, boy," Tae-cheong said, his voice cold and flat.

"I am Mok Gyeong-un. And I am here to meet the leader of the Silent Strides."

Tae-cheong smiled, a cold and predatory smile.

"I am the leader. and you are my next target."

He drew his sword, and the confrontation began.

The game was becoming a duel of masters. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the challenge.
