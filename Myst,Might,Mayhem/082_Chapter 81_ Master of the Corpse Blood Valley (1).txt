Chapter 81: Master of the Corpse Blood Valley (1)

Upon hearing about some of the memories gleaned from Gyu Soha, who had possessed Neung Hwa-yang's body, Mok Gyeong-un felt a wave of recognition.

Gyu Soha was a high-ranking member of the Corpse Blood Valley, and her memories contained information about the Master of the Valley.

"The Master... he is not what he seems," she had whispered before her soul was consumed by the void.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently sitting in his study, contemplating this information.

The Master of the Corpse Blood Valley was a man of great power and mystery.

But he was also a man with a past. A past that was connected to the Mok family.

"The Master... he's a Mok," Mok Gyeong-un muttered.

The Demonic Monk, now a mere shadow, watched with interest.

"A Mok? But the Mok family was supposed to be the only one of its kind."

"That's what they wanted everyone to believe. But the truth is far more complicated."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up.

"Now, tell me. Where is the Master hiding?"

"He's... in the heart of the Valley. In the Chamber of Souls."

"Then I'll just have to go there. And I'll meet my... relative."

The game was becoming a family drama. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was revealing the secrets.
