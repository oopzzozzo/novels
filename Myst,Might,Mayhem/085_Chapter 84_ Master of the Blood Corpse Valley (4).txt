Chapter 84: Master of the Blood Corpse Valley (4)

Cheong-ryeong, who had noticed Mok Gyeong-un's intention, clicked her tongue inwardly.

What kind of game was he playing now?

He was standing in the middle of the Valley, surrounded by the remnants of the Lee family's power.

And he was holding a small, black key.

"The key to the secret records," he whispered, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

The Demonic Monk stood beside him, his form flickering with anticipation.

"Now, Great One. Open the gate. Reveal the secrets of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un inserted the key into a hidden lock in the wall.

And the gateway opened.

It was a portal to another world, a place of shadow and bone.

"The true history of the world is waiting," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice echoing with the power of the void.

"And I am the one who will rewrite it."

He stepped through the gateway, followed by the Demonic Monk.

The game was no longer a shared history. It was a new beginning. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was creating it.
