Chapter 53: Proposal (2)

"You… become my disciple."

'!?'

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes sharpened at diviner Jo's words.

He was already quite surprised by the diviner's presence, but this offer was entirely unexpected.

"Your disciple? And why would I do that?"

Diviner Jo smiled, a wise and knowing smile.

"Because you have a gift, boy. A gift that few in this world possess."

"And what gift is that?"

"The gift of the void. You can see things that others cannot. You can feel the rhythm of the universe."

Jo stepped forward, his eyes fixed on Mok Gyeong-un.

"But you lack guidance. You're like a wild horse, powerful but uncontrolled. I can show you how to harness that power. I can show you how to become a true master of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un was silent for a moment.

"And what do you want in exchange?"

"Your potential. Your legacy. I want to be the one who guided the greatest master of the void in history."

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"A tempting offer, diviner. But I have already found my own path."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"And I don't need anyone's guidance."

The game was becoming a choice. And Mok Gyeong-un had already made his.
