Chapter 56: Corpse Blood Valley (2)

Heaven and Earth Society.

Two people were walking side by side on a path surrounded by a wall leading to the inner hall.

They were Han Gyeong-rok and the Bright Blade King, Son Yun.

They were on their way to see the leader of the Society.

"The situation is worse than I thought," Han Gyeong-rok said, his voice low.

"The boy... he's more than just a master of the void. He's a disruption to the natural order."

Son Yun nodded.

"I know. But he's also our only hope of reaching the ultimate truth. The Society needs him."

"And what if he destroys us before we reach it?"

Son Yun was silent for a moment.

"Then we will die having witnessed the greatest power in history."

They arrived at the inner hall, where a man in dark robes was waiting for them.

He was the leader of the Heaven & Earth Society, known only as the Master.

"Report," the Master commanded.

Han Gyeong-rok and Son Yun began to recount the events at Yeon Mok Sword Manor.

The Master listened intently, his eyes unreadable.

The game was becoming a grand strategy. And the Master was the one who was playing it.
