Chapter 41: Dangerous Visit (4)

Everything appeared black.

Guard Go Chan felt no sensation in his body.

And he realized that everything was over.

The smoke... it wasn't just a distraction. It was a poison.

A poison that paralyzed the body and clouded the mind.

He tried to focus, to call upon the power of the binding, but there was nothing.

The connection was gone.

"Go Chan... you fool."

A voice whispered in his ear. It was Ha Chae-rin.

She was standing over him, her daggers pressed against his throat.

"I told you to stand aside. Now, you will die for nothing."

Go Chan tried to speak, but his tongue was heavy and unresponsive.

"But don't worry. Your Young Master will follow you soon enough."

Ha Chae-rin raised her dagger, ready to strike the final blow.

But then, a sudden, cold wind swept through the forest.

The smoke began to clear, revealing a figure standing in the distance.

It was Mok Gyeong-un.

His eyes were glowing with an intense, crimson light, and his left arm was pulsing with dark energy.

"No one... touches... my servant," he said, his voice echoing with the power of the void.

The game was no longer a hunt. It was an execution. And Mok Gyeong-un was the executioner.
