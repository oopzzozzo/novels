Chapter 122: Demon Sword (2)

Mok Gyeong-un's hand was reaching towards the wooden box covered in talismans.

[That sword makes humans go crazy.]

The Demonic Monk's voice was a warning, echoing in the boy's mind.

"Crazy? Or just... honest?" Mok Gyeong-un replied, his eyes fixed on the box.

He tore off the talismans and opened the lid.

Inside was the Evil Commandment Sword, its blade a dark, swirling mass of shadow.

As he touched the hilt, he felt a sudden surge of malice and bloodlust.

It was a feeling he had experienced before, but never with such intensity.

"This is... perfect," he whispered, his eyes turning a deep, void-black.

The sword hummed with power, its presence filling the room with a cold, suffocating pressure.

"Now, let's see what this 'insanity' can really do."

He raised the sword, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a wave of destruction that leveled everything in its path.

The game was no longer a quest. It was a rampage. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the charge.
