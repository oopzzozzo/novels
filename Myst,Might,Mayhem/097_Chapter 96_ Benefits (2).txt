Chapter 96: Benefits (2)

'Ah…'

At the mention of the Ascending Martial Arts, Mu Jang-yak's expression turned regretful.

That was the one benefit that Mok Gyeong-un had rejected.

"The Ascending Martial Arts... the most powerful techniques in the Society," Mu Jang-yak muttered.

"And he said he didn't need them."

He looked at the report again, his brow furrowed in concentration.

"Why? Why would someone with his potential reject the chance to learn the ultimate arts?"

Mu Jang-yak knew that there must be a reason. And he intended to find out what it was.

He called for one of his informants.

"Find out everything you can about Mok Gyeong-un's recent activities. Especially his training in the secret chamber."

"Yes, Master."

The informant disappeared, leaving Mu Jang-yak to his thoughts.

The game was becoming a puzzle. And Mu Jang-yak was the one who was trying to fit the pieces together.
