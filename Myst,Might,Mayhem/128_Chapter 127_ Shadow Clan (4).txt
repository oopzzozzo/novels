Mok Gyeong-un was currently meeting with the Shadow Clan Master, a man who sat in a dark room, his face hidden by a mask.

"You've caused a lot of trouble for my clan, Young Master Mok," the Master said, his voice a mere rasp.

"I've only done what was necessary, Master. To secure my position. And to reveal the truth."

The Shadow Clan Master let out a small, thoughtful hum.

"The truth... it's a dangerous commodity in this society. Especially the truth about the void."

"I am the master of the void. And I am not afraid of the truth."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped closer to the Master.

"Now, tell me. What is your relationship with the Society Leader? Are you his servant, or his rival?"

The Master was silent for a moment.

"I am his shadow. And shadows have their own agendas."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. Then we have a lot to talk about."

The game was becoming a conspiracy. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was revealing the players.
