Chapter 177: Jang Neung-ak (1)

Yeo Su-rin's expression was a mask of cold fury.

She was currently meeting with Jang Neung-ak, the leader of the Imperial City's guard unit.

"Mok Gyeong-un has successfully infiltrated the temple, General," she said, her voice sharp and clear.

"And he has eliminated the Three Eyes."

Jang Neung-ak looked up from his reports. He was a man of great strength and character, known for his absolute loyalty to the Emperor.

"Eliminated them? You mean they're dead?"

"Yes, General. Every single one of them."

Jang Neung-ak was silent for a moment.

"Then the boy is even more dangerous than I thought. We must find a way to stop him. Before he reaches the Palace."

"And how do we do that, General? He has the power of the void."

"The Palace is not just a building, Su-rin. It is a focus of its own. A source of power that is connected to the origins of the Empire."

He stood up and drew his sword.

"Prepare the guard. We're going to the Palace."

The game was becoming a military operation. And Jang Neung-ak was the one who was leading the defense.
