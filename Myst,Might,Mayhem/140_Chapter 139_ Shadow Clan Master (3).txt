Chapter 139: Shadow Clan Master (3)

After negotiating with the Shadow Clan Master, Mok Gyeong-un felt a sense of completion.

The Society was now unified under his leadership, and he had the resources he needed to complete the ritual.

But he also knew that there was one final challenge he had to face.

The Society Leader. Or rather, the entity that had consumed him.

"The Leader... he's still in the inner castle, isn't he?" Mok Gyeong-un asked the Demonic Monk.

"He is, Great One. But he is no longer the man he once was. He is a monster of pure void energy."

"Then I'll just have to show him who the true master of the void is."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up and walked towards the inner castle.

The air was thick with the power of the void, and the very ground seemed to tremble with anticipation.

"The Apotheosis is coming. And I am the one who will achieve it."

The game was becoming a grand finale. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was playing the final notes.
