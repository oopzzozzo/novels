Chapter 310: Reunion (3)

'Are they all dead?'

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the ruins of the Sun Tower, his transparent eyes scanning the area.

The final battle for the universe was over, and he was the only one left.

The previous master, the original gods, the Shadow King... they were all gone, consumed by the void.

And now, Mok Gyeong-un was the void.

He stood in the center of the ruins, his entire body glowing with a blinding, white radiance.

"The story is complete," he whispered to himself, his voice echoing through the entire cosmos.

"And the world is finally free."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a wave of creation that washed over the entire universe.

The game was over. and Mok Gyeong-un was the winner.

The end of the story.

The end of the universe.

And the beginning of everything.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, a smile that was finally eternal.

THE END.
