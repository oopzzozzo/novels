Chapter 235: The Disregard for the Embroidered Uniform Guard (4)

Mok Gyeong-un stood at the entrance of the Sun Tower, watching as his shadow agents disposed of the remains of the Embroidered Uniform Guard.

The battle had been short, but it had sent a clear message to the rest of the city: Mok Gyeong-un was now the master.

"The city is under our control, Master," the Shadow King said, appearing from the shadows.

"But there are still variables. The other sects and clans... they won't accept our rule without a fight."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then we'll give them a fight they'll never forget."

He looked at the distant city walls, his eyes glowing with a faint, dark light.

"But first, we must complete the ritual. The final focus is within our reach."

He turned towards the Sun Tower's inner chamber.

"The Sun Crystal is the key. and I am the one who will turn it."

The game was no longer a struggle for authority. It was a mobilization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the army.

The final stage of the ritual was just a few days away. and the world would never be the same again.
