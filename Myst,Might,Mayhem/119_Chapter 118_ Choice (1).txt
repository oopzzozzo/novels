Chapter 118: Choice (1)

-Bulge! Bulge!

At that moment, the veins on Mok Gyeong-un's left hand bulged prominently, and soon, the dark energy of the void began to seep out.

He was currently in his secret chamber, facing a difficult choice.

The ritual was complete, the gateway was open, and he had his army of shadows.

But there was still one thing missing: a focus.

A point of origin for the void's energy in the physical world.

"The focus... it must be something of great power. Something that is connected to the origins of the Society," the Demonic Monk said, his voice full of gravity.

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the two items that sat on the table in front of him.

One was the Manor Master's seal, the symbol of authority and wealth.

The other was the Original Tablet, the source of the base principles of the void.

"Which one, Master?" the Monk asked.

Mok Gyeong-un was silent for a moment.

"The seal is power. but the tablet is knowledge. And in the void, knowledge is the ultimate power."

He reached out his hand towards the tablet.

"I choose the tablet."

The game was becoming a definition. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was defining the rules.
