Chapter 90: Goal (1)

On the way back to the inner castle of Heaven and Earth Society, Diviner Jo Ui-gong had a complicated expression.

He was thinking about the boy, Mok Gyeong-un.

The boy had achieved something that no one else in the Society had ever managed: he had contacted the void.

And he had done it at such a young age.

"Young Master Mok... you are truly a marvel," Jo muttered to himself.

But he also knew the danger. The void was a fickle and destructive force.

And if Mok Gyeong-un wasn't careful, he would be consumed by it.

"I must find a way to guide him. To ensure that he uses his power for the good of the Society."

Jo's goal was clear: he wanted to be the one who controlled the master of the void.

He wanted to be the power behind the throne.

But he also knew that Mok Gyeong-un was not a boy who could be easily controlled.

The game was becoming a long-term strategy. And Jo Ui-gong was the one who was planning the moves.
