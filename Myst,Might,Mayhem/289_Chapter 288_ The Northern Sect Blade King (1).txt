Chapter 288: The Northern Sect Blade King (1)

Gu Seong-baek, the Northern Sect Blade King, was a man whose name was synonymous with power and ruthlessness.

He was the leader of the Northern Sect, a powerful alliance of clans that had long been a rival to the Heaven & Earth Society.

But now, he was facing a threat that was greater than any he had ever encountered.

"Mok Gyeong-un... he has finally appeared," he said to his top generals, his voice a deep and resonant bass.

"And he has taken the Imperial City. The Empire is now under his control."

The generals looked at each other, their faces full of suspicion and alarm.

"The master of the void... he is a monster, King! We must find a way to stop him before he reaches our territory!" one of them shouted.

Gu Seong-baek smiled, a cold and predatory smile.

"I am not afraid of a mere boy. I am the Blade King! and my steel is the strongest in the world!"

He stood up and drew his massive blade, the steel glinting in the morning sun.

"Prepare the army. We're going to the Imperial City. and we're going to show this 'Young Master' what true power looks like."

The game was becoming a war between the North and the South. And Gu Seong-baek was the one who was leading the charge.
