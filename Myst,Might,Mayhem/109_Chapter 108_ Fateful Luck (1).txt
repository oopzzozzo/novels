Chapter 108: Fateful Luck (1)

Mok Gyeong-un's collapsed body was crumbling like tree bark.

And from within that shattering shell, a new form was emerging.

It was a body made of shadow and bone, pulsing with the power of the void and the sea.

The Demonic Monk watched in absolute terror.

"The... the transformation... it's happening," he whispered.

Mok Gyeong-un opened his eyes. They were no longer blue or red. They were black, like the deepest depths of the ocean.

"I am... Mok Gyeong-un," he said, his voice echoing with a power that was not of this world.

He stood up, his new form towering over the remains of his old body.

"And I have been reborn."

The red thread, once a web that spanned the manor, was now a cloak that draped around his shoulders.

"The ritual is complete. The gateway is open. And I am the master of all."

He walked out of the cavity, the ground trembling with every step.

The game was no longer an evolution. It was a manifestation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was manifesting.
