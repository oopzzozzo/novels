Chapter 196: Loyalty (3)

Mok Gyeong-un said nothing, but his presence was enough to silence the room.

The members of the Society were all looking at him, their expressions a mixture of awe and apprehension.

They knew that their master was no longer just a boy. He was a force of nature.

"The old leader is dead. and the Society is mine," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice flat and clear.

"But I am not interested in your petty politics or your minor resources. I am interested in the void."

He walked to the high throne and sat down.

"Now, tell me. What is the status of the ritual? I want a full report. and I want it now."

The Shadow Clan Master stepped forward and began to recount the details of the ritual's progress.

Mok Gyeong-un listened intently, his transparent eyes scanning the records.

"The ritual is almost complete, Master. The final stage is just a few days away."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I am ready to achieve the Apotheosis."

The game was becoming a grand finale. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was playing the final notes.
