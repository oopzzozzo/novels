Chapter 221: Mixed-blooded (2)

Song-ah, the representative of the Mixed-blooded Clan, was staring at Mok Gyeong-un with a mixture of shock and admiration.

She had witnessed the boy's power firsthand, and she was beginning to understand why the old leader had been so afraid of him.

"You're not human, are you?" she asked, her voice a mere whisper.

"I am the Void Sun, Song-ah. and human is just one of the things I am," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his golden-white eyes fixed on her.

"The Mixed-blooded Clan... you're all seeking a purpose. A reason for your existence."

Song-ah nodded.

"We are. and we thought the Society was that purpose. but now we see that we were just tools."

"And what if I offer you a new purpose? A purpose that is worthy of your lineage?"

Mok Gyeong-un stepped closer to her.

"Join me. become the vanguard of my new world. and together, we will show the void gods what true power looks like."

Song-ah hesitated for a second, but then she bowed her head.

"I accept. The Mixed-blooded Clan is yours, Master."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of work for you to do."

The game was becoming a unification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the merger.
