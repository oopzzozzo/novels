Chapter 137: Shadow Clan Master (1)

The Five Kings of the Society.

They were the most powerful and influential members of the organization, each ruling over their own domain.

And now, they were all dead.

Mok Gyeong-un had eliminated them one by one, securing his position as the new leader of the Society.

But there was still one person who remained: the Shadow Clan Master.

He was the most secretive and dangerous of the five, and his loyalty to the previous leader was absolute.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently meeting with him in the dark room, the Shadow Clan Master still hidden behind his mask.

"You've won, Young Master Mok. The Society is yours," the Master said, his voice a mere rasp.

"But you're still here, Master. And your clan is still active."

The Shadow Clan Master let out a small, thoughtful hum.

"The Shadow Clan is not easily destroyed. We are the shadows. and shadows are eternal."

"I am the master of the void. And I can erase any shadow."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped closer to the Master.

"Now, tell me. Will you serve me, or will you follow your old master into the void?"

The Shadow Clan Master was silent for a moment.

"I will serve the truth. and the truth is that you are the new master."

The game was becoming a unification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was bringing the pieces together.
