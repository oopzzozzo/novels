Chapter 250: Consort Ho (4)

"Haa. I'll turn you into a demon myself."

Consort Ho's voice was full of hidden malice as she made the declaration that would change her destiny forever.

She had finally submitted to the void, and she was ready to become a part of Mok Gyeong-un's new world.

Mok Gyeong-un stood over her, his golden-white eyes glowing with a faint radiance.

"The transformation is complete, Consort. You are now a demon of the void."

Lady Seo was no more. In her place was a being of shadow and fire, her eyes glowing with a cold, blue radiance.

"I am... yours, Master," she whispered, her voice a haunting melody.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For the world is now ready for the final stage of the ritual."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a blinding light that illuminated the entire city.

"The Void Sun has risen. and the shadow of the Empire is no more."

The game was over for the mortal world. but for the void gods, it was just beginning.

Mok Gyeong-un was now the undisputed ruler of the universe.
