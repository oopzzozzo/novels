Chapter 65: Gu Poison (1)

Dozens or hundreds of poisonous creatures are put into a jar and the lid is not opened until only one remains.

That survivor is the Gu Poison.

It is a concentration of all the toxins and malice of the other creatures, a weapon of ultimate destruction.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently holding a small, ornate jar, his eyes fixed on the lid.

The Demonic Monk, now a spectral advisor, watched with a mixture of awe and apprehension.

"Gu Poison... you're creating one yourself?"

"I am. But not just any Gu Poison. I'm using the essence of the void to refine it."

Mok Gyeong-un opened the lid, revealing a single, pulsating insect.

It was a creature of shadow and light, its body glowing with a faint, ominous radiance.

"This is... the void Gu," he whispered.

"And what will you do with it, Great One?"

"I'll use it to bind those who are beyond the reach of the red thread. I'll use it to infect the very souls of my enemies."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, his eyes glowing with the same radiance as the insect.

"The ritual is almost complete. The gateway to the void is opening. And I will be the one who steps through."

The game was no longer about conquest. It was about transformation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming a god.
