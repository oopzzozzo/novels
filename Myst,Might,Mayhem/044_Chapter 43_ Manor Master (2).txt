Chapter 43: Manor Master (2)

Mok Yu-cheon approached with quick strides, as if he had something to say.

However, upon reaching Mok Gyeong-un, his expression became complicated.

"What is it, Young Master?" Mok Gyeong-un asked, his voice neutral.

"I... I wanted to ask about the training. The Breath of Death."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at him, his lifeless eyes piercing.

"And why are you interested in that?"

"I want to become stronger. Stronger than anyone else in this manor."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Strength... it seems to be a common desire among the Mok family."

He reached out his hand towards Yu-cheon.

"But the Breath of Death is not for everyone. It requires a certain... disposition."

"I am ready. I'll do whatever it takes."

Mok Gyeong-un's smile widened.

"Very well. Then let's see if you have what it takes."

The training courtyard was silent as the two of them began their lesson.

The game was becoming a trial. And Mok Yu-cheon was the one who was being tested.
