Chapter 57: Corpse Blood Valley (3)

There is a saying that if you lose your momentum, it's over.

The burly-looking boy had his momentum completely crushed.

He was a disciple of the Corpse Blood Valley, a warrior who had been trained to be a killing machine.

But he was no match for Mok Gyeong-un.

The boy stood over him, his left arm glowing with a dark, intense light.

"You're fast, but you're predictable," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice flat and cold.

"Wh-who are you?" the disciple managed to gasp, his body broken and bleeding.

"I am the master of the void. And you are just... a nuisance."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his hand, and the red thread surged forward, wrapping around the disciple's neck.

"Now, tell me. Where is the entrance to the Corpse Blood Valley?"

The disciple tried to resist, but the binding was absolute.

"I... I will... never... tell you," he managed to choke out.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"We'll see about that."

The courtyard was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the agonizing screams of the disciple.

The battle was over. But the invasion was just beginning.
