Chapter 79: Silent Strides (2)

Silent Strides (暗踐).

It was a secret organization created by the Righteous Alliance, which could be considered the arch-nemesis of the Society.

Their primary mission was to identify and eliminate the members of the Heaven & Earth Society.

And they were very good at it.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently reading a report on the Silent Strides' recent activities.

"They're getting closer, Great One," the Demonic Monk said, his voice full of concern.

"They've already identified several of our fronts. And they're targeting our high-ranking members."

Mok Gyeong-un turned a page.

"Interesting. So, they think they can stop me."

"They're a powerful force, Master. And they have the support of the Alliance."

"The Alliance... they're just another group of mortals who are afraid of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up.

"Now, tell me. Where is their headquarters?"

"It's... in the Imperial City. But it's heavily guarded."

"Then I'll just have to go there. And I'll show them the true meaning of silence."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

The game was becoming an infiltration. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was going to the heart of the enemy.
