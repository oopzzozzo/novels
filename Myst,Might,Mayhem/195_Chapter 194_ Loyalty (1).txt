Chapter 194: Loyalty (1)

In a room with only a single candle burning, Mok Gyeong-un was meeting with his shadow unit.

The unit was composed of the survivors of the final test, and they were all bound to him by the red thread and the void Gu.

But Mok Gyeong-un wanted more than just a physical connection. He wanted their absolute, fanatical loyalty.

"You have all proven your strength," he said, his voice a low and raspy whisper.

"But strength is not enough. I need your commitment. Your willingness to die for my cause."

One of the members, a young man named Hwan Yun-myeong, stepped forward.

"We are yours, Master. We have seen your power, and we have witnessed your grace. We will follow you to the end of the world."

The other members nodded in agreement, their eyes glowing with a cold, blue fire.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of work for you to do. Starting with the Imperial City."

The game was becoming a military operation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the assault.
