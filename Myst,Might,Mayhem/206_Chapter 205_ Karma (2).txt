Chapter 205: Karma (2)

"…He was expelled for committing a serious crime against the Society."

Mok Gyeong-un was listening to Seop Chun's account of his brother's past.

The investigator was currently bound by the red thread, his face a mask of pain and humiliation.

"And what was this crime, Seop Chun?" Mok Gyeong-un asked, his voice neutral.

"He... he stole the secret records. The ones that detailed the origins of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes glinted with interest.

"Interesting. So my brother was a seeker of truth, just like me."

"He was a traitor! He betrayed the Society and the leader!"

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"The Society was a prison, Seop Chun. and the leader was the warden. My brother didn't betray them. He liberated himself."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"And now, I'm going to finish what he started. I'm going to reclaim the records. and I'm going to destroy the Society."

The investigator's eyes widened in terror.

"You... you can't... the leader..."

"The leader is already dead, Seop Chun. he was consumed by the void. and now, it's your turn."

The game was becoming a completion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was finding the last piece.
