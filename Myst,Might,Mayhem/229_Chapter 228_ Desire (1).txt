Chapter 228: Desire (1)

Mok Gyeong-un wrapped the bandages around his left arm, his movements slow and deliberate.

He was currently in his private quarters in the Sun Tower, contemplating the future.

The takeover was complete, the Empire was his, and he had his army of ghosts and assassins.

But there was still one thing missing: a reason to keep going.

"The void... it's a cold and lonely place, Great One," the Demonic Monk said, his voice now a mere ripple in the air.

"And now that you have everything, what else is there to desire?"

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the monk.

"Desire... it's a mortal concept, Monk. and I am no longer a mortal."

"But you still have a body. and you still have a mind. and minds need a purpose."

Mok Gyeong-un was silent for a moment. He knew that the monk was right.

He had achieved everything he had set out to do. and now, he was faced with the ultimate emptiness.

"I need... a challenge. A foe that is worthy of the master of the void."

The game was no longer a conquest. It was a search for meaning. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was looking for the answer.
