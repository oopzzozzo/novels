Chapter 208: Karma (5)

Indigo Spirit.

According to the ancient records, the Indigo Spirit was one of the most powerful and elusive entities of the void.

It was a being of pure energy, capable of manipulating the thoughts and emotions of those it touched.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently experiencing the presence of this spirit, the indigo energy from the sixth focus swirling around him like a mental vortex.

"So this is the Indigo Spirit," he whispered, his own dark energy clashing with the vibrant radiance.

The Demonic Monk watched in silent awe.

"It's... it's a spirit of the mind, Great One. Not a creature of the physical world."

"And what is the difference, Monk?"

"The mental spirits are bound by the laws of thought. They have no form, and they have no limits. The void... the void is both."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then I'll just have to show this 'spirit' the infinite nature of my mind."

He reached into the indigo energy and grasped the source of the entity's presence.

And for a moment, he felt the combined thoughts and emotions of the entire city on his shoulders.

The game was becoming a mental wrestling match. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was pinning the opponent.
