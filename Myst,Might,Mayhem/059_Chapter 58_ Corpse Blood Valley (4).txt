Chapter 58: Corpse Blood Valley (4)

Out of curiosity, Mok Gyeong-un slightly extended his arm towards the void in the direction where the disciple had pointed.

Suddenly, he felt a strange, cold wind.

It was a draft that seemed to come from nowhere, carrying with it the scent of blood and decay.

"The entrance... it's here," he whispered.

The Demonic Monk, as always, was watching.

"Are you sure, Great One? The Corpse Blood Valley is a dangerous place. Even for someone like you."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Danger... it's just another word for opportunity."

He stepped into the drafts, and the world around him seemed to shift and change.

The trees became twisted and gnarled, and the sky turned a dark, sickly green.

He was no longer in the mortal world. He was in the threshold of the Corpse Blood Valley.

"Now, let's see what these assassins are really made of."

Mok Gyeong-un walked into the darkness, his left arm glowing with a blinding, crimson light.

The game was becoming an invasion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the invader.
