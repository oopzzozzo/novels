Chapter 159: Choice (1)

Mok Gyeong-un, smiling brightly, was currently in the meeting hall of the manor, facing the survivors of the Society's leadership.

The purge was complete, the leader was gone, and he was the new master.

But he knew that his position was still precarious.

The other sects and clans in the region were all watching, waiting for their chance to strike.

"You have a choice, members," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice calm and steady.

"You can either serve me, and participate in the creation of a new world. Or you can leave, and face the consequences of the void."

The members looked at each other, their faces full of suspicion and fear.

"And what are those consequences, Master?" one of them managed to ask.

"The void is not just a place of power, member. It's a place of reckoning. And those who are not with me, are against it."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"The choice is yours. Choose wisely."

The meeting hall was silent for a moment. No one wanted to be the first to speak.

But then, one by one, the members began to bow their heads.

"We serve... Master," they whispered.

The game was no longer a revolution. It was a new order. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was running it.
