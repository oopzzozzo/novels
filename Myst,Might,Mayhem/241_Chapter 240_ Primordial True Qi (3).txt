Chapter 240: Primordial True Qi (3)

Whoosh!

Mok Gyeong-un stood in the center of the ritual chamber, his entire body glowing with a blinding, golden-white radiance.

He had just finished integrating the Primordial True Qi, and the process had been more intense than he had ever imagined.

The original god was gone, consumed by its master.

"I am the source," Mok Gyeong-un whispered, his voice echoing through the entire temple.

"And I am the void."

He raised his left arm, and the golden-white energy surged forward, creating a gateway that led directly to the heavens.

The Demonic Monk, now a mere whisper in the boy's mind, watched in absolute awe.

"The Apotheosis is complete, Great One. You are now a true god."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Now, for the final stage. The transformation of the world."

He stepped through the gateway, followed by the light of the Primordial True Qi.

The game was over for the Forbidden Temple. but for the universe, it was just beginning.
