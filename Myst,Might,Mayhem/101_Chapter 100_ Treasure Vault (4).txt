Chapter 100: Treasure Vault (4)

'This…'

Cheong-ryeong hesitated as she saw a tall, handsome young man approaching Mok Gyeong-un.

Judging by his presence, he was no ordinary warrior.

He was a high-ranking member of the Society, someone who had been sent to oversee the Treasure Vault.

"Young Master Mok. I am Wi So-yeon, the overseer of this vault," the man said, his voice calm and melodic.

Mok Gyeong-un looked at him with his lifeless eyes.

"And what do you want, So-yeon?"

"I am here to ensure that you use the knowledge you have gained for the good of the Society."

So-yeon stepped closer, his eyes fixed on Mok Gyeong-un.

"The Moonless Void Sword... it is a powerful technique. But it is also a heavy burden. I hope you are ready to bear it."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"I am more than ready, So-yeon. In fact, I think I'll start using it right now."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

The game was no longer an awakening. It was a demonstration. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was showing off.
