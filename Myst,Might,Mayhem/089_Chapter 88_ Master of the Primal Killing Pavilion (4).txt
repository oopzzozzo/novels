Chapter 88: Master of the Primal Killing Pavilion (4)

It was something no one had anticipated.

The incantation shackle was originally designed to restrain the power of the void, to prevent it from consuming the user.

But in Mok Gyeong-un's case, it had become a conduit.

A way for the energy of the void to flow directly into his body, enhancing his power and his control.

The Demonic Monk watched in silent awe as the shackles on the boy's left arm began to glow with an intense, crimson light.

"It's... it's perfect," the Monk whispered.

Mok Gyeong-un flexed his arm, and the shackles hummed with power.

"Yes. It's exactly what I needed."

He turned to the remains of the Primal Killing Pavilion, which were now under his absolute control.

"Now, we have everything we need. The ritual can proceed to the final stage."

He looked at the gateway to the void, which was pulsing with a dark, rhythmic light.

"The Apotheosis is coming. And I am ready."

The game was no longer a struggle for dominance. It was a countdown to divinity. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was counting.
