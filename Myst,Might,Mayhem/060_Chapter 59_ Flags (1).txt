Chapter 59: Flags (1)

An odd, uncomfortable feeling that for some unknown reason, it seemed they shouldn't team up together.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently in the meeting hall of the manor, facing a group of representatives from various sects and clans.

They had come to offer their support, but their motives were clear: they wanted a piece of the pie.

"Young Master Mok. We are honored to be in your presence," one of them said, his voice dripping with false sincerity.

"We have heard of your... remarkable achievements. And we wish to offer our services to the new master of Yeon Mok Sword Manor."

Mok Gyeong-un watched them with his lifeless eyes.

"Services? And what services would those be?"

"We can provide warriors, resources, and... political influence. Everything you need to secure your position."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"And what do you want in exchange?"

The representative hesitated.

"We... we only ask for a small portion of the manor's territory. And a share of the trade routes."

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"A small portion? You're already dividing up my manor before I've even finished the ritual."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"The Yeon Mok Sword Manor is mine. And I don't share."

The game was becoming a political minefield. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was planting the bombs.
