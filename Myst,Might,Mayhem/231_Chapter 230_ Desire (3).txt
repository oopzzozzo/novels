Chapter 230: Desire (3)

'Su-submit?'

Imperial Noble Consort Lady Seo was staring at Mok Gyeong-un with a mixture of shock and disbelief.

She had just been told by the boy that her desire was the key to reaching the Forbidden Temple.

"My desire? What are you talking about, Master?" she asked, her voice trembling slightly.

"I mean that the temple is not just a place, Consort. It's a manifestation of our collective desires. And your desire for the throne is the strongest of them all."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, his golden-white eyes fixed on her.

"If you want to reach the temple, you must be willing to sacrifice that desire. You must be willing to submit to the void."

Lady Seo was silent for a moment. She knew that the boy was right.

Her desire for power had been her driving force for years. but now, she saw that it was also her cage.

"I... I will... submit," she finally said, her voice a mere whisper.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. Then the journey can continue."

The game was becoming a trial of the soul. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the test.
