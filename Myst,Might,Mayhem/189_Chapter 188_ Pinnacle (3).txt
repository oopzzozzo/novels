Chapter 188: Pinnacle (3)

Half a quarter-hour passed.

The ritual chamber was silent, save for the hum of the glowing runes.

Mok Gyeong-un was still standing on the pedestal, his eyes closed and his expression calm.

But his presence was no longer that of a boy.

He was now a being of pure light and shadow, his form constantly shifting and changing like the void itself.

"Master... you've done it," Gyu Soha whispered, her eyes full of awe and reverence.

"You've reached the pinnacle."

Mok Gyeong-un opened his eyes. They were no longer black or red. They were transparent, like clear water.

"I haven't just reached it, Soha. I've surpassed it."

He stepped off the pedestal, his feet touching the floor with a weightless grace.

"The void is no longer a place. It's a state of being. and I am that state."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a gateway that led directly to the void.

"Now, for the final stage."

The game was no longer an expansion. It was a transformation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the way.
