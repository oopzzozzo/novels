Chapter 30: Guyeo (2)

The Classic of Mountains and Seas, one of the three great forbidden books that has existed since the ancient times.

It was said to contain the secrets of the gods, the origins of the world, and the path to ultimate power.

But it was also said to be cursed, bringing madness and death to those who dared to read it.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently reading a copy of this book, his eyes moving across the ancient script with intense focus.

The Demonic Monk, as always, was watching from the shadows.

"The Classic of Mountains and Seas... you're reading that forbidden book?"

Mok Gyeong-un didn't look up.

"It's not forbidden to me. It's... informative."

"Informative? It's a collection of myths and legends. Most of it is probably fiction."

"Is it? Or is it just a history that the world has forgotten?"

Mok Gyeong-un turned a page, revealing an illustration of a monstrous entity.

"This... this is Guyeo. The god of the underworld."

The Monk's form flickered.

"Guyeo... the one who rules over the souls of the dead."

"Yes. And the one who is the true master of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un looked at the Monk.

"Now, tell me. How do I contact him?"

The Monk's form trembled with fear.

"You... you want to contact a god?"

"Not just contact him. I want to make a deal."

The game was no longer about this world. It was about the next. And Mok Gyeong-un was ready to cross the boundary.
