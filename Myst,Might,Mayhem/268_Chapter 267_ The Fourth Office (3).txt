Chapter 267: The Fourth Office (3)

Murmur murmur!

The researchers of the Fourth Office were in a state of shock as they witnessed Mok Gyeong-un's power.

He had just finished a sparring session with one of their top experimental subjects, and he had won without even breaking a sweat.

"He's... he's a monster," one of the researchers whispered, his eyes wide with fear and awe.

"No. He's a masterpiece," Su-ryun replied, her eyes glowing with an intense, analytical light.

"He is the perfect fusion of human and void. and he is exactly what we need for our research."

Mok Gyeong-un stood in the center of the ritual chamber, his lifeless eyes fixed on the researchers.

"Your research is over, Su-ryun. from now on, you work for me."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, creating a faint radiance that illuminated the entire chamber.

"The Fourth Office is now a part of my shadow unit. and your techniques are mine to command."

The researchers bowed their heads in absolute submission.

The game was no longer an experiment. It was a merger. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the consolidation.
