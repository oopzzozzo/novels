Chapter 142: Annihilating Poison King (3)

Inside the laboratory, the tension was palpable.

Dok Go-jin, the Poison King, was facing Mok Gyeong-un, the master of the void.

The laboratory was filled with toxic fumes, and the very air seemed to burn with a sickly, green light.

"Your masterpiece... it is a failure," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice echoing through the chamber.

"A failure? You lie! It is the most powerful toxin in the world!" Dok Go-jin shouted, his face a mask of fury.

"It is powerful. but it is not the void. And the void is the only true power."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, consuming the toxic fumes like a hungry beast.

"Now, give me the formula. And I'll show you what a true masterpiece looks like."

Dok Go-jin hesitated. He could feel the power of the boy's will, and the absolute dominance of his presence.

"I... I will... give it to you," he finally said, his voice a mere rasp.

He reached into a hidden drawer and pulled out a small, leather-bound book.

"This... this is the formula. The secret of the Annihilating Poison."

Mok Gyeong-un took the book, his eyes glinting with dark energy.

"Good. Now, let's see what we can do with this."

The game was no longer an interrogation. It was a partnership. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the research.
