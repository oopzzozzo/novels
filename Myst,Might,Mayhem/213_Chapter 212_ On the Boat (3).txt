Chapter 212: On the Boat (3)

"It wasn't merely about the money, Master."

The boatman was still talking, even after Mok Gyeong-un had left the vessel.

He was thinking about the boy's eyes, and the way they seemed to look right through him.

"He... he was like a god. A god of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un was currently walking through the city streets, his mind focused on the sixth focus.

He could feel its presence, a mental vibration that was becoming increasingly intense as he approached the Temple of the Moon.

"The Indigo Spirit... it's waiting," he whispered to himself.

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"The temple is heavily guarded, Master. The Three Eyes are already there."

"I know. and I intend to show them the true meaning of vision."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled, his eyes glowing with dark energy.

"The second focus is mine. and soon, the city will follow."

The game was no longer an occupation. It was a siege. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was leading the assault.
