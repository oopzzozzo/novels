Chapter 138: Shadow Clan Master (2)

Just an ordinary man.

This was the impression Mok Gyeong-un had of the Shadow Clan Master after he removed his mask.

He had no distinguishing features, no scars, no tattoos.

He was just a man in his fifties, with a calm and unassuming demeanor.

"You're surprised, aren't you?" the Master asked, his voice now clear and normal.

"I expected someone... more imposing," Mok Gyeong-un admitted.

"The best shadows are the ones you don't notice, Young Master. The ones who can blend into any crowd, in any place."

The Master sat down at his desk.

"Now, let's discuss our arrangement. What do you want from the Shadow Clan?"

"I want you to be my eyes and ears. I want you to find the ones who are still serving the void gods. and I want you to eliminate them."

The Master nodded.

"I understand. And what do we get in exchange?"

"You get to live. and you get to witness the birth of a new world."

The Master smiled.

"A fair trade. Then the Shadow Clan is at your service."

The game was becoming a network. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was connecting the nodes.
