Chapter 160: Choice (2)

"Originally, Eldest disciple Wi Maeng was the one who was supposed to take the leader's place."

Mu Jang-yak's voice was low and serious.

He was currently meeting with Mok Gyeong-un in the Sun Tower, discussing the future of the Society.

"But he chose to stand with you instead. Why?"

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Because he saw the truth, Jang-yak. He saw that the old leader was a dead man walking. and he saw that I am the future."

"And you think the others will see it that way too?"

"They will. eventually. because they have no other choice."

Mok Gyeong-un stood up and walked to the window.

"The Society is just a tool, Jang-yak. A tool for the ritual. And once the ritual is complete, the Society will be... irrelevant."

Mu Jang-yak's eyes widened.

"Irrelevant? But Young Master, the Society is our life! Our legacy!"

"Your legacy is a shadow, Jang-yak. and shadows are meant to be erased."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward.

"Now, tell me. Where is the second focus? I know there's more than one."

Mu Jang-yak was silent for a moment. He knew that revealing the location of the second focus would be the end of the Society as he knew it.

"It's... in the Imperial City. In the Temple of the Moon."

The game was becoming a global conquest. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was expanding the map.
