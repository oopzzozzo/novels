Chapter 180: Jang Neung-ak (4)

It all happened so fast.

One moment, Mok Gyeong-un was facing the general, and the next, he was the master of the Palace.

The imperial guards who were still alive were staring at him with a mixture of awe and terror.

"The General... he's gone," one of them whispered.

"No. He's just been replaced," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his voice neutral.

He was currently standing in the center of the Palace's main hall, his eyes black and lifeless.

"I am the new master of this empire. and the void is my mandate."

He raised his left arm, and the dark energy surged forward, filling the hall with a cold, suffocating pressure.

"Now, tell me. Where is the final focus? I want it. and I want it now."

The guards were silent for a moment. No one wanted to be the first to speak.

But then, one of them pointed towards the Emperor's private quarters.

"In the Chamber of the Moon. But only the Emperor has the key."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then I'll just have to ask him for it."

The game was no longer a search. It was an audience. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was providing the entertainment.
