Chapter 203: Water Demon (5)

Three years ago, the previous master of the Water Demon's domain had reached the final stage of the ritual and then disappeared.

No one knew what had happened to him, or where the fourth focus had gone.

But now, Mok Gyeong-un knew.

He was standing in the middle of the grotto, his left arm glowing with a faint, blue fire.

"I see... you've been waiting for me," he whispered, his voice echoing through the chamber.

A figure appeared from the pool. It was a man in ancient, water-stained robes, his eyes glowing with a cold, blue radiance.

He was the previous master.

"You've come a long way, boy," the master said, his voice a low and watery sound.

"I've come to take what is mine," Mok Gyeong-un replied, his eyes transparent and clear.

The master laughed.

"Take what is yours? You think you can handle the power of the tides?"

"I am the void. and the tides are just a part of me."

Mok Gyeong-un raised his left arm, and the final battle for the Water Demon's domain began.

The game was no longer a marathon. It was a deification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming a god.
