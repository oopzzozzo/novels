Chapter 291: Convergence (1)

Heum-won (also known as Qinyuan) was a man of great power and influence, known for his mastery of the art of convergence.

He was the leader of the convergence unit of the Society, a group that specialized in the integration of different types of energy.

And he had been tasked with finding a way to integrate the power of the void gods with the power of the mortal world.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently meeting with him in the ritual chamber, watching as he prepared for the final experiment.

"The convergence is critical to the completion of the ritual, Master," Heum-won said, his voice full of hidden excitement.

"And I believe I've found the key to making it work."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"And what is that key?"

"A catalyst. A source of power that is equal to the void and the light."

Heum-won pointed towards the Sun Crystal, which sat on the altar.

"The Sun Crystal... it's the perfect catalyst. and once we integrate it with the void energy, we'll be able to create a new world."

Mok Gyeong-un's eyes glinted with interest.

"Good. Then let the experiment begin."

The game was becoming a scientific endeavor. And Mok Gyeong-un was the primary sponsor.
