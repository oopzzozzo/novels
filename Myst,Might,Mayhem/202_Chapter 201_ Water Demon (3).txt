Chapter 201: Water Demon (3)

"You don't seem to understand the situation, Demon."

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was calm, even as the Water Demon's vortex threatened to consume him.

He was currently standing in the center of the pool, his left arm glowing with a blinding, crimson light.

"I am not here to fight you. I am here to integrate you."

The Water Demon's eyes widened.

"Integrate? You mean consume?!"

"Call it what you will. But the result is the same. Your power will become mine. and the void will be complete."

Mok Gyeong-un surged forward, his light cutting through the vortex like a hot knife through butter.

"You are just a remnant of a lost world, Demon. A shadow that has stayed too long in the light."

He reached out his hand and touched the demon's forehead.

And for a moment, the grotto was filled with a blinding, white radiance.

The Water Demon's voice became a mere ripple.

"You... you are... the Great One."

The game was becoming a completion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was finding the last piece.
