Chapter 299: Diabolic Beast (2)

Long ago, a much smaller form of the diabolic beast had been captured by the first master and used as a tool for the ritual.

But now, the beast was fully grown, and it was more powerful than the first master could have ever imagined.

It was currently locked in a struggle with Mok Gyeong-un, the two of them clashing in the center of the City of the Void.

The city was a place of shadow and bone, where the laws of physics didn't apply.

And the battle was a spectacle of energy and light.

"You're fast, beast. but you're still bound by your own nature," Mok Gyeong-un said, his voice echoing through the city.

"And my nature is to consume!" the beast replied, its claws tearing through Mok Gyeong-un's light.

But Mok Gyeong-un didn't flinch. He just raised his left arm and caught the claws between his fingers.

The dark, golden energy from his arm instantly shattered the beast's claws, and it fell back in shock.

"Your nature is a shadow, beast. and shadows are meant to be erased."

He raised his hand and struck the beast's head.

The game was becoming a liquidation. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was doing the counting.
