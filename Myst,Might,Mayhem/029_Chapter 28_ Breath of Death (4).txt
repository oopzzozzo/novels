Chapter 28: Breath of Death (4)

Inside Hyehwa Hall, the residence of Lady Seok.

Lady Seok sat with her chin resting elegantly on her hand, her expression unreadable.

She was thinking about the boy, Mok Gyeong-un.

He had survived her attempts to kill him, and now he was the master of the manor.

It was an intolerable situation.

"Madam."

Her personal maid entered the room.

"What is it?"

"The second Young Master, Mok Eun-pyeong, is here to see you."

Lady Seok's eyes flickered.

"Show him in."

Eun-pyeong entered, his expression calm and composed.

"Mother."

"Eun-pyeong. What brings you here?"

"I have information regarding the Breath of Death."

Lady Seok leaned forward.

"Go on."

"It's not a martial art, Mother. It's a curse. A curse that binds the user to the void."

Lady Seok's eyes widened.

"A curse? And the boy is using it?"

"Yes. And he's becoming more powerful every day. If we don't stop him now, it will be too late."

Lady Seok smiled a cold, calculating smile.

"Then we must find a way to break the curse. Or rather, we must find a way to turn it against him."

The game was becoming a war. And Lady Seok was ready to fight.
