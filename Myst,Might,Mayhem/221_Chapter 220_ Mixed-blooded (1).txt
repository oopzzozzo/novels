Chapter 220: Mixed-blooded (1)

"Would it be better to just kill them all?"

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was calm, even as he suggested the annihilation of an entire clan.

He was currently in the meeting hall, facing the leadership of the Mixed-blooded Clan, a group of warriors who were descendants of both humans and the void gods.

The clan members were staring at him with a mixture of disbelief and fury.

"You speak of genocide as if it's a mere administrative task, Master," the clan leader said, his voice full of hidden power.

"In the world of the void, genocide is just... clearing the board. and your clan is currently in the way."

Mok Gyeong-un stepped closer to the leader.

"The Heaven & Earth Society is no longer your master. I am. and I don't tolerate competition."

The clan leader drew his sword, the steel glowing with a faint, blue fire.

"We are the descendants of the gods! We will never serve a mere human!"

Mok Gyeong-un laughed.

"A mere human? I am the Void Sun. and your 'gods' are just shadows in my presence."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward.

The battle had begun. And the Mixed-blooded Clan was the target.
