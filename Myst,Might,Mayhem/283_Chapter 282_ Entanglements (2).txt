Chapter 282: Entanglements (2)

Ssshhh ssshhh ssshhh ssshhh.

The sound of the city's collective thoughts was like a background hiss, a constant stream of information that Mok Gyeong-un was processing.

He was currently sitting on the Imperial Throne, his eyes closed and his expression serene.

But his mind was working at incredible speed, weaving the entanglements into a single, cohesive web.

"The city is a symphony, Master," Gyu Soha whispered, appearing beside him.

"And you are the conductor."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"I am the symphony, Soha. and the city is just one of my movements."

He raised his left arm, and the dark, golden energy surged forward, creating a faint radiance that illuminated the meeting hall.

"Now, tell me. What is the status of the outer provinces? I want to know if the entanglements have reached them yet."

Gyu Soha began to recount the reports from their informants.

The entanglements were spreading, connecting the souls of the entire empire to the Void Sun.

The game was no longer an endurance test. It was a global synchronization. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was setting the tempo.
