Chapter 219: Craftsman (3)

Everyone was baffled by what they were witnessing in the Sun Tower.

The dark energy of the void and the golden radiance of the Sun Crystal were clashing in the center of the ritual chamber, creating a spectacle of light and shadow that was unlike anything anyone had ever seen.

Mok Gyeong-un stood in the middle of the storm, his entire body glowing with a blinding, white radiance.

He was absorbing both energies, his form becoming increasingly refined and powerful.

"He's doing it... he's achieving the final fusion," Wi Maeng whispered, his sightless eyes fixed on the boy.

"The master of the void and the master of the light... combined into one."

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows, his form now a mere ripple in the air.

"The Apotheosis is complete, Great One. You are now a god."

Mok Gyeong-un opened his eyes. They were no longer transparent or clear. They were a brilliant, golden white, like the sun itself.

"I am... the Void Sun," he said, his voice echoing through the entire castle.

"And I am the master of everything."

The game was over. and Mok Gyeong-un was the winner.

The world of men and the world of the void were now one. and he was their ruler.
