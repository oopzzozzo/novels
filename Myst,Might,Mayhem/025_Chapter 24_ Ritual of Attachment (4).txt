Chapter 24: Ritual of Attachment (4)

"You went through all the trouble to switch sides to the second young master… but this fake one didn't even notice you?"

Mok Gyeong-un's voice was cold and mocking.

He was standing in the doorway of the secret chamber, his left arm glowing with a dark, intense light.

Go Chan and Mok Eun-pyeong were inside, their faces pale with fear.

"Mok Gyeong-un! How did you get here?!" Mok Eun-pyeong shouted.

"The binding... it led me here. It led me to the pure blood I need."

He stepped into the chamber, the dark energy swirling around him.

"Now, give me the seal. And give me your blood."

Mok Eun-pyeong tried to reach for his sword, but his body wouldn't move.

The binding was too strong.

"Wh-what are you going to do?"

"I'm going to complete the ritual. I'm going to become the master of this world."

He raised his left arm, the red thread pulsing with a fierce, crimson light.

"And you... you're going to help me."

The secret chamber was filled with the sound of a dark, ancient chant, followed by the agonizing screams of the second Young Master.

The ritual was complete. And Mok Gyeong-un was the new master of Yeon Mok Sword Manor.

The game was over. And Mok Gyeong-un had won.
