Chapter 98: Treasure Vault (2)

"It doesn't seem to be a copy?"

-Yes. At least not this one.

At Mok Gyeong-un's query, Cheong-ryeong spoke in a neutral tone.

They were currently examining a large, black stone tablet that sat in the center of the Treasure Vault.

It was covered in ancient, glowing runes that seemed to pulse with a hidden power.

"This is the Original Tablet," Cheong-ryeong continued. "The one that contains the base principles of the void."

Mok Gyeong-un reached out his hand towards the tablet.

"And how do I read it?"

"You don't read it, Great One. You... experience it. You let the knowledge flow into you."

Mok Gyeong-un closed his eyes and touched the tablet.

And suddenly, he was no longer in the Treasure Vault.

He was in the void.

The game was no longer a feast. It was an immersion. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was diving in.
