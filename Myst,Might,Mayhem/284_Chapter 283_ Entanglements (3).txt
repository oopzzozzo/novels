Chapter 283: Entanglements (3)

[Joo Woonhyang… Do you know who I am?]

Mok Gyeong-un was currently communicating with one of the most powerful diviners in the empire, a woman named Joo Woonhyang.

She was a master of the mental plane, and she had been trying to resist the entanglements of the void.

"I know who you are, boy. You are the one who is destroying the world," she replied, her voice echoing in the mental plane.

"I'm not destroying it, Woonhyang. I'm... upgrading it. and I want you to be a part of that upgrade."

Mok Gyeong-un's presence in the mental plane was a blinding, white radiance that dwarfed Woonhyang's own power.

"I have no interest in your 'upgrade', Gyeong-un. I only want to protect the minds of my people."

"And how can you protect them if you are not a part of the void? The minds of your people are already connected to mine."

He surged forward, his light cutting through Woonhyang's mental barriers like they were paper.

"Now, join me. become the master of the mental plane in my new world."

Woonhyang's eyes filled with a mixture of fury and despair.

"I... I will... never..."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Then the consequences it is."

The mental battle was coming to an end. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was winning.
