Chapter 234: The Disregard for the Embroidered Uniform Guard (3)

The battle at the base of the Sun Tower was brutal and fast.

Mok Gyeong-un's shadow agents were more than a match for the Embroidered Uniform Guard, their movements fast and fluid, their power overwhelming.

One by one, the guard members fell, their bodies broken and their souls consumed by the void.

Zhao Gao watched in absolute terror. He had never seen such power before.

"Wh-what are they? They're not humans!" he managed to gasp as he was knocked to the ground.

One of the shadow agents stepped over him, his eyes glowing with a cold, blue fire.

"We are the void, Zhao Gao. and you are just a memory."

Suddenly, a figure appeared at the entrance of the tower. It was Mok Gyeong-un.

He looked down at Zhao Gao with his transparent eyes.

"So, you're the leader of the secret police. The one who thinks he rules the city."

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Your 'authority' is a shadow, Zhao Gao. and shadows are meant to be erased."

He raised his left arm, and the final surge of dark energy consumed the leader of the Embroidered Uniform Guard.

The battle was over. The city was under Mok Gyeong-un's absolute control.

The game was no longer a struggle for authority. It was a deconstruction. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was taking the pieces apart.
