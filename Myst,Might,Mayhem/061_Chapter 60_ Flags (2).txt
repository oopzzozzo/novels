Chapter 60: Flags (2)

'!!!!!!'

The sight of Sohwa dying with her neck twisted left the teammates momentarily speechless.

It had happened in an instant.

One moment, she was standing there, and the next, she was a corpse on the floor.

Mok Gyeong-un stood over her, his left arm glowing with a dark, intense light.

"One down," he said, his voice flat and cold.

The other representatives drew their weapons, their faces pale with terror.

"You... you killed her!" one of them shouted.

"She was a traitor. She was working for the Heaven & Earth Society," Mok Gyeong-un replied.

He looked at the others.

"Now, who's next?"

The representatives looked at each other, then at the boy.

They knew they were no match for him.

"We... we surrender," one of them said, dropping his sword.

Mok Gyeong-un smiled.

"Good. For I have a lot of work for you to do."

The game was becoming a purge. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was holding the broom.
