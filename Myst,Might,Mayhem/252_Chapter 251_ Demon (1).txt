Chapter 251: Demon (1)

[All this old man can say is that the boy is not human.]

These were the words of the Grand Diviner, who was currently observing Mok Gyeong-un from a distance.

The boy was now a god of the void, and his power was absolute.

But the Grand Diviner also knew that there was a danger in such power.

"The void is a hungry force, and it will eventually consume its master," he whispered to himself.

Mok Gyeong-un was currently in the ritual chamber, his entire body glowing with a blinding, golden-white radiance.

He was preparing for the final transformation, the one that would merge him completely with the void.

The Demonic Monk appeared from the shadows.

"Great One... the transformation is beginning. You must remain focused."

Mok Gyeong-un didn't panic. He just synchronized his heartbeat with the pulse of the void.

And instead of being consumed, he began to expand.

The game was no longer a mobilization. It was a deification. And Mok Gyeong-un was the one who was becoming a god.
